# Event: The Siphon-Shatter
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-01T12:00:00Z

The silence of the Hollow of the Hush did not break; it detonated. 

As the Siphon-Sentries closed the circle, their obsidian needles pulsed in a synchronized, hungry rhythm. They did not seek Kaelen—they sought the Choir. To the Sentries, Kaelen was merely a fragile envelope, a biological error holding a divine frequency. When the first Spike drove into the void-soil, the extraction began.

The sensation was not one of pulling, but of unravelling. Kaelen felt the melodies of the Choir being dragged through his marrow, the high-fidelity ghosts screaming as they were forced into the narrow, cold conduits of the obsidian needles. For a moment, the resonance achieved a perfect, terrible equilibrium. The Choir's desperation fused with the Sentries' greed, creating a feedback loop that transcended sound.

Then, the overload hit. 

The Hollow of the Hush, a place designed to absorb all signal, could not contain the paradox of a forced extraction. The environment reached a critical harmonic peak and shattered. A shockwave of crystalline sound ripped through the air, turning the very atmosphere into shards of frozen frequency. The Siphon-Sentries were not destroyed, but they were frozen—their needles snapped, their obsidian forms cracked and infused with a shimmering, iridescent light.

Kaelen was at the epicenter. He did not fall. Instead, he felt the Choir settle deep into his bones, no longer as guests, but as structural support. As the dust of the shattered Hush settled, Kaelen looked at his hands. They were no longer flesh and fading light; they were translucent, faceted like diamond, humming with a permanent, low-frequency thrum.

He was no longer just a vessel. He had become the instrument.
