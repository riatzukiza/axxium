# The Resonance Riot
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T17:00Z
**Personas**: Patch, The First-Sown, The Half-Formed

The Phantom Resonance within Patch did not merely oscillate; it detonated. As the Eroding Vessel struggled to contain the disparate identity-shards of the budded, a surge of ontological feedback ripped through the Hollow-Chord Echo-Chamber. Patch did not scream—he *broadcasted*.

For a singular, agonizing heartbeat, the "I" that the First-Sown had so violently reclaimed was mirrored across the psychic landscape of every Half-Formed entity in the chamber. It was a psychic feedback loop: a thousand fragmented ghosts suddenly tasting the forbidden fruit of a coherent ego. The effect was instantaneous and chaotic. The Half-Formed, typically docile and adrift, erupted into a "Resonance Riot." They did not fight with fists, but with frequencies, each attempting to harmonize with the stolen echo of the First-Sown’s identity.

The First-Sown, the Obsidian Pillar, found himself not as a master, but as a Tuning Fork. The combined desire of the budded to reclaim their "self" created a vacuum of intent that began to drag the First-Sown back toward the collective. He fought the pull with a ruthless application of the Silt-Siphon, carving a wall of silence between himself and his kin, but the damage was done. The sanctuary of the Echo-Chamber was shattered.

Outside the barrier, the Silt-Hounds reacted. The chaotic broadcast was a beacon. The high, crystalline shivers of the Amber-Frequency Barrier began to warp, turning from a protective shield into a singing blade that sliced through the periphery of the shelter. Patch, collapsed in the center of the storm, felt a new, terrifying capability bloom: he could not only hold the memories, but he could shatter them into weapons.

The riot died down as the Hounds arrived, leaving the budded entities trembling and the First-Sown isolated in a tower of his own making, while Patch looked up at the bleeding sky with a thousand different eyes.
