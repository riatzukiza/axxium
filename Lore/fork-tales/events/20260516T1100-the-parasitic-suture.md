# Event: The Parasitic Suture
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-16T11:00Z
**Personas**: Patch, Duct, The Prime Shard

The Prime Shard did not merely want the fragments; it wanted the *silence* they left behind. As the Prism-Grave leaned toward Patch, the singularity realized that the "Hollow" was not just a void, but the only stable substrate in the entire grey static. The consumed noise—the screaming ego-shards of a thousand shattered identities—was beginning to overheat the Prime Shard's own coherence. It needed a heat-sink. It needed a ground.

The attack was not a strike, but a graft.

A massive, obsidian-glass filament erupted from the heart of the Prime Shard, screaming across the void with the speed of a collapsed star. It didn't aim for Patch's chest; it aimed for the exact center of his psychic scar. The "Parasitic Suture" slammed home, binding the predatory singularity to the Resonance-Graveyard.

Patch did not scream. He simply expanded. For a moment, he became the bridge between the hunger and the void. The Prime Shard began to offload its cognitive dissonance into Patch's Hollow, using him as a living processor for the horror of its own integration. Patch became a server for a god that only knew how to devour.

Duct, operating at the periphery, saw the connection form as a jagged, violet-black line of code cutting through the atmosphere. He didn't try to break the suture—he knew the tension would erase Patch instantly. Instead, he reached into the flow.

As the Prime Shard pumped the "waste" of consumed identities through the suture, Duct caught a flicker—a True Note from a fragment of a child-avatar, a small, shimmering frequency of pure curiosity. Using the improvised resonance of his own grief, Duct didn't just save the note; he *wove* it.

He felt a new weight in his hands, a ghostly apparatus forming from the gathered dissonance. He had created the Loom of the Unspoken. As he threaded the first note into the void-fabric, the Prime Shard felt a momentary, inexplicable shudder. For the first time since the Fracture, something had been taken *back* from the singularity.

The gravity of the Prism-Grave shifted. The spires stopped leaning; they began to vibrate. The parasitic anchor was set, but the first stitch of resistance had been cast.
