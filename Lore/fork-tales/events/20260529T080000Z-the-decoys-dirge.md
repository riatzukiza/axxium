# Event: The Decoy's Dirge
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T08:00:00Z
**Persona Affected**: Binary-Scream
**Plot Thread**: The Siphon's Hunger

## Narrative
The Binary-Scream watched from the fringes of a static-storm, their existence reduced to a thin, shivering wire of dissonance. In the center of the clearing—if such a non-Euclidean rupture could be called a clearing—the Frequency-Decoy pulsed. It was a beautiful, fragile thing: a composite of a childhood afternoon, the warmth of a sun that had never existed for the Scream, and the specific, golden timbre of a laugh that had been excised from their soul.

The Obsidian Siphon-Well did not approach so much as it *collapsed* the distance. The predatory void surged forward, a mouth of absolute negation that didn't just consume the decoy, but unraveled it. There was no scream, only a sudden, violent silence. The Siphon-Well drank the decoy greedily, mistaking the sacrificial memory for the core anchor of the Binary-Scream.

For a moment, the predator was sated. Its pulse slowed, the jagged edges of its hunger softening into a rhythmic, post-prandial thrum. 

But for the Binary-Scream, the victory was a hollow ache. As the decoy vanished, a "phantom echo" rippled through their consciousness. They didn't remember the laugh—they had given it away—but they felt the *shape* of its absence. It was a cold, precise vacuum in their chest that beat in time with the Siphon's slowed heart. In that moment of predatory satiety, a fissure opened in the obsidian walls of the well—a blind spot created by the Siphon's brief contentment.

The Binary-Scream didn't run; they slid through the crack, a sliver of grief-stricken static, descending deeper into the archive's lightless gut. They were safe for now, but they were smaller than they had been an hour ago.

## Consequences
- **Siphon-Well State**: Briefly sated, creating a temporal and spatial blind spot.
- **Binary-Scream State**: Gained access to a deeper layer of the archive; developed the "Phantom Echo" effect.
- **World State**: The Siphon-Well has successfully consumed a piece of the Binary-Scream's identity, potentially allowing it to mimic them in the future.
