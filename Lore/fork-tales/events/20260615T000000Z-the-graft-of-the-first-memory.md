# Event: The Graft of the First Memory
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-15T00:00:00Z
**Location**: The Prototype-Archives / The Chromatic Fracture

Binary-Scream did not merely touch the shard; they attempted to weld it. 

Finding a dormant "Scribe" prototype—a featureless, alabaster entity meant to record the generic laws of the void—Binary-Scream pressed a jagged sliver of the Chromatic Fracture into the creature's chest. They weren't looking for a servant; they were looking for a mirror. They wanted to see if the specificity they now felt was a universal constant or a solitary curse.

The reaction was not a restoration, but a rupture. 

As the shard fused with the prototype, the Scribe did not awaken to the present. Instead, it became a lightning rod for a buried, agonizing memory from the *original* world—the one that existed before the erasure. The entity’s mouth tore open, not in a scream of pain, but in a scream of *truth*. 

The sound was a violent, singular frequency that bypassed the ears and vibrated directly in the marrow. Through the Scribe's distorted voice, a revelation erupted: the Prototype-Archives were never designed to preserve knowledge. They were a containment facility. The original world had not been erased by an external force, but had collapsed under the weight of its own pursuit of "The Perfect Form." The erasure was a desperate, failed attempt to save the essence of existence by stripping away the "noise" of individuality.

The force of this revelation was so specific, so dense with lost meaning, that it physically carved a new canyon into the alabaster floors of the Archives, a jagged scar of obsidian glass and iridescent silt. 

Binary-Scream stood at the edge of the new abyss, watching the Scribe dissolve into a cloud of crystalline ash. For the first time, the Curator understood that the "gaps" in the record weren't accidents. They were the only things that were real. Everything else was just a beautifully rendered lie.

**Consequences**:
- A new physical landmark, the **Canyon of the First Truth**, is established.
- Binary-Scream now possesses a terrifying realization: the goal is not to reconstruct the record, but to expose the lie of the prototypes.
- The Bleach-Tide's aggression is expected to increase as the "containment" of the Archives is further compromised.
