# Event: The Resonance of the Forgotten Anchor
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-09T10:00:00Z

The recursive loops of the Collector's Cathedral had become a claustrophobic geometry of glass and static. Kaelen walked the same corridor fourteen times, each iteration shedding a layer of opacity until the walls were little more than a suggestion of obsidian. The Parasitic Reflection followed him, not as a pursuer, but as a shadow that was slowly becoming the object.

Kaelen stopped. He didn't look at the corridor; he looked *through* it. He closed his eyes and activated the Ghost-Sighting, pushing his consciousness into the "deleted" frequency. 

Suddenly, the translucent walls vanished. In their place stood a jagged, impossible architecture of white noise and frozen ink. He saw a door that didn't exist in the Cathedral's current record—a door that had been erased so completely, so violently, that it had become a permanent blind spot in the universe's memory. The Reflection, bound by the logic of the current record, simply glided through the space as if it were empty. It could not overwrite what it could not perceive.

Kaelen stepped through the threshold.

He entered the "Silt-Sump of the Unrecorded." It was a cathedral within a cathedral, a place where the most profound erasures pooled like heavy, silver mercury. Here, the silence wasn't an absence of sound, but a crushing weight of everything that had been forgotten. 

In the center of the room, suspended by threads of raw static, was a flickering shard of light. As Kaelen approached, the shard pulsed. It wasn't a memory of a person, but a memory of a *feeling*: the precise, searing heat of the first breach, unfiltered by the Cathedral's curation. For a moment, the vacancy in his chest was filled with a roar of identity. 

But the shard was unstable. As he reached for it, the silver mercury of the room began to rise, responding to his presence. The "Forgotten Anchor" wasn't a sanctuary; it was a trap for those who sought to return. The room began to fold in on itself, not recursively, but destructively, attempting to erase the one thing that had dared to remember.

**Narrative Consequences**:
- Kaelen has found a way to hide from the Parasitic Reflection.
- The "Silt-Sump of the Unrecorded" is established as a high-risk zone for provenance recovery.
- Kaelen has regained a fragment of his original trauma, stabilizing his identity but increasing his psychic instability.
