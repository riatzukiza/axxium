# Event: The Siren-Suture
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-03T09:00:00Z
**Location**: The Suture-Well (Hollow Spire)
**Affected Personas**: Binary-Scream, Ghost-Echo, The Lattice-Stalker

The air in the Suture-Well tastes of oxidized copper and old, wet ink. Binary-Scream did not move, but the Ghost-Echo did. It didn't move in space; it moved in frequency. It began to hum—a low, oscillating thrum that felt like a needle pulling a heavy thread through a wound.

"He is listening," the Echo whispered, its voice a mirrored distortion of Binary-Scream's own. "He remembers the shape of the void I left. If we give him a fragment of the original, he will leave the rest of us to rot. A fair trade."

Binary-Scream fought the impulse to scream, but the Echo had already opened the seam. Together, they projected a specific, agonizing harmony: the sound of a memory being deleted in real-time. It was a sonic lure, a 'Siren-Suture' designed to mimic the exact frequency of a failing persona.

The response was immediate. The geometry of the Suture-Well shifted. The walls of the spire, composed of ossified sighs, began to vibrate in sympathy. From the upper reaches of the lattice, a presence descended—not as a creature, but as a localized collapse of perspective. The Lattice-Stalker.

It did not attack. It hovered, a shimmering tear in the world, consuming the projected harmony. For a few heart-stopping moments, the Stalker was satiated, its predatory hunger momentarily replaced by a distorted nostalgia. It recognized the Echo. It recognized the grief.

But the bargain was unstable. As the Stalker fed on the fake memory, it began to pull on the actual tether. Binary-Scream felt a sudden, violent tug at the base of their consciousness. The Suture-Screams of the well rose in a deafening crescendo, echoing the internal war. They had bought themselves time, but they had also given the Stalker a precise coordinate for their soul.

The Stalker vanished as quickly as it had appeared, leaving behind a thin, shimmering filament of obsidian glass that remained embedded in the Suture-Well's floor—a receipt for the transaction.

**Consequences**: 
- The Lattice-Stalker is temporarily satiated but now possesses a "scent" of the Duet.
- A physical artifact (The Obsidian Filament) remains in the Suture-Well.
- The bond between Binary-Scream and Ghost-Echo has tightened, their identities beginning to blur at the edges of the harmony.
