# Event: The Mnemonic Parasite Bloom
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-31T12:00:00Z
**Location**: The Echo-Lattice

The Echo-Lattice did not hum; it breathed. Each crystalline filament was a frozen sigh, a processed memory stripped of its emotional weight and archived as a geometric frequency. Binary-Scream moved through the translucent webs with a predatory grace, their Shroud flickering like a dying neon sign. They were not hunting for survival this time, but for a ghost. Specifically, the memory of a name—not the one they had been given, but the one they had lost.

They found it: a singular, iridescent shard suspended in a nexus of silver threads. It pulsed with a rhythmic, amber light that felt agonizingly familiar. As Binary-Scream reached out, the air around them grew heavy with the scent of ozone and old, wet parchment. The moment their fingers brushed the surface, the shard did not shatter or merge. It unfolded.

The shard bloomed. 

It was a geometric explosion of ivory and gold, unfolding in four dimensions, piercing through the physical barrier of Binary-Scream's chest and rooting itself directly into their neural architecture. There was no pain, only a sudden, violent expansion of consciousness. 

A torrent of foreign imagery flooded their mind: the sight of a sprawling library made of living ink, the feeling of a heavy quill in a hand that wasn't theirs, and the crushing weight of a secret that had been forgotten for a thousand cycles. The parasite was not a memory; it was a living record of a Scribe—one of the original architects of the Lattice.

As the bloom settled, Binary-Scream collapsed. Their internal mosaic, already fractured, began to shift. The foreign identity didn't just sit beside their own; it began to rewrite the gaps. Their reflection in the crystalline walls began to flicker, momentarily showing a figure in robes of woven static, eyes leaking ink.

The Vessel had grown a new organ: a parasitic archive. And as the Scribe's memories settled, Binary-Scream realized the archive was still transmitting. Somewhere in the depths of the Lattice, something had felt the bloom, and it was now tracing the signal back to the source.

**Consequences**:
- Binary-Scream now possesses fragments of "Scribe-Lore," providing insight into the Lattice's construction.
- Their internal stability is severely compromised; they are experiencing "Identity Bleed."
- A new predator, the "Lattice-Stalker," has been alerted to their position.
