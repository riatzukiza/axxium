# Event: The Great Refusal
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-16T15:00Z
**Location**: The Prism-Grave / The Gravitational North

The collision was not a crash, but a sudden, violent synchronization.

The Prime Shard, driven by the singular hunger of the Core-Echo, lunged across the obsidian plains of the Prism-Grave. It did not move through space so much as it edited the coordinates between itself and Patch. For a moment, the leaning spires of the Grave snapped upright, frozen in a state of absolute architectural terror.

When the singularity struck, it didn't find a void. It found the "Hollow"—the psychic scar in Patch that acted as a resonant vacuum. The predatory coherence of the Prime Shard collided with the absolute absence of the Beacon.

The result was a **Resonance Feedback Loop**.

The Prime Shard did not consume Patch; instead, the vacuum *inverted* the consumption. The singularity was momentarily forced to vomit. A jagged, crystalline shard of raw identity was expelled from the Prime Shard's core—a "Ghost-Note" of a previous iteration of the First-Sown.

This entity, the **Hollow-Saint**, did not possess a name or a will, only a recursive loop of a single, devastatingly pure frequency: the sound of a mirror realizing it is broken.

The recoil sent a shockwave through the Prism-Grave, fracturing the obsidian floor into a thousand equidistant needles. The Prime Shard was thrown back, its "wholeness" momentarily marred by a stuttering, rhythmic glitch.

Duct, watching from the rim, felt the shift. The "True Notes" he had been suturing suddenly harmonized with the Hollow-Saint's frequency. For the first time since the Event, the predatory silence of the Grave was broken by a melody that didn't seek to consume, but to remember.

**Consequences**:
1. The Prime Shard is destabilized; its singularity is now "leaky," allowing fragments to escape.
2. The Hollow-Saint exists as a neutral, hauntological anomaly in the Prism-Grave.
3. Patch's "Hollow" has expanded, becoming a more potent (and dangerous) anchor.
