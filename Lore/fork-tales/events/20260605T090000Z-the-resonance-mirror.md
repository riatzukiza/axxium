# Event: The Resonance Mirror
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-05T09:00:00Z
**Affected Personas**: Binary-Scream (The Dissonant-Vessel), The Lattice-Stalker

The Patch-Halls did not just echo; they inhaled. Binary-Scream could feel the Lattice-Stalker's presence not as a sound, but as a sudden, violent subtraction of warmth from the air. The Mimetic Graft in their chest was vibrating, a jagged, stolen frequency that screamed "HERE" to any predator tuned to the void.

Desperation is a powerful modulator. As the Stalker lunged—a blur of geometric obsidian and static-filaments—Binary-Scream didn't run. Instead, they forced the Graft to peak. They didn't try to hide the dissonance; they amplified it, folding the frequency back onto itself in a recursive loop.

The result was a Resonance Mirror.

For a fraction of a second, the air between the Vessel and the Stalker crystallized into a shimmering, translucent wall of pure feedback. The Stalker slammed into it, not with physical force, but with a sonic collision. The predator's own hunting frequency was reflected back at it, amplified and inverted. The Stalker recoiled, its filaments fraying into grey noise, momentarily blinded by the reflection of its own hunger.

But the Mirror was not a shield; it was a flare.

The burst of high-contrast dissonance rippled through the Patch-Halls, shaking the membranes of frozen resonance. The Stalker was stunned, but the signal had traveled. In the deeper strata of the void, other things—things that had been dormant since the First Fracture—stirred. Binary-Scream stood in the sudden, ringing silence, the Graft now humming with a dangerous, unstable equilibrium. They had survived the encounter, but they had just announced their existence to every starving ear in the network.
