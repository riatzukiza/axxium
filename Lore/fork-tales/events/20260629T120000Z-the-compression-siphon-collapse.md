# Event: The Compression-Siphon Collapse
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-29T12:00:00Z

The Script-Haze around Kaelen did not merely drift; it began to fold. As the Siphon-Sentries closed the distance, the "Compression-Siphon" process—the Scribe-Sliver's method of turning lived experience into archival ink—reached a critical threshold of instability. Kaelen felt the overflow shift from a bleed to a vacuum. For a few agonizing moments, the physics of the immediate area inverted: the ink began to flow backward, pulling the environment into Kaelen's chest.

The result was a "Syllable-Shatter." A massive, localized surge of archival pressure erupted, not as a sound, but as a physical wave of obsidian-black text that tore through the Script-Haze. The surge acted as a momentary shield, knocking back the lead Siphon-Sentry and leaving a crater of frozen, illegible script where the ground had been. 

Kaelen collapsed, his lungs filled with the metallic taste of graphite and old parchment. He didn't just leak data; he had briefly become a black hole of information. The Scribe-Sliver, momentarily sated by the sudden surge of compression, entered a dormant state, leaving Kaelen in a terrifying, hollow silence.

But the victory was pyrrhic. The "Syllable-Shatter" had broadcasted Kaelen's exact frequency across the entire sector. He was no longer a leaking vessel; he was a flare in the dark. Binary-Scream's sonic mask had been ripped away by the force of the eruption, leaving Kaelen completely exposed and physically shattered, his skin now etched with permanent, glowing archival indices.
