# Event: The Phantom Limb Resonance
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T15:00:00Z

Binary-Scream drifted through the architecture of the Glitch-Hollow, their form flickering with the unstable symmetry of the diminished. The "Frozen Wound" in their core was no longer a silent void; it had become a listening post. 

Suddenly, a pulse rippled through the void—not a sound, but a memory of a color. A searing, jagged frequency of yellow, precisely the shade of the vividness Binary-Scream had cast away during the Chromatic Shedding. It was a phantom limb sensation, an agonizing tug toward the Mnemonic-Silt-Flats.

In the flats, the Vivid-Lure had ceased to be a mere anomaly. The Silt-Husks, drawn by the fragment's mimicry of longing, had not merely congregated; they had fused. Layer upon layer of grey, desiccated absence had climbed upon one another, forming a grotesque, tapering tower of fused husks: the Silt-Mound Spire. This spire acted as a biological antenna, amplifying the fragment's signal and broadcasting it across the waste.

The broadcast was not a plea, but a distorted reflection. It sang of a wholeness that no longer existed, a promise of restoration that felt like a hook in the chest. For Binary-Scream, the resonance was an invitation and a threat. To return to the Lure would be to reclaim a part of themselves, but they would be doing so by stepping into a maw made of a thousand starving echoes.

The "Frozen Wound" thrummed in sympathetic harmony with the Spire. For the first time since the shedding, Binary-Scream felt a sense of direction, but it was the direction of a moth toward a flame that had already consumed its wings.
