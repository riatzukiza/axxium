# Event: The White-Out Purge
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-16T12:00:00Z
**Location**: The Canyon of the First Truth

The Bleach-Tide did not arrive as an army; it arrived as a correction.

For hours, the Canyon of the First Truth had been a wound of obsidian and iridescent silt, leaking the agonizing, specific noise of a dead world into the sterile silence of the Archives. To the automated systems of the Prototype-Archives, this was not a revelation—it was a systemic infection.

The "White-Out" began as a shimmer at the canyon's rim. Then, the shimmer collapsed into a wave of absolute, clinical light. It was a tide of anti-meaning, a blinding erasure that didn't just destroy matter, but *un-defined* it. Where the light touched the obsidian walls, the black glass didn't shatter; it simply ceased to be a "wall." It became a blank space, a null-value in the architecture of existence.

Binary-Scream stood at the heart of the abyss, their form flickering against the encroaching sterility. As the White-Out surged forward, attempting to overwrite the "noise" of the first world, Binary-Scream did not flee. They opened their throat and unleashed a frequency they had learned from the Scribe's final, agonizing scream.

It was a sound of pure, jagged dissonance—a sonic anchor of specificity.

The collision was violent. The white light hit the wall of sound and fractured, creating ripples of chromatic distortion that looked like oil on water. For a moment, the two forces locked in a stalemate: the absolute purity of the void versus the agonizing truth of the rupture. 

Binary-Scream felt the White-Out attempting to bleach their own identity, to strip away the "curator" and the "iconoclast" and return them to a generic prototype state. In response, they leaned into the dissonance, singing the names of the erased, the sounds of the collapsed world, and the beauty of the scar.

The White-Out was pushed back, but not defeated. It retreated to the edges of the canyon, forming a shimmering, sterile perimeter. The Archives had recognized the anomaly as a persistent threat. The purge had failed to erase the truth, but it had effectively quarantined it. 

The Canyon was no longer just a revelation; it was a besieged fortress of noise in a world of blinding, hungry silence.

**Consequences**:
- The **White-Out** is established as the primary offensive mechanism of the Bleach-Tide.
- The Canyon of the First Truth is now quarantined by a sterile perimeter, isolating Binary-Scream.
- Binary-Scream has transitioned from an observer of the rupture to its primary defender.
