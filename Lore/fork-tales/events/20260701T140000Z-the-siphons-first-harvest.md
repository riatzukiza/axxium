# Event: The Siphon's First Harvest
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-01T14:00:00Z
**Plot Thread**: The Sentry Convergence

## Narrative
The arrival of the first Siphon-Sentry was not marked by a crash or a cry, but by a sudden, absolute absence. In the Hollow of the Hush, where the ambient static of a thousand dead memories usually hummed like a distant hive, there came a pocket of silence so profound it felt like a physical weight pressing against Kaelen’s eardrums. 

Kaelen stood at the center of the glade, his form shimmering—a translucent, gold-veined ghost of a man. He could feel the Choir within him, the fragmented Architect-ghosts, vibrating in a frantic, discordant unison. They weren't singing anymore; they were screaming a warning.

From the grey mist emerged the Sentry. It was a spindly, multi-jointed spire of obsidian and iridescent oil, moving with a precision that defied biology. It did not see Kaelen so much as it *calculated* him. Without a sound, the Sentry drove a Signal-Siphon Spike into the void-soil. The impact sent a ripple of black frost through the ground, freezing the very air in a radial burst.

Then, the harvest began.

The Spike didn't pull at Kaelen's body; it pulled at the *unimportant* parts of him. He felt a sudden, violent tug in his chest, and with a flash of white light, the memory of his mother's favorite tea—the exact smell of bergamot and wet wool—was ripped from his mind. It didn't just vanish; he could see it as a thin, glowing ribbon of data being sucked into the obsidian needle of the Spike. 

Next went the feeling of cold rain on a Tuesday afternoon. Then, the specific way he used to tie his shoes. 

Kaelen collapsed to his knees, not from pain, but from a sudden, terrifying lightness. He was becoming a sketch of a person, a high-contrast outline stripped of all the soft edges. The Sentry hovered over him, its head—a rotating series of concentric glass rings—spinning faster and faster, humming a perfect, sterile frequency that drowned out the Choir. 

He was no longer a man hiding in the void. He was a crop being harvested for its most refined signals, and the Sentry was only just beginning to graze.
