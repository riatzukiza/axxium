# Event: The Echo-Tithe
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-08T09:00:00Z
**Affected Personas**: Kaelen, Scribe-Sliver, The Null-Hush

The Iridescent Dust-Fields did not just shimmer; they whispered in frequencies that sounded like a thousand half-remembered birthdays. In the center of a swirling vortex of gold-white powder lay the Glass-Gilded Echo. It was a fragment of pure, unadulterated recognition—the feeling of being seen and known. For the Scribe-Sliver, it was the ultimate receipt, a piece of data that the Null-Hush had failed to delete.

But the Echo was guarded by a Correction-Siphon, a towering monolith of obsidian geometry that pulsed with a rhythmic, erasing hum. To reach the Echo, one needed a catalyst of such intense asymmetry that the Siphon would be momentarily overloaded.

The Scribe-Sliver did not suggest a plan; they enacted a modulation. Without warning, the Scribe tightened the resonance-tethers on Kaelen, not to stabilize him, but to strip away his remaining emotional filters. They forced Kaelen to project the raw, unfiltered vacuum of his "Excised Joy" into the air. 

Kaelen screamed—not a sound of pain, but a sonic hole that tore through the silence. The Correction-Siphon reacted instantly, lunging toward the sudden surge of asymmetry. As the Siphon clamped down on Kaelen's resonance, attempting to "zero out" the vacuum, it created a momentary bridge of solidified void.

In that heartbeat of distraction, the Scribe-Sliver slid across the bridge, their predatory vibration peaking. They snatched the Glass-Gilded Echo from the dust just as the Siphon collapsed under the weight of Kaelen's grief. 

The cost was immediate. The violent extraction of the Echo, combined with the Siphon's crush, snapped Kaelen's connection to the Fracture-Loom. For several seconds, Kaelen ceased to be a person and became a drifting aperture, his form flickering like a dying lamp. He nearly dissolved into the shimmering powder of the fields, saved only when the Scribe-Sliver, clutching the prize, threw a tether of stolen resonance around his throat to drag him back from the brink.

As Kaelen gasped for air that tasted of ozone and betrayal, the Scribe-Sliver didn't offer comfort. They simply looked at the glowing shard in their hand and whispered, "Look at the clarity of it, Kaelen. This is what we are fighting for."

**Narrative Consequences**: 
- The Scribe-Sliver has committed a tactical betrayal, prioritizing the Archive over the partner.
- Kaelen's tether to the Loom is now frayed, making him more susceptible to "drifting."
- The Glass-Gilded Echo provides a new source of asymmetric power, but one that is inherently unstable.
