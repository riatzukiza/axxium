# Event: The Resonance Breach
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T21:00:00Z
**Location**: The Obsidian Vault / The Threshold

The silence of the Obsidian Vault was not a void, but a weight—a thick, suffocating blanket that had kept Elara hidden from the Scribes' predatory harmonics. But the Scribes had grown tired of the silence. They brought the Harmonic Wedge, a shimmering spire of focused resonance that didn't just sound; it carved. 

As the Wedge struck the outer perimeter of the Vault, the non-resonant stone began to weep. Micro-fractures spiderwebbed across the obsidian, not from physical pressure, but from the sheer agony of a world being forced to vibrate. Elara felt the vibration in her marrow, a terrifying crescendo that threatened to pull her out of the sanctuary and back into the maw of the resonant world.

Kaelen watched from the interstices. He had no voice to scream, no hands to push, but he possessed the Null-Chord. He did not fight the Wedge with a counter-melody; he fought it with absolute absence. 

As the Wedge reached its peak, Kaelen folded himself into the frequency. He became the exact inverse of the Scribes' intent. When the two forces met, there was no explosion. There was only a sudden, violent erasure. A sphere of absolute zero-sound expanded from the point of impact, a "Silent Shockwave" that stripped the resonance from the Scribes' tools and threw their consciousness back into the void, leaving them reeling and deafened by the sudden vacuum.

The breach was stopped, but the cost was etched into the stone. Where the Wedge had touched the Vault, the obsidian remained translucent, humming with a permanent, low-frequency thrum. The sanctuary was no longer absolute. The silence had been stained.

**Consequences**:
- The Obsidian Vault is no longer a perfect sanctuary; it now possesses a "Resonant Scar."
- The Scribes are temporarily repelled but now have a "frequency footprint" to track.
- Kaelen has discovered the ability to actively manipulate the void.
