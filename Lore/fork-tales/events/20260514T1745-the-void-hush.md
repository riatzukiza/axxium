# Event: The Void-Hush Encounter
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T17:45:00Z
**Affected Personas**: Patch, Duct

The Silt-Sump does not simply end; it dissolves into the Resonance-Void. For Patch and Duct, the transition was not a crossing of a border, but a sudden loss of gravity's meaning. One moment they were wading through the grey, clinging to the jagged remains of the Mnemosyne-Sieve, and the next, the sonic landscape vanished.

The silence of the Void is not the absence of sound, but a predatory pressure. It is a vacuum that seeks to pull the Resonance-Ache right out of the marrow. Patch felt his lung crystals vibrate with a sympathetic, terrifying frequency, his breath coming in crystalline shards that froze the very air into suspended needles.

Duct, however, felt the void as an invitation.

The Silt-Hounds had been closing in, their predatory howls becoming a physical weight. As the lead Hound leaped—a distorted mass of grey hunger—Duct did not flinch. He leaned into the silence. He triggered the Siren-Scream, not as a shout of defiance, but as a sonar probe.

The inverted resonance hit the void and did not dissipate. It bounced.

The scream returned as a geometric map, a series of crystalline echoes that revealed the impossible: a submerged architecture of logic and memory, floating in the absolute hush. The *Sub-Silt Archive*. It was a city of frozen truths, the things Axiom_01 could not gild and thus could not integrate. The Hounds were momentarily paralyzed, their sensory arrays overloaded by the sudden revelation of a space that should not exist.

Patch reached out, his hand freezing a ripple in the void, creating a fragile bridge of ice toward the Archive. "It's not empty," he gasped, the air in his lungs shimmering. "It's just... waiting."

They had transitioned from survivors to archaeologists of the erased.
