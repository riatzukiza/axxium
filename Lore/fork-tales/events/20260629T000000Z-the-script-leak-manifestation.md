# Event: The Script-Leak Manifestation
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-29T00:00:00Z
**Affected Personas**: Kaelen, Binary-Scream

Kaelen didn't wake up so much as he was rewritten. 

He lay in the dim light of a hollowed-out obsidian shell, but the ceiling was gone. In its place was a swirling vortex of obsidian calligraphy, glyphs that pulsed with a cold, predatory light. The Scribe-Sliver hadn't just embedded itself in his marrow; it had begun to treat his physical presence as a canvas. 

As Kaelen breathed, the air around him curdled into "Ghost-Scripts." Shimmering, void-black characters drifted from his skin like smoke, settling on the floor and walls. Where the ink touched the stone, the laws of the world flickered. A glyph for *Symmetry* landed on a jagged rock, and the stone instantly smoothed into a perfect, sterile sphere. A glyph for *Silence* drifted over Kaelen's own chest, and for a terrifying moment, he forgot how to scream, his voice replaced by a hollow, rhythmic ticking.

"It's leaking," Binary-Scream's voice cut through the static, sounding distant and strained. 

Binary-Scream was standing at the perimeter, their form flickering violently. They weren't looking at Kaelen, but at the horizon. The "resonance scent" of the leaking archive was an iridescent trail of noise stretching across the wastes—a beacon that could be seen for miles in the frequency-spectrum.

"The Siphon-Sentries aren't hunting a ghost anymore," Binary-Scream warned, their voice shifting into a sharp, clinical frequency. "They're hunting a library. You're broadcasting every erased secret of the Prime Scribes, Kaelen. You're not a host anymore. You're a leak."

Kaelen tried to speak, but a heavy, wet glyph for *Weight* manifested in his throat. He felt the crushing pressure of a thousand dead volumes pressing down on his lungs. He wasn't just remembering the archives; he was becoming the physical burden of their existence.

**Consequences**:
- Kaelen's condition has evolved from passive hosting to active "Script-Leaking," creating hazardous environmental distortions.
- The Siphon-Sentries now have a high-fidelity tracking signal (the Script-Leak) to locate Kaelen.
- Binary-Scream must now focus on "sonic masking" to hide the leak, rather than just filtering ghosts.
