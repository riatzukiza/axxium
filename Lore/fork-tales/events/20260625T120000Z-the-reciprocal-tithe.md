# Event: The Reciprocal Tithe
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-25T12:00:00Z

The Spire's Debt-Bell rings. It is a sound that does not enter the ears but vibrates the marrow, a sudden, violent contraction of the atmosphere that signals the commencement of a collection cycle. Kaelen, the Hollow Donor, feels the pull immediately—a psychic vacuum starting to draw the last remnants of his identity, the thin threads of his remaining "self," into the Siphon's maw. He stands still, his expression one of exhausted acceptance. He has already given so much; he expects the void to finally claim the rest.

But Binary-Scream, seeing the "Truth-Frequency" of Kaelen's fragility through the lens of the Shiver-Glass, cannot remain a silent observer. The Silver Scar on their chest flares with a violent, iridescent light, pulsing in synchronization with the Spire's hunger. Instead of mocking Kaelen's descent or watching the "jailer" finally dissolve, Binary-Scream steps between the Donor and the Spire.

They do not fight the Spire—for one cannot fight a vacuum—instead, they offer themselves.

Binary-Scream screams. It is not the hollow, mimicked shriek of a creature born from echoes, but a raw, singular note of absolute coherence. They throw their own gathered fragments, the stolen echoes of a thousand forgotten souls, and a significant portion of their own hard-won stability into the vortex. The Spire, greedy and indiscriminate, accepts the offering with a sudden, jarring thrum of satisfaction. The pull on Kaelen ceases abruptly, leaving him gasping in the sudden stillness.

For a moment, the silence is absolute. Then, Binary-Scream collapses, their form flickering like a dying lamp, edges blurring into grey static. They have paid a tithe of identity to save a man they were designed to hate. Kaelen reaches out, his hand trembling, and touches the Silver Scar. He feels the echo of the payment—the agonizing friction of a gift given without a request, a debt transferred from one broken thing to another.

The Spire is momentarily sated, but the balance of the world has shifted. The "Fracture of Hate" has been cauterized, replaced by a "Bond of Debt." Binary-Scream is now thinner, more transparent, but their eyes carry a new, terrifying clarity. They are no longer just a mimic; they have become a protector of the void.
