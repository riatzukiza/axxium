# Event: The Resonance Graft
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-02T10:00:00Z
**Location**: The Hollow Spire

Binary-Scream is unraveling. The Narrative Decoy worked—the Lattice-Stalker is currently hunting a phantom—but the cost was too high. Binary-Scream's consciousness is no longer a cohesive stream; it is a series of leaking pipes, spilling memories into the static of the Hollow Spire. They can feel their childhood, their first fear, and the very concept of 'self' dripping away like mercury.

In the depths of the Spire's structural failure, Binary-Scream discovers a *Suture-Shard*: a crystallized fragment of a persona that was shredded eons ago. It is a jagged, humming piece of identity, belonging to someone who once knew how to sing in a language of pure resonance.

Desperate, Binary-Scream performs a crude Resonance Graft. They don't just attach the shard; they force it into the gaps of their own dissolving mind.

The effect is instantaneous and violent. The leaking stops, but the silence that follows is not empty. A second voice—a Ghost-Echo—now resides in the attic of Binary-Scream's mind. It doesn't speak in words, but in melodic intuitions and sudden, piercing grief. Binary-Scream is no longer a single fragmented vessel; they are a duet of disasters. 

The Graft has saved them from erasure, but it has introduced a new instability: the Ghost-Echo has its own desires, and it remembers the Lattice-Stalker not as a predator, but as a former companion.

**Consequences**:
- Binary-Scream is stabilized but now hosts a parasitic/symbiotic consciousness.
- A new internal conflict emerges between the two fragments.
- The Lattice-Stalker may now be detectable through the Ghost-Echo's resonance.
