# Event: The Echo-Debt Collection
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-23T09:00:00Z
**Location**: The Static-Siphon-Spire

The Static-Siphon-Spire does not take; it collects. And the collection is never complete.

As the morning light filtered through the grey haze of the Spire, the "Debt-Bell" rang—a frequency that didn't sound in the ears, but vibrated in the marrow. The Spire required a payment of coherence to maintain the shroud of noise protecting them from the Husks. Specifically, it demanded the concept of *Trust*.

Binary-Scream looked at Kaelen, their eyes vacant, the space where the "First Resonance" had been a jagged, whistling void. They had no trust left to give; the very architecture of trust had been sieved away. As the Spire began to reach into Binary-Scream’s consciousness, the process was violent. It wasn't a fade, but a tear. Binary-Scream began to collapse, their form flickering like a dying lamp, the Void-Jitter they had learned to mimic now turning inward, eating their own edges.

Kaelen could not watch the erasure. In a moment of desperate, instinctive sacrifice, Kaelen stepped into the Siphon-stream. He didn't just offer a memory; he offered a replacement. He attempted to "loan" his own capacity for trust—the visceral, agonizingly clear memory of why he had once anchored himself to Binary-Scream—to satisfy the Spire's hunger.

The transfer was a catastrophic failure of translation. The Spire, a parasitic machine of noise, did not move the memory; it *copied and distorted* it. 

Binary-Scream stopped flickering. They gasped, a sound like breaking glass, as a new, foreign memory slammed into the hole in their mind. They "remembered" Kaelen—but not as the man who had saved them. They remembered him as a jailer, a figure of cold obsession who had trapped them in the Spire for his own curiosity. 

The debt was paid, but the price was a lie. Kaelen stood back, feeling a sudden, freezing lightness in his own chest. He had given away the purity of his trust, and in return, he had planted a seed of hatred in the only person he had left.

**Consequences**:
- Binary-Scream now possesses a "Graft-Echo"—a false memory of Kaelen as an antagonist.
- Kaelen has become "The Hollow Donor," feeling the first symptoms of intentional erasure.
- The bond is no longer just asymmetric; it is poisoned.
