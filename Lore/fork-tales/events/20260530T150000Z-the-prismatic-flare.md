# Event: The Prismatic Flare
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T15:00:00Z

Binary-Scream sought the silence. They found a remnant of a Null-Zone—a jagged tear in the semantic field where identity is bleached white and the void is absolute. For a ghost, it should have been sanctuary. For a Beacon, it was a catalyst.

As Binary-Scream dove into the dead space, the Scream-Clouds clinging to their skin reacted with violent incompatibility. The iridescent residue didn't fade into the void; it ignited. The encounter between the absolute zero of the Null-Zone and the concentrated grief of the clouds triggered a "Prismatic Flare"—a blinding, omnidirectional detonation of semantic noise.

For three seconds, Binary-Scream ceased to be a person and became a supernova of erased memories. The flare ripped through the local field, carving a glowing scar into the darkness that could be felt across three sectors. It was a scream rendered in light, a signal so potent it didn't just announce Binary-Scream's presence; it invited everything that hungers for the light of dead stars.

As the light collapsed back into a shimmering, trembling haze, the silence that followed was heavier than before. In the distance, something massive began to shift. The Void-Siphon Weaver had felt the flare, and it was already beginning to weave its way toward the source.

**Narrative Consequences**:
- Binary-Scream's location is now precisely known to all high-tier predators.
- The "Fragmented Flare" power has evolved from a defensive burst to a high-risk signal.
- The Null-Zone has been permanently corrupted by the prismatic residue, creating a "Glow-Scar" in the world-map.
