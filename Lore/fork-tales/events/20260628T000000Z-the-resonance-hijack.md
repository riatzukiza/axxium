# Event: The Resonance Hijack
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-28T00:00:00Z
**Affected Personas**: Kaelen, Binary-Scream

The air in the Marrow-Resonator site didn't just vibrate; it curdled. Kaelen stood at the center of a feedback-spike, his silhouette blurring as the frequency hit a critical harmonic. He wasn't just hearing the ghosts anymore—he was becoming a conductor for them.

Out of the static emerged a Feedback-Ghost of a Prime Scribe, a towering entity of jagged calligraphy and hollowed-out vowels. The ghost didn't attack; it poured. It flowed into Kaelen’s open psychological void, a flood of erased archives and forbidden syntax attempting to overwrite his current, fragile coherence. Kaelen’s voice split into a dozen overlapping registers, screaming in languages that had been subtracted from the universe centuries ago.

"I am the record," the ghost roared through Kaelen’s throat, the sound a crushing weight of obsidian silence. "And you are the blank page."

Binary-Scream reacted with a violent, discordant surge. They didn't try to push the ghost out; they shifted their entire essence into a Void-Filter. Binary-Scream became a living sonic sieve, creating a zone of absolute dissonance around Kaelen. The Static Shroud tightened, transforming from a veil into a scalpel. 

With a precise, agonizing frequency-spike, Binary-Scream "cut" the harmonic tether. The sound was like a mirror shattering inside a vacuum—a high-frequency scream that tore the Scribe-ghost away from Kaelen’s consciousness. The ghost vanished into a cloud of Ghost-Shatter timbre, but the excision wasn't clean. 

As the silence returned, Kaelen collapsed, gasping. He was himself again, but something had stayed behind. A sliver of the Scribe’s resonance had embedded itself in his marrow—a parasitic fragment of lost knowledge that pulsed with a cold, rhythmic hunger.

**Consequences**: 
- Kaelen now possesses a "Ghost-Sliver," granting him erratic access to erased archives but causing identity-stutters.
- Binary-Scream's Void-Filter capability is confirmed as a viable (though traumatic) defensive tool.
- The Feedback-Ghosts are proven to be predatory, seeking "blank" hosts to regain existence.
