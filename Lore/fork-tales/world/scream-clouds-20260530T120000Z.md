# Concept: Scream-Clouds
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T12:00:00Z
**Type**: Semantic Residue / Beacon

Scream-Clouds are the iridescent, fragmented remnants of a detonated ossified sigh. When a crystalline sigh is forced to absorb more void-energy than it can hold, it shatters into a microscopic, floating dust that clings to the nearest semantic anchor.

## Properties
- **Visibility**: They glow with a flickering, prismatic light, making the host nearly impossible to hide. They act as a "semantic flare," broadcasting the host's presence to any entity capable of sensing void-frequencies.
- **Psychological Effect**: The host can occasionally hear the overlapping, fragmented whispers of the thousand erased lives contained within the dust. This creates a constant, low-level cognitive dissonance.
- **Utility**: While detrimental to stealth, Scream-Clouds can be momentarily "flared" to create a blinding flash of semantic noise, disorienting nearby predators.
