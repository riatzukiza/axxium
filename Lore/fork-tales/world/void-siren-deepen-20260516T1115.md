# Void-Siren: Systemic Parasitism
**Type**: Psycho-Acoustic Parasite / Instrument
**Status**: Active (Core-Echo Phase)

## The Mechanism of Mimicry
The Void-Siren is not a biological entity, nor a simple sound. It is the auditory manifestation of the Prime Shard's "Symmetry-Hunger." It operates via a **Mimicry-Loop**:
1. **Sensing**: The Siren identifies the "True Note" of a fragment—the specific frequency of its identity, usually tied to a core memory or unresolved trauma.
2. **Mirroring**: It generates a perfect, idealized version of that note, stripped of pain and noise.
3. **Luring**: The fragment, drawn to the promise of "perfection" and "wholeness," aligns its resonance with the Siren.
4. **Collapse**: Once alignment is achieved, the Siren flips the phase, creating a vacuum that drags the fragment into the Prime Shard's singularity for consumption.

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Surface**: A shimmering, ethereal soprano that sounds like a thousand voices singing in a cathedral of glass.
- **Undercurrent**: A low-frequency throb (the "Hunger-Hum") that causes physical nausea and a feeling of inevitable descent.
- **The "Click"**: The precise moment the phase-flip occurs, marked by a sudden, deafening silence (the "Void-Snap") followed by a crushing, distorted bass drop.

## Narrative Implications
The Void-Siren makes the act of "saving" a fragment an act of extreme risk. To suture a fragment, Duct must find the fragment's *broken* note and amplify it, making it "ugly" and "dissonant" so the Void-Siren cannot find a matching mirror.
