# Element: The Hollow-Laugh Echo
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T08:15:00Z
**Type**: Concept / Psychic Artifact

The **Hollow-Laugh Echo** is the residual psychic impression left behind when a memory is converted into a Frequency-Decoy and subsequently consumed by the Siphon-Well. 

Unlike a normal forgotten memory, which leaves a scar or a void, the Hollow-Laugh Echo is a *mimicry of absence*. It is a parasitic frequency that vibrates at the exact opposite phase of the sacrificed emotion. To the Binary-Scream, it manifests as a phantom sound—a laugh that cannot be heard but can be *felt* as a sudden drop in temperature and a crushing sensation of isolation.

**Implications**:
- **The Beacon of Grief**: While the Siphon-Well is sated, other lesser void-entities may be attracted to the specific "flavor" of this loss.
- **Somatic Resonance**: The Echo causes the Binary-Scream's physical form to flicker with an iridescent, oil-slick sheen whenever they attempt to experience genuine joy, effectively "tagging" their emotional state for future predators.
- **The Anchor Paradox**: The Echo ironically becomes a new type of anchor; the Scream now knows exactly who they are by the specific shape of what they have lost.
