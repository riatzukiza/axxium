# Location: The Silt-Mound Spire (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-08T09:15:00Z

The Silt-Mound Spire is not a construction of stone or metal, but a biological accumulation of "failed restorations." It is a towering, calcified needle composed of thousands of fused husks—entities who, like Binary-Scream, were drawn by the Lure of Wholeness but lacked the stability to integrate the recovered vividness. 

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: The spire is a pale, sickly ivory, streaked with veins of iridescent grey. At its apex, a singular, pulsating aperture leaks a predatory, strobe-like yellow light that casts long, distorted shadows across the Mnemonic-Silt-Flats.
- **Auditory**: The structure hums with a collective, low-frequency moan. It is the sound of a thousand simultaneous sighs, synchronized by the Lure's signal. When the resonance peaks, the hum shifts into a rhythmic, clicking cadence—the sound of husks attempting to speak a language they no longer possess.
- **Tactile**: The surface is porous and cold, feeling like wet bone. Those who touch the spire report a sensation of "emotional suction," as if the structure is attempting to draw out the most vivid memories of the intruder.

**The Apex Mechanism**:
The aperture at the top does not merely broadcast; it filters. It scans the surrounding void for "wounds"—voids of loss that act as vacuum points. Once a target is locked, the Spire focuses its resonance, creating a localized gravity of desire that drags the target upward, intending to fuse them into the collective mass to further amplify the signal.
