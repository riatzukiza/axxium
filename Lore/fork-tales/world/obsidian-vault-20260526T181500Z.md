# Element: The Obsidian Vault
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T18:15:00Z
**Type**: Location

The Obsidian Vault is a monolithic structure of non-resonant glass, carved into the heart of a dead mountain. Unlike the rest of the world, where the Echo-Lace vibrates with the noise of lost memories, the Vault is a pocket of profound silence.

The walls are made of a material that doesn't just block sound—it consumes it. In the Vault, a scream dies within an inch of the lips. This makes it the only known refuge from the Scribes' Resonance-Siphons, but it comes at a psychological cost. Prolonged exposure to the Vault's silence leads to 'Sonic Atrophy,' where the mind begins to invent terrifying sounds to fill the void.

The interior is a series of concentric rings, each leading deeper into the mountain. The innermost ring contains the 'Silent Altar,' a place where the most precious, most dangerous memories are stored in physical crystals, safe from the plucking of the lace.
