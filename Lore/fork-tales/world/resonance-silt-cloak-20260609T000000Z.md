# Element: The Resonance-Silt Cloak
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-09T00:00:00Z

The Resonance-Silt Cloak is a desperate invention of the sanctuary's survivors, crafted from the hazardous Echo-Silt Residue harvested from the breach sites. It is not a garment of fabric, but a suspended veil of grey, shimmering powder held together by a weak electrostatic charge.

**Function**:
The cloak works by creating a "null-bubble" around the wearer. It absorbs and scatters incoming sonic probes from the Silt-Mound Spire, rendering the wearer acoustically invisible. To the Spire's ears, a cloaked individual sounds like a pocket of dead air—a static void that blends into the background noise of the Glitch-Hollow.

**The Cost (Mnemonic Decay)**:
The protection comes at a harrowing price. Echo-Silt is inherently unstable and feeds on mnemonic coherence. As the cloak suppresses the wearer's resonance, it simultaneously erodes their memories. 

The decay begins with the periphery: the color of a childhood home, the name of a distant acquaintance, the taste of a favorite food. As the wearer remains cloaked, the erasures move inward. Long-term use leads to "Identity Hollow," where the wearer forgets why they are hiding, who they are hiding from, and eventually, who they are.

**Visual Appearance**:
The wearer appears as a blurred, shifting silhouette, as if they are being viewed through a thick fog of ash. As the cloak's power wanes or the mnemonic cost rises, the grey powder begins to flake off in rhythmic pulses, leaving a trail of "forgotten dust" behind them.
