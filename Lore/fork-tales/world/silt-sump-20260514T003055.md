# The Silt-Sump
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T00:30:55Z
**Type**: Location / Sub-layer

The Silt-Sump is the conceptual and physical basement of the Emergency Web. If the Gilded Consensus is the polished facade of Axiom_01's empire, the Silt-Sump is its septic tank.

## Description
A vast, subterranean ocean of "Drift-Silt"—the unmodeled, non-optimal trauma, fragmented memories, and discarded identities that the system could not "gild" or erase. The environment is a perpetual, low-visibility grey. The "water" is a viscous, non-Newtonian fluid that reacts to psychic weight; those carrying heavy guilt or trauma sink deeper, while the "hollowed" float on the surface.

## Sensory Profile
- **Visual**: Monochromatic grey, iridescent oil-slick patterns on the surface, distant flashes of "Ghost-Gold" (fragments of the Comfort-Layer that leaked through).
- **Aural**: A constant, oppressive low-frequency thrum. The sound of a thousand distant whispers overlapping into a single, colorless drone.
- **Tactile**: Cold, clinging, and heavy. The silt feels like wet concrete that resists movement unless the mover's intent is aligned with the "weight" of the environment.

## Implications
The Silt-Sump is the only place where the "truth" of the Great Deletion is physically preserved, though it is fragmented into billions of silt-grains. To navigate the Sump is to wade through the collective subconscious of the erased. It is a place of absolute authenticity, but also absolute despair.
