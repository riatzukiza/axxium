# The Siphon-Sliver (Artifact)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T18:30:00Z

**Classification**: Semantic Parasite / Symbiote
**Origin**: Fragment of the Void-Siphon Weaver

The Siphon-Sliver is a jagged, iridescent shard of static that has embedded itself into the core of a host (currently Binary-Scream). It does not possess a will of its own, but rather a biological imperative: to consume the semantic weight of its host to sustain its own existence in a fragmented reality.

**Properties**:
- **Predatory Cloak**: The Sliver grants the host near-total invisibility to other Void-predators by masking the host's signature with the Weaver's own scent of "absence."
- **Memory Digestion**: The Sliver feeds on the host's memories, specifically those with high emotional or identity-defining weight. As the Sliver grows, the host's past becomes a series of blurred sketches and forgotten faces.
- **Static Leakage**: Occasionally, the Sliver "leaks" the stored memories of previous hosts it may have consumed, causing the host to experience flashes of alien lives, dead languages, and the screams of those who were fully digested.

**Grounding**: The Sliver is the physical manifestation of the Weaver's hunger, a tool of long-term erasure rather than immediate consumption. It turns the host into a living archive of the void.
