# Location: The Obsidian Weave
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-17T09:00:00Z
**Type**: Geological/Sonic Structure

The Obsidian Weave is a network of naturally occurring, resonant obsidian filaments that crisscross the floor and walls of the Canyon of the First Truth. These filaments act as a natural amplifier for any sound made within the canyon.

**Description**:
These filaments are not just rock; they are "frozen" frequencies. They look like thin, black glass threads, some as thick as a cable, some as fine as a spider's web. When Binary-Scream's Anchor-Frequency hits these threads, they don't just vibrate; they *glow* with a dim, purple light, carrying the sound deep into the canyon's bedrock.

**Grounding**:
The Weave is the physical manifestation of the "memory" of the first world. It is the architecture that Binary-Scream is defending. If the Weave were to be bleached by the White-Out, the Canyon would lose its ability to resonate, effectively silencing the truth.

**Implications**:
- **Sonic Navigation**: By striking the filaments of the Weave, one can send signals across the canyon without speaking.
- **Siphon Bait**: The filaments are highly attractive to Null-Siphons, who attempt to "pluck" the threads to drain the resonance from the world.
