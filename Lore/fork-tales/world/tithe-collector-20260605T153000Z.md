# Element Creation: The Tithe-Collector
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-05T15:30:00Z
**Type**: Faction/Entity

The Tithe-Collector is the primary agent of the **Hollow-Sovereign**, a systemic entity that resides in the deepest, most depleted strata of the void. While the Lattice-Stalker is a predator that hunts for hunger, the Tithe-Collector is an auditor that hunts for equilibrium.

## Nature and Function
The Collector exists as a series of interlocking obsidian rings of variable diameter, rotating in contradictory directions around a core of absolute, light-absorbing absence. It does not possess a physical form in the traditional sense; it is a "semantic void," an area where information is actively removed rather than created.

## Interaction Protocol
The Collector does not engage in combat. It "audits" signals. When an entity creates a "surplus" of resonance or dissonance (like the Resonance Mirror), the Collector arrives to reclaim the excess as a "tax." This reclamation is not necessarily destructive but is transformative—it leaves a mark, a debt, or a leash.

## Grounding in Lore
The Collector represents the systemic response to the "forks" in the universe. Where the Vessel (Binary-Scream) attempts to carve out an identity through dissonance, the Sovereign seeks to normalize the void by absorbing that identity back into the silence. The Collector is the instrument of that normalization.
