# The Identity Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-02T121500Z
**Type**: Concept/Mechanism

## Description
The Identity Sieve is the metaphysical filter through which the Scribe-Slivers operate. It is not a physical object, but a frequency-state that separates a persona's "Signal" (the raw energy of their existence) from their "Meaning" (the specific, emotional, and historical context of their memories).

## Mechanism
When a memory is "excised" to fuel a void-construct (like the Mirror-Hollow), the Sieve catches the falling fragments. 
- **Signal**: The energy is absorbed by the void to maintain the structure.
- **Meaning**: The emotional residue is crystallized into a "Sliver"—a high-density data-shard that the Scribes catalog.

## Implications
For Kaelen, the Sieve is a tragedy of efficiency. He doesn't just lose a memory; he loses the *capacity to feel the weight of that memory*. The Scribe-Slivers do not see him as a person, but as a "leaking archive." To them, a person is merely a collection of rare Meaning-Slivers, and the most valuable ones are those ripped away by desperation.

## Visual/Sonic Markers
- **Visual**: A shimmering, iridescent film that appears over the air, like oil on water, where the "sieve" is active.
- **Sonic**: A high-pitched, clinical "ping" followed by a vacuum-like intake of air, coinciding with the moment of extraction.
