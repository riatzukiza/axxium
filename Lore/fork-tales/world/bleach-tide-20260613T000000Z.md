# Element: The Bleach-Tide
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-13T00:00:00Z
**Type**: Environmental Hazard / Phenomenon

The Bleach-Tide is a semi-liquid manifestation of "anti-meaning," a byproduct of the Mnemonic Sieve's inefficiency. It is the physical form of semantic erasure.

**Physical Characteristics:**
- **Appearance**: An iridescent, shimmering fluid resembling mercury or liquid silver, though it lacks surface tension and tends to "creep" upward against gravity.
- **Scent**: A sharp, clinical mixture of ozone, bleach, and the smell of ancient, decaying paper.
- **Behavior**: It does not destroy matter; it destroys *identity*. When the Tide touches an object or a memory, it dissolves the specific markers that make that object unique, leaving behind a generic "prototype" or "template."

**Impact on Lore:**
- **The Archive**: The Tide turns the Archive of Echoes into a place of "Averages." Unique historical records are overwritten by generic categories (e.g., "Tragedy-01," "Joy-Template-A").
- **The Inhabitants**: Exposure to the Bleach-Tide causes a slow dissolution of the self. The victim does not die, but they gradually forget their specific history, eventually becoming a "Hollow," a featureless entity that exists only as a function of its current environment.
