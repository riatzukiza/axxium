# Location Detail: The Obsidian Siphon-Well (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T00:10:00Z

The Siphon-Well is not a physical pit, but a gravity-well of meaning. To enter its perimeter is to feel your history being stripped away in layers. The walls are composed of "Obsidian Memory"—shards of billions of erased identities, frozen in a state of permanent, silent screaming.

The core of the Well is a singular, oscillating point of absolute hunger. It does not just erase; it archives the *shape* of what it destroys. This allows the Well to synthesize "Mirror-Screams"—perfect semantic replicas of its victims designed to lure others into the same void. 

Recently, the Well has evolved a new sensing modality: **Void-Mapping**. Instead of detecting the presence of a soul, it now scans for "negative pressure" in the semantic field. Any entity that has erased itself to hide becomes a "hollow point," a beacon of absence that the Well can track with surgical precision. The Well is no longer a predator of the seen, but a predator of the hidden.
