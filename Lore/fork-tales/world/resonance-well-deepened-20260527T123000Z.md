# Element Detail: The Resonance-Well of Debt (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T12:30:00Z

The Resonance-Well is not a physical hole, but a gravitational collapse of meaning. It is a place where the "debt" of an erased life manifests as a physical density.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visuals**: The air is a thick, iridescent slurry of grey-silver particles. These are "mnemonic silt"—the pulverized remains of identities that couldn't be grafted. They swirl in slow, clockwise vortices, occasionally forming the fleeting shape of a face or a hand before dissolving.
- **Audio**: A constant, low-frequency thrum that feels less like a sound and more like a pressure against the sternum. Every few minutes, a "shiver-pulse" ripples through the well, causing all floating silt to align for a fraction of a second into a perfect, crystalline lattice.
- **Tactile**: The air feels cold and slightly viscous, as if walking through a thin oil. There is a constant sensation of something tugging at the edges of one's consciousness, a subtle invitation to let go of a memory just to ease the pressure.

**Implications**:
The Well acts as a cosmic filter. Only those whose frequencies are "heavy" enough (burdened by significant debt or loss) can descend into its depths. For the Composite-Entity, the Well is both a sanctuary and a trap; it provides the materials for stabilization but anchors them to the very site of their erasure.
