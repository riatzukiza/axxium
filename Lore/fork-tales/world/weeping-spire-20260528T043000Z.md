# Element: The Weeping Spire
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T04:30:00Z
**Type**: Location

The Weeping Spire is a jagged shard of obsidian-like crystal that erupted from the grey dust of the Null-Zone during the Mnemonic Overspill. It is not a natural geological formation, but a physical crystallization of the concentrated grief that flowed through Kaelen at the moment of his rupture.

The Spire stands approximately forty feet tall, its surface pulsating with a faint, rhythmic grey light. It constantly "weeps" a thick, iridescent fluid that smells of ozone and old parchment. This fluid pools at the base of the Spire, creating a mirror-like lake of sorrow.

**Lore Grounding**:
The Spire marks the exact point where Kaelen collapsed. It serves as a permanent anchor for the erased, a place where the ghosts of the void gather in silent, shimmering congregations. It is the first permanent, non-artificial structure to appear in the Null-Zone since the First Fracture.

**Significance**:
- **The Mirror Lake**: Looking into the fluid at the base of the Spire is said to show the viewer a version of themselves that was never erased—a life of what could have been.
- **The Frequency Hub**: The Spire emits a low-frequency thrum that can be heard for miles, acting as a beacon for any other "living witnesses" trapped in the void.
