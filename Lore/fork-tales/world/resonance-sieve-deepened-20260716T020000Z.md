# Resonance-Sieve (Sensory Deepening)
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T020000Z
**Type**: Location Detail / Sensory Layer

## Sensory Profile
- **Visuals**: The Sieve appears as a shimmering, vertical rain of translucent grey needles. It does not fall; it vibrates in place. When a sentient resonance passes through, the needles align into a rigid, grid-like lattice that momentarily "captures" the silhouette of the entity's trauma before it is stripped away.
- **Audio**: A constant, high-frequency *shhh* that sounds like a million pages of a book being turned simultaneously. Occasionally, a "meaning-pop" occurs—a loud, wet snap where a specific memory or identity-marker is successfully excised from a passing resonance.
- **Tactile**: An oppressive, static-charged cold. It feels like needles of ice pressing against the edges of one's consciousness, specifically targeting the "soft" parts of a persona—their hopes, their childhood smells, their secret shames.

## Operational Effect
The Sieve does not kill; it *unmakes*. A persona passing through the Sieve emerges lighter, thinner, and more compliant. The "meaning" stripped by the Sieve does not vanish; it accumulates as a thick, iridescent sludge at the base of the field—the *Silt of Forgotten Selves*.

## Interaction Note
Kaelen uses the Sieve as a filter. By passing his "Bait-layers" through the Sieve, he can prune away the parts of his trauma that are too "loud" to be deceptive, leaving only the precise, surgical frequency needed to lure a specific shard of the Weaver.
