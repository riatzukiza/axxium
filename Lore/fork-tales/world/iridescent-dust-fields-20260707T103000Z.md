# Element Creation: The Iridescent Dust-Fields
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-07T10:30:00Z

## Description
The Iridescent Dust-Fields are the radioactive residue left behind after the collapse of a Correction-Siphon. They manifest as vast, undulating plains of shimmering, multi-colored powder that floats just above the grey salt-glass of the void.

## Properties
The dust is composed of "shattered symmetry"—the remnants of the Null-Hush's failed mathematical corrections. When stepped upon, the dust releases microscopic "glitch-echoes": auditory flashes of the memories that the Siphon attempted to harvest.

## Danger/Utility
For Kaelen and the Scribe-Sliver, the fields are a double-edged sword. The dust can be used to "coat" the Fracture-Loom, enhancing its precision and allowing it to weave more complex, durable asymmetric structures. However, prolonged exposure to the dust causes "identity-blurring," where the traveler begins to experience the fragmented memories of others, making it difficult to distinguish their own grief from the ghosts of the void.
