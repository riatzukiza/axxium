# Element: The Null-Zone (Location)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T18:00:00Z
**Type**: Location / Environmental Anomaly

The Null-Zone is the radius of devastation surrounding the former Suture-Scar. It is a region of "semi-existence" where the obsidian geometry of the Obsidian Vault has been reduced to a fine, gravity-defying grey ash.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: A landscape of floating ash-dunes and shimmering heat-haze, despite the lack of heat. The sky is a flat, matte grey that absorbs all light.
- **Auditory**: The "Static-Lullaby"—a constant, low-frequency hum that sounds like a thousand distant voices trying to speak at once, but never quite forming words.
- **Tactile**: The air feels thick, like walking through cold water, and carries a metallic taste of ozone and old ink.

**Laws of the Null-Zone**:
- **Emotional Gravity**: Physical movement is governed by emotional state. Those consumed by grief move slowly, as if wading through lead; those driven by desperate hope can leap distances that should be physically impossible.
- **Memory-Echoes**: Brief, translucent images of the Vault's former inhabitants manifest spontaneously, repeating the last three seconds of their most traumatic memory before dissolving into ash.

**Grounding**:
The Null-Zone is the physical manifestation of the "Suture's Hemorrhage." It represents the moment where the Scribes' attempt at erasure failed so spectacularly that it erased the very concept of "structure" in the local area.
