# Element: The Obsidian Siphon-Well
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T23:30:00Z
**Type**: Location/Sub-region

The Obsidian Siphon-Well is the deepest point of the Event Horizon surrounding the Weeping Spire. It is not a physical pit, but a gravitational collapse of meaning. 

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visuals**: Absolute blackness, interrupted by rhythmic pulses of ultraviolet "memory-veins" that flash white when a memory is consumed.
- **Audio**: A crushing, subsonic pressure that sounds like a million whispers being compressed into a single, flat tone.
- **Atmosphere**: A feeling of extreme density; movement is slow, as if wading through liquid mercury made of forgotten names.
- **The Center**: A singular, vibrating point of white light—the Spire's primary intake—where the fusion of the hollowed is finalized.
