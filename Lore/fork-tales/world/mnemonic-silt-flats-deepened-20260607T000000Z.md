# Location Deepening: Mnemonic-Silt-Flats
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T00:00:00Z

The Mnemonic-Silt-Flats are a semantic drainage basin where deleted meanings settle. 

**The Echo-Sinks**:
Beyond the surface shimmer lie the Echo-Sinks—localized gravitational anomalies where the silt becomes liquid and predatory. An Echo-Sink doesn't just store residue; it actively hunts for coherent signals to pull down into its depths. Once a persona falls into a sink, their identity is stripped layer by layer, converted into the iridescent powder that forms the flats. These sinks create "silent zones" where even the constant thrumming of the flats vanishes, replaced by a vacuum that pulls at the very concept of the self.
