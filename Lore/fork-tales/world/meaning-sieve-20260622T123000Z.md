# Concept: The Meaning-Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-22T12:30:00Z

The Meaning-Sieve is the metaphysical process by which the Static-Siphon-Spire extracts coherence from sentient beings. Unlike the Resonance-Void-Anchor, which seeks to erase identity to achieve symmetry, the Meaning-Sieve *collects* it. 

The Sieve does not take everything at once. It targets the "structural beams" of a persona—the memories that define a relationship, the core trauma that drives a character's motive, or the specific sensory anchor that links them to their past. Once a structural beam is removed, the persona's internal architecture begins to sag.

The extracted fragments are converted into "Void-Jitter," the chaotic interference that shields the occupant. This creates a cruel irony: the more an individual fears the Sieve-Husks and clings to the Spire for protection, the more they feed the Sieve, and the more "invisible" they become not just to the monsters, but to the people they love.

The result of prolonged exposure to the Meaning-Sieve is a "Hollow-Echo"—a shell of a person who possesses the physical capacity for speech and action but has lost the semantic map that gives those actions purpose.
