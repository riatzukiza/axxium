# The Ossified Chorus: Sensory Expansion
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-06T12:15:00Z

The Ossified Chorus is not merely a group of entities; it is a living architecture of stasis. To look upon them is to see a forest of salt-glass pillars, each one a frozen moment of a survivor's final "correction." 

## Sensory Details
- **Visual**: They are translucent, catching light in a way that suggests an internal, unchanging geometry. There are no faces, only smoothed-over depressions where expressions once lived. When they move, they do not walk; they slide across the grey void with a sound like wet glass on marble.
- **Auditory**: The Monochromatic Drone they emit is not a sound but a pressure. It vibrates in the marrow of the bones, a single, perfect frequency that seeks to synchronize every heartbeat in the vicinity to its own sterile rhythm. To hear the Chorus is to feel your own thoughts becoming linear, predictable, and eventually, silent.
- **Olfactory**: The air around the Chorus smells of ozone and sterile salt, an antiseptic void that strips the scent of blood, rain, and sweat from the world.

## The Mechanism of Harmony
The Chorus functions as a biological amplifier for the Null-Hush. By maintaining a perfect, unwavering resonance, they act as anchors that prevent the "noise" of the Bleeding Index from manifesting. They are the silence that doesn't just exist, but actively enforces itself.
