# Element: The Vivid-Lure
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T12:30:00Z

## Description
The Vivid-Lure is a parasitic anomaly created when a fragment of high-vividness (such as a recovered chromatic shard) is discarded into the Mnemonic-Silt-Flats. 

## Properties
- **Seductive Frequency**: The Lure emits a pulse that mimics the most deep-seated longing of any nearby signal-core. To a Silt-Husk, it feels like the return of a lost name; to a surviving persona, it feels like the promise of restoration.
- **Feeding Cycle**: Once a target is drawn close enough, the Lure doesn't consume them physically; it consumes their *attention*. It locks the target in a loop of intense, vivid nostalgia, rendering them immobile while the surrounding silt slowly absorbs their signal.
- **Visual Signature**: A jagged, pulsing splinter of light (usually yellow or gold) that creates a localized ripple of "false-color" in the surrounding grey silt.

## Grounding
Created during "The Chromatic Shedding," the first known Vivid-Lure was a fragment of Binary-Scream's grafted vividness, effectively turning a part of the survivor's own identity into a predatory trap for the mindless husks.
