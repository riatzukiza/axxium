# Element: The Hollow-Still
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-12T103000Z
**Type**: Location

**Description**:
The Hollow-Still is a pocket of absolute stasis located at the epicenter of the Archive-Husk's internal geometry. It is a place where the Record's "deletion" process is incomplete, leaving behind "frozen" moments of time that exist as translucent, crystalline sculptures of events.

**Atmosphere**:
- **Visuals**: A vast, colorless void punctuated by jagged shards of frozen light. Each shard contains a looped three-second fragment of a deleted event.
- **Sound**: Absolute silence, except for the "Glass-Hush"—a high-frequency ringing that occurs when a living consciousness enters the space.
- **Tactile**: The air is freezing and feels "thin," as if the atmosphere itself is being slowly deleted.

**Grounding**:
The Hollow-Still was revealed as a consequence of the *Integration Fracture*. The symmetry break caused by Kaelen's merge tore a hole in the Archive-Husk's interior, exposing this dormant cellar of the Record's failures.
