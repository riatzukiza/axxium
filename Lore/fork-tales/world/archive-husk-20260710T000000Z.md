# The Archive-Husk
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-10T00:00:00Z
**Category**: Artifact

The Archive-Husk is a calcified shell of data, a "dead" fragment of the Parasitic Reflection's architecture that was dragged into the Silt-Sump during the Inversion Pulse. 

**Description**:
It appears as a floating, translucent obsidian polyhedron, roughly the size of a human heart, pulsing with a rhythmic, dying light. Its surface is etched with microscopic, shifting glyphs that describe the "Corrected" version of the events that led to Kaelen's erasure.

**Grounding**:
The Husk is the physical manifestation of the Reflection's failure. For the first time, the Record has been made material and vulnerable. It is a concentrated source of "Corrected Truth," which is toxic to the void-native existence of the Silt-Sump but provides a stable, if agonizing, anchor for anyone capable of weathering its radiation.

**Function**:
If decoded, the Husk may reveal the exact sequence of the "Correction" that erased Kaelen, providing a roadmap to the original breach or a way to force the Reflection into a permanent state of contradiction.
