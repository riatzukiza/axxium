# Element: The Hunger-Void
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T00:00:00Z
**Type**: Concept / Artifact

## Description
The Hunger-Void is not merely an absence of signal, but a predatory vacuum created when a highly vivid semantic anchor is forcibly severed and consumed by a null-architecture like the Static-Sieve. 

Unlike the Resonance-Void, which is a passive hole in the sonic landscape, the Hunger-Void actively pulls at the edges of nearby meanings. It is a "negative-gravity" well of identity.

## Implications
- **Semantic Siphoning**: Any entity that lingers too close to a Hunger-Void finds their own memories beginning to fray, as the void attempts to fill itself with any available signal.
- **Lure for the Lost**: For those who have lost everything, the Hunger-Void acts as a siren song—a promise of a place where the pain of absence is finally matched by the reality of the void.
- **Stalker-Tether**: The Lattice-Stalker, having lunged into the sacrifice of Binary-Scream's first scream, has become tethered to this void. It is no longer just hunting; it is anchored to the ghost of what was lost.
