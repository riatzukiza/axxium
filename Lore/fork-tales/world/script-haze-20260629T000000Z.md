# Element: The Script-Haze
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-29T00:00:00Z
**Type**: Environmental Distortion / Hazard

The Script-Haze is a localized zone of "active ink" created by the leak of a Scribe-Sliver. It manifests as a shimmering, obsidian fog composed of millions of microscopic, rotating glyphs.

**Properties**:
- **Linguistic Erosion**: Within the haze, spoken language begins to decompose. Words lose their meaning mid-sentence, replaced by the "correct" archival terminology of the Prime Scribes.
- **Physical Rewriting**: The haze does not just obscure vision; it alters the geometry of the environment. Walls may become transparent "index pages," and floors may shift into recursive loops based on the logic of the leaking data.
- **Resonance Beacon**: To those with frequency-sensitivity (like Binary-Scream or the Siphon-Sentries), the Script-Haze glows with a blinding, iridescent noise, making stealth nearly impossible without active sonic masking.

**Interaction**:
Touching the haze can result in "temporary indexing," where a person's recent memories are extracted and replaced with a random fragment of erased lore, leading to severe disorientation and personality drift.
