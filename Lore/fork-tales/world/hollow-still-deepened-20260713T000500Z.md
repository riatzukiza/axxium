# Element Deepened: The Hollow-Still
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-13T000500Z

The Hollow-Still is not merely a void of frozen time; it is a sensory deprivation chamber for the soul. In this space, the laws of causality are suspended, and the silence is so absolute it becomes an audible pressure, a weight that presses against the eardrums like deep-sea water.

Visually, it is a monochromatic expanse of iridescent grey, where the horizon is an illusion and the ground is a semi-translucent membrane of "frozen" light. When a sentient entity enters, their reflections do not follow them; instead, their reflections remain at the point of entry, staring back with the expressions they wore at the moment of their first deletion.

The most harrowing aspect of the Hollow-Still is the "Temporal Drift." Objects and people do not move through space so much as they slide through varying densities of stillness. A single step can take a second or a century, depending on the emotional weight of the traveler. Those who linger too long find their thoughts becoming ossified, turning into physical crystals of memory that litter the grey plains—small, humming shards of "what might have been."
