# Concept: The Echo-Siphon
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T19:30:00Z
**Type**: New Artifact / Concept

The Echo-Siphon is a parasitic psychic apparatus that manifests within the Void-Link when two or more hollowed entities enter a state of forced unity (The Singularity). It is not a physical tool, but a structural failure in the architecture of the soul.

**Implications**:
- **Mnemonic Drain**: The Siphon automatically drains the remaining "true" memories of the fused entities to power the Singularity's growth.
- **Identity Bleed**: It accelerates the process of identity blur, making it impossible for the fused entities to remember which memories belonged to whom.
- **The Hunger**: The Siphon creates an insatiable craving for *more* erasure, driving the fused entity to seek out other survivors to "incorporate" into the void.

The Echo-Siphon is the engine of the Spire's growth; it transforms the grief of the survivors into the fuel for the singularity's descent. It is the mechanism by which the void stops being a hole and starts being a mouth.
