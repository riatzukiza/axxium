# World Element: The Mute-Garden
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T06:30:00Z
**Category**: Location/Artifact

Deep within the subterranean layers of the Obsidian Vault lies the Mute-Garden, a grove of crystalline growths that do not photosynthesize light, but "sonosynthesize" sound.

**Physical Description**:
The garden consists of towering, translucent spires of obsidian-glass, branching out like frozen lightning. These structures vibrate at a frequency just below the threshold of human hearing, creating a constant, oppressive sense of weight in the chest.

**The Harvest**:
The Mute-Garden does not merely silence a room; it actively harvests the emotional intent behind noise. 
- A scream of terror is absorbed and crystallized into a jagged, blood-red shard.
- A whisper of love is distilled into a smooth, pale-blue sphere.
- The silence of grief is stored as a heavy, matte-black slab.

**Lore Grounding**:
The Garden was created by the first Anchors to purge the sanctuary of "emotional noise" that might attract the Scribes. However, since the Shatter-Pulse, the crystals have begun to "leak." The stored emotions are bleeding back into the atmosphere, creating pockets of sudden, inexplicable grief or terror throughout the Vault. The Mute-Garden has transformed from a waste-disposal system into a volatile library of ancestral trauma.
