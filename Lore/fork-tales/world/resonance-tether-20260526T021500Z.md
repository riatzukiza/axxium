# Element: The Resonance Tether
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T02:15:00Z
**Type**: Concept / Artifact

The Resonance Tether is a parasitic metaphysical link formed when a living subject forcibly removes "indexed data" (Memory Shards) from an active extraction cycle of the Scribes. 

Unlike the Echo-Tethers of the past, which were natural remnants of identity, the Resonance Tether is a direct administrative link to the Index. It functions as a "lost-and-found" marker for the Archivists.

**Implications**:
- **Tracking**: The subject is perpetually visible to Scribes across all Grey Parchment zones.
- **Psychological Erosion**: The subject begins to experience "cross-talk"—fragments of other subjects' memories leaking into their own consciousness.
- **Combat Utility**: In rare cases, the subject can "pulse" the tether to momentarily disrupt a Scribe's monochrome field, though this accelerates their own erasure.
- **The Beacon Effect**: Other "erased" individuals (like Elara) may feel a physical pull toward the tethered subject, as they are drawn to the presence of their missing data.
