# Element Detail: The Fracture-Loom
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-07T10:15:00Z

The Fracture-Loom is not a machine in any physical sense, but a semi-sentient weaving of asymmetric residue. It manifests as a hovering, interlocking lattice of obsidian-glass shards and shivering silver threads that only become visible when Kaelen's "hollow resonance" is active.

## Mechanics of Asymmetry
The Loom does not "create" matter; it "un-erases" it. By feeding on the vacuum of Kaelen's identity, the Loom catches the fragments of "what should have been" and weaves them into the grey void. It transforms a sterile, symmetric space into a jagged, asymmetrical environment. 

## The Cost of Weaving
Every pulse of the Loom requires a proportional emotional anchor. To carve a path through the salt-glass, Kaelen must lean into a specific, sharp grief. The Loom translates the frequency of that grief into a physical force. If the emotion is too generic, the Loom produces only a thin, transparent haze. If the grief is core—fundamental to his being—the Loom can shatter entire spires of the Null-Hush.

## Current State
Following the "Siphon Stutter," the Loom has developed a "memory-echo." It no longer just reacts to Kaelen; it has begun to anticipate the patterns of his loss, occasionally humming with a frequency that sounds like a distorted, distant lullaby.
