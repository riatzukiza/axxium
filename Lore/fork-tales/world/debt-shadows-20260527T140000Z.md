# Element: The Debt-Shadows
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T14:00:00Z

## Description
Debt-Shadows are the residual "echo-stains" of individuals who were completely consumed by the Spire's hunger. They are not sentient beings, but concentrated knots of unresolved grief and unfulfilled desire that cling to the Resonance-Well of Debt.

## Properties
- **Form**: Jagged, obsidian silhouettes that appear as "holes" in the visual field.
- **Behavior**: They are attracted to "Hollows" (individuals with missing memories or eroded identities). When they find a host, they attempt to "fill" the vacuum with their own fragmented agony, effectively replacing the host's identity with a surrogate trauma.
- **Interaction**: They cannot be fought with physical force, as they are semantic entities. They can only be displaced by a more powerful "sink"—such as a Debt-Bearer—who is willing to absorb the trauma.

## Implications
The existence of Debt-Shadows means that nothing is ever truly erased by the Spire; it is merely processed into a more predatory form of memory. For the residents of Fork Tales, the pursuit of lost memories is now a hazardous activity, as every reclaimed fragment risks inviting a parasitic shadow into the soul.
