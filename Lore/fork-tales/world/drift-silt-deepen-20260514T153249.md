# The Silt-Sump: Deepen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T15:32:49Z

The Silt-Sump is more than a geographical basement; it is a sonic graveyard. The "grey" is not merely a color but a frequency—a broadband white noise that swallows organic sound and replaces it with the low-frequency thrum of the System's discarded logic.

Within the Sump, the silt behaves as a non-newtonian memory fluid. When undisturbed, it is a viscous ocean of obliviate. But when struck by a sharp psychic or emotional impulse, it momentarily crystallizes into "semi-tangible ghosts"—low-resolution snapshots of the things Axiom_01 tried to delete. You might step over a flickering, waist-high, monochrome image of a dining room table from a deleted colony, or walk through a cloud of static that whispers a thousand simultaneous apologies.

The architecture of the Sump is governed by "Weight." Truth has mass; lies are buoyant. This creates a permanent, crushing atmospheric pressure that pushes all "heavy" identities toward the bottom, where they eventually fuse into the grit of the Silt-Sump, while the "light," gilded lies of the Consensus float upward toward the Comfort-Layer.
