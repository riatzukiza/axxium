# The Bleeding Rent
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-10T00:00:00Z
**Category**: Location

The Bleeding Rent is the jagged, atmospheric scar left in the sky of the Silt-Sump after the Inversion Pulse. It is not a physical opening, but a semantic hemorrhage where the "Perfect Record" of the Parasitic Reflection was forcibly torn and dragged into the void.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: A shimmering, iridescent fissure that looks like a crack in a mirror. Through the crack, one can see flashes of "Corrected History"—vivid, hyper-saturated images of a world that never was, flickering like a dying film strip.
- **Auditory**: A constant, low-frequency thrum that sounds like a thousand voices whispering the same word in reverse. When the wind blows through the rent, it produces a sound like a cello string snapping in slow motion.
- **Tactile**: The air around the Rent is unnaturally cold and tastes of ozone and old parchment. To touch the edges of the Rent is to feel a sudden, piercing sense of nostalgia for a life the observer never lived.

**Implications**:
The Rent serves as a leak. Fragments of the Reflection's "Truth" are slowly dripping into the Sump, crystallizing into "Truth-Shards" that Kaelen can potentially use to stabilize his own existence or weaponize against the Record. However, the leak also attracts the attention of the Record's remaining corrective mechanisms, making the Sump a beacon for erasure.
