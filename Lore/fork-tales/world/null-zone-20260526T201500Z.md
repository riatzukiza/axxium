# World Element: The Null-Zone (Expanded)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T20:15:00Z
**Type**: Location / Phenomenon

The Null-Zone is not merely a place of absence, but a region of "Emotional Gravity." Here, the laws of physics have been replaced by the laws of resonance and grief.

**Sensory Details**:
- **The Grey Ash**: The atmosphere is saturated with fine, weightless ash—the remains of un-existed obsidian and forgotten memories. The ash does not settle; it drifts in slow, concentric circles around points of high emotional intensity.
- **Emotional Gravity**: In the Null-Zone, sorrow has physical mass. A person experiencing profound grief will find themselves physically pulled toward the center of the zone, as if the void were a planetary body. Conversely, joy or hope acts as a buoyant force, pushing the individual toward the periphery.
- **The Static-Lullaby**: A constant, low-frequency background hum consisting of millions of overlapping, whispered voices. It is the sound of the "Erased" attempting to synchronize their fragments.
- **Visual Distortion**: Light does not travel in straight lines; it curves around "pockets of trauma," creating shimmering, lens-like distortions in the air that reveal fleeting glimpses of the Vault as it existed before the First Suture.

**Invariants**:
- Structure is impossible without a stabilizing frequency.
- Silence is not the absence of sound, but the presence of absolute synchronization.
