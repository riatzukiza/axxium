# Artifact: The Shiver-Glass Fragment
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-24T10:00:00Z

The Shiver-Glass Fragment is a physical manifestation of a "truth-frequency"—a rare piece of the Spire's architecture that has detached due to a resonant collision between a lie (the Graft-Echo) and a genuine emotion.

**Properties**:
- **Frequency**: It vibrates at a frequency that bypasses the cognitive filters of Graft-Echoes. It does not provide a full narrative, but rather an "emotional snapshot" of a moment of absolute truth.
- **Effect**: When absorbed into a persona, the fragment creates a "Silver Scar." This scar acts as a permanent anchor for doubt, preventing the Graft-Echo from ever fully dominating the persona's perception again.
- **Origin**: It is a piece of the "Loom of Sanity" that has been shattered by the paradoxical pressure of a selfless act (Kaelen's donation) existing within a system designed for transactional debt.
