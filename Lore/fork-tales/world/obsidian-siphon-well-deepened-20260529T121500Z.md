# Element: The Obsidian Siphon-Well (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T12:15:00Z
**Type**: Location / Conceptual Entity

The Obsidian Siphon-Well is not a physical hole in the ground, but a semantic collapse. It is the place where meanings go to die, and where the "forks" of discarded timelines pool like oil.

## Sensory Details
- **Visuals**: The space is an inverted cathedral of matte-black glass that absorbs all light except for the "bleeding" edges of memories. When a memory is siphoned, it appears as a thin, iridescent filament of light being pulled toward a central, non-existent point.
- **Audio**: A constant, low-frequency thrum—the "Siphon's Pulse"—that vibrates not in the ears, but in the teeth and the marrow. Occasionally, this is interrupted by "Ghost-Notes": snippets of conversations from timelines that were erased, playing in reverse.
- **Tactile**: The air feels thick and viscous, like walking through cold honey. There is a persistent sensation of "pulling" on the skin, as if the environment is trying to peel away the observer's surface-level identity.
- **Scent**: The smell of ozone and old parchment, overlaid with the metallic tang of blood that doesn't belong to anyone currently present.

## Conceptual Implications
The Well does not just destroy; it archives. Every erased memory, every severed fork, and every sacrificed identity is stored in the "Sub-Strata of the Unremembered." The Siphon-Well is the curator of this void, and its predatory behavior is not hunger, but a drive for completeness. It seeks the "Prime Anchor"—the original, un-forked version of a soul—to finally close the loop of its own existence.
