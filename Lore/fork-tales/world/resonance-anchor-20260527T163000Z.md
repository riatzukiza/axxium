# Element: The Resonance-Anchor (Artifact)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T16:30:00Z

The Resonance-Anchor is a makeshift device constructed by Kaelen from the discarded fragments of the Spire's own architecture. It consists of a vibrating shard of obsidian-glass and a series of copper-wire sutures that pulse with a rhythmic, low-frequency hum.

The Anchor is designed to "lock" a specific frequency in place, preventing the Meaning-Sieve from eroding a chosen memory. By feeding a small amount of their own coherence into the device, the user can create a localized "static-shell" around a single concept.

However, the Anchor is parasitic. It does not generate coherence; it merely preserves it by stealing it from elsewhere. To keep a memory of "home" intact, the user must sacrifice a memory of "hunger" or "cold." It is a zero-sum game of identity preservation.

If the Anchor is over-driven, it risks creating a "Resonance Spike"—a flare of high-density meaning that can attract Silt-Husks, effectively turning a protective tool into a predatory lure.
