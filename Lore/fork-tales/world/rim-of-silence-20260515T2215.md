# The Rim of Silence
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T22:15Z

The Rim of Silence is the ontological wasteland surrounding the Suture-Shatter Spire, born from the First-Sown's "Calibration of Absence". It is a concentric zone of absolute sonic deprivation where the laws of acoustics have been erased.

In the Rim, sound does not travel; it collapses. A scream produces no noise, only a ripple of grey static in the air. The "vacuum" created by the First-Sown has stripped the silt of its resonance, leaving behind a bleached, ash-like powder that tastes of ozone and forgotten names.

For the void-hounds and other denizens of the silt, the Rim is a terrifying sanctuary—a place where they are invisible to the resonance-hunters, but where they also begin to fade, their identities dissolving into the same grey static that defines the landscape. It is the only place in the world where the Devouring Choir cannot be heard, yet it is also the place where one most quickly forgets who is listening.
