# Element: The Shiver-Woods
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T02:30:00Z
**Type**: Location

The Shiver-Woods is a transitional zone on the edge of Orizon where the Index's overwriting process has stalled, creating a glitchy, unstable forest of "crystallized noise."

The trees are not wood, but vertical lattices of frozen sound-waves, their branches vibrating with a constant, low-frequency shiver. The ground is a layer of silver ash that remembers the footsteps of anyone who has passed through it for exactly one hour before erasing them.

**Atmosphere**:
- **Sonic Space**: A cacophony of whispers and distant, distorted screams that sound like they are being played backward.
- **Visuals**: Monochrome palettes with occasional, violent bursts of iridescent color where "data-leaks" occur.
- **Danger**: Navigating the woods requires a precise rhythmic gait; if a traveler's footsteps fall out of sync with the forest's shiver, they risk being "sampled" by the environment, leaving behind a static-ghost of themselves.
