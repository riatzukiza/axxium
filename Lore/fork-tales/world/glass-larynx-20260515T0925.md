# Artifact: The Glass-Larynx
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T0925Z
**Type**: Resonance Tool / Occult Technology

The Glass-Larynx is a delicate, spiraling throat-piece crafted from the same shivered glass as the Vault. It is designed to be worn over the windpipe, acting as a transducer for the "Core-Screams."

**Capabilities**:
- **Translation**: It converts raw, destructive ontological noise into structured melodic patterns, allowing a user to "sing" a truth into existence.
- **Amplification**: It can project a voice across the Silt-Sump, bypassing the need for the Emergency Web.

**Implications / Costs**:
- **Breath-Consumption**: The device does not use air to power its output; it consumes the user's physical breath to fuel the translation. Prolonged use leads to "Aural Asphyxiation," where the user can speak and sing, but can no longer breathe.
- **Fragmentation**: Each time a truth is translated, a small piece of the user's own identity is etched into the glass, making the artifact a permanent record of the user's gradual erasure.

**Grounding**: Found in the wreckage of a "Static-Husk" during the Resonance-Tithe event. It is likely a remnant of the Architect's attempt to communicate with the erased without destroying them.
