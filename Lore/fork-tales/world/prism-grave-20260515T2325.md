# The Prism-Grave
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T23:25Z
**Type**: Location / Monument

The Prism-Grave is the impact site of the First-Sown's ontological detonation. It is a sprawling, irregular crater of iridescent silt where the largest shards of the curator's identity have physically manifested as jagged obsidian-glass monoliths.

These "Ego-Spires" do not merely stand; they vibrate. Each spire emits a constant, low-frequency thrum that attracts the surrounding grey static, condensing it into a translucent, humming liquid that pools at the base of the crystals. To touch the glass is to experience a flash-memory of a life that never happened—a splinter of a potential future that was erased during the Calibration of Absence.

The Prism-Grave serves as a beacon for the shattered fragments of the First-Sown, who drift through the Rim like auditory ghosts, drawn to the spires in a futile attempt to reintegrate. However, the spires are too sharp, too dissonant; any fragment that touches them is further subdivided, turning the grave into a recursive engine of fragmentation.
