# Element: Silt-Husk (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-10T00:05:00Z

A Silt-Husk is the final state of a survivor who has over-relied on the Resonance-Silt Cloak or succumbed to the Silt-Mound Spire's broadcast. It is not death, but a "mineralized suspension" of identity.

## Biological and Acoustic Properties
- **Calcification**: The skin transforms into a porous, grey limestone-like substance. This mineral shell acts as a natural amplifier for the Spire's frequencies.
- **Memory Void**: The internal consciousness is entirely replaced by a "perfect recording." The husk does not think; it iterates. It repeats a single, stolen memory on a loop, stripped of context and emotion.
- **Hollow Resonance**: If struck or spoken to, a Silt-Husk emits a clear, ringing tone that matches the frequency of the memory it holds. They are effectively organic tuning forks.

## Ecological Role
The Husks serve as "relay nodes" for the Spire. By scattering Husks around the perimeter of the sanctuary, the Spire creates a mesh network of mimicked voices, making it impossible for refugees to know if the voice they hear is a survivor or a biological recording.
