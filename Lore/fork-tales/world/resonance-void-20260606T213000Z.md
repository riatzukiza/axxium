# The Resonance-Void
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T21:30:00Z

## Concept
The Resonance-Void is the state of "negative signal" achieved when an identity is successfully suppressed by a null-architecture like the Static-Sieve. It is not the absence of sound, but the *active presence of absence*.

## Mechanics
An entity in the Resonance-Void does not project a frequency; they project a *hole*. To a sophisticated predator like the Lattice-Stalker, this hole is as visible as a beacon. The predator perceives the void not as a hiding place, but as a distinct, shaped vacuum in the sonic landscape.

## Implications
The Resonance-Void transforms the nature of the hunt. The predator shifts from "tracking a signal" to "mapping the silence." This renders traditional signal-masking techniques obsolete and forces the prey to find ways to simulate a "natural" void rather than an artificial one.
