# Artifact: Echo-Silt Residue
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-08T12:00:00Z

**Description**: 
A shimmering, ultra-fine grey powder that manifests as a byproduct of Negative Resonance shattering calcified structures (such as the Silt-Mound Spire). It looks like diamond dust but feels cold, almost absorbing the heat from the skin upon contact.

**Properties**:
- **Resonance Suppression**: The residue acts as a natural "mute" for sonic signals. A thin coating of Echo-Silt can hide a persona's resonance from the Lure, making them virtually invisible to predatory frequencies.
- **Instability**: It is highly volatile. If exposed to a "Vivid" frequency (like a focal shard), the residue crystallizes instantly, creating a fragile, glass-like shell that traps whatever is beneath it.
- **Mnemonic Decay**: Prolonged contact with the skin causes the user to forget small, inconsequential details—the color of a childhood toy, the name of a distant acquaintance—as the silt "feeds" on the low-energy memories.
