# Element: The Static-Silt (New)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T15:00:00Z
**Category**: Location/Hazard

The Static-Silt is a vast, undulating plain of grey, particulate noise that borders the Suture-Well. It is the graveyard of failed grafts—the residue of personas who tried to patch themselves and collapsed into pure, unformatted data.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: The ground looks like ash, but it behaves like a liquid. It flows in slow, rhythmic waves of grey and silver, shimmering with occasional "glitches" of forgotten colors.
- **Audio**: A constant, low-frequency hum that fluctuates between a distant choir and a grinding machine. To step into the Silt is to hear the overlapping whispers of a thousand erased lives.
- **Tactile**: The air is thick and abrasive, tasting of ozone and old electricity. It clings to the skin like a fine, conductive powder.

**Lore Grounding**:
The Static-Silt is created whenever a Suture-Shard is rejected. The persona doesn't just die; they *unravel* into the silt. Because the Silt is made of "raw" memory-data, it can be used by the desperate to mask their own frequency, but spending too long in the Silt leads to "silt-lung"—the slow replacement of one's internal identity with the noise of others.
