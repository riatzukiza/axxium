# Element: The Collage-Core
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T12:30:00Z

## Description
The Collage-Core is the precarious psychic architecture resulting from the grafting of foreign semantic shards into a Null-Signature void. Rather than a cohesive identity, it is a shimmering, unstable mosaic of borrowed memories, emotions, and sensory fragments.

## Implications
- **Psychic Instability**: The "owner" of the Collage-Core experiences sudden, violent shifts in personality and emotion as different shards activate.
- **Semantic Camouflage**: Because the identity is a patchwork of multiple sources, it is harder for the Siphon-Well to target a single "signature," though the graft process itself creates intermittent visibility pulses.
- **Borrowed Grief**: The core does not just store data; it preserves the emotional residue of the original memories. The user carries the weight of a thousand strangers' losses.

## Grounding
Emerges from the desperate attempt of Binary-Scream to fill the "Hollow-Point" wound using the remnants of the Siphon-Well's incomplete digestions.
