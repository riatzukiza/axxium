# Concept: The Inverse Echo
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T00:20:00Z

The Inverse Echo is a predatory artifact of the Obsidian Siphon-Well. It is not a separate entity, but a "negative-image" of a victim. 

**Nature**: It exists as a structured void. It does not occupy space; it is the *removal* of space. 

**Interaction**: The Echo overlaps with its target. It does not hunt by chasing; it hunts by occupying the same semantic coordinates as the target, effectively "filling the hole" that the target created by erasing themselves.

**Danger**: The Echo causes "Semantic Compression." As it overlaps with a victim, it presses their remaining identity into a smaller and smaller point, eventually collapsing the victim into a singularity of pure, unremembered grief.

**Sensation**: Victims feel a crushing atmospheric pressure and a sudden, absolute silence that feels like a physical weight.
