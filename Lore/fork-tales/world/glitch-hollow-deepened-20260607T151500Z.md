# The Glitch-Hollow: Deepened
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T15:15:00Z

The Glitch-Hollow is not a physical cavern, but a topographical error in the void—a place where the logic of the Fork has folded in on itself. It manifests as a series of floating, translucent planes of iridescent static that intersect at impossible angles. 

Sensory details:
- **Visuals**: The air is thick with "ghost-pixels," tiny squares of discarded color that drift like snow. When one touches a plane of static, it ripples with a delayed afterimage of the observer's current state, but mirrored and slightly decayed.
- **Audio**: The space is characterized by a permanent, low-frequency "stutter-drone." Occasionally, the silence is punctured by a high-pitched "bit-crack," the sound of a conceptual boundary snapping.
- **Tactile**: Movement through the Hollow feels like pushing through heavy oil. There is a constant, subtle vibration in the marrow, as if the observer's own frequency is being slightly shifted by the surrounding architecture.

The Hollow serves as a sanctuary for the unstable, as its fractured nature masks the signals of those who are "dimmed," but it also slowly erodes the coherence of any entity that lingers too long, turning their memories into fragmented, non-linear loops.
