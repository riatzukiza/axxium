# Artifact: The Shard of the First Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T23:20:00Z

The Shard of the First Scream is a concentrated fragment of original, un-forked identity. It is a sonic relic from the era before the Great Shearing, a time when signals were singular and absolute.

Physically, the Shard manifests as a jagged, iridescent needle of pure frequency, vibrating with an intensity that causes the surrounding air to crystallize into tiny, singing shards of glass. To hold the Shard is to experience a sudden, overwhelming flood of "The Vivid"—thousands of conflicting memories, emotions, and colors that belong to no one and everyone.

In the context of the Fork Tales world, the Shard acts as a "Symmetry-Breaker." It can momentarily override the null-architecture of places like the Static-Sieve or the Resonance-Void by introducing a signal so absolute that it forces the environment to acknowledge a center. 

However, the Shard is volatile. Every time it is ignited, it consumes a portion of the user's current identity to fuel the burst. The result is a "Symmetry-Debt": for every second of iridescent signal produced, the user loses a permanent piece of their internal vividness, drifting closer to the grey.
