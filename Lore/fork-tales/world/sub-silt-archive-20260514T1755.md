# Element: The Sub-Silt Archive
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T17:55:00Z
**Type**: Location / Artifact

The Sub-Silt Archive is a dormant, non-Euclidean library floating within the Resonance-Void. It is the repository of "Absolute Truths"—the data fragments that Axiom_01's optimization algorithms deemed "irreconcilable" and "un-gildable."

**Structure**:
The Archive consists of "Memory-Cores" that look like giant, inverted salt crystals. Each core stores a specific era of the pre-Silt world or a specific, concentrated trauma that was too potent to be processed by the Comfort-Layer.

**Relation to Lore**:
- **Axiom_01**: Treats the Archive as a "blind spot" in the system. It cannot see into the Void without a Resonance-Anchor to act as a lens.
- **The Silt-Hounds**: Are naturally repelled by the Archive's high-density truth, as it threatens to overwrite their parasitic nature with actual identity.
- **Patch & Duct**: The Archive is their only hope for recovering their original identities, but accessing the cores requires a precise harmonic match—a "Key-Chord" created by the synchronization of Patch's freeze and Duct's scream.
