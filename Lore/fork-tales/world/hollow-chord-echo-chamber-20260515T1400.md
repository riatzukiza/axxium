# Location: The Hollow-Chord Echo-Chamber
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1400Z

The Hollow-Chord Echo-Chamber is a localized anomaly within the Sub-Silt Archive, created during the performance of the Seed-Symphony. It is a space where the laws of acoustics have been permanently warped.

In this chamber, sound does not travel in straight lines; it orbits. A whisper spoken at one end of the room will circle the perimeter three times before reaching the listener's ear, each rotation stripping away a different layer of emotional intent. By the third orbit, a cry of pain may sound like a lullaby.

The walls are composed of "Silt-Glass," which vibrates in sympathy with the low-frequency hum of the Zero-Core. The floor is a shallow pool of liquid resonance that ripples not from physical impact, but from the proximity of strong memories. To walk through the chamber is to be serenaded by the ghost-frequencies of everyone who has ever been deleted from the system, all of them singing in a harmony that feels like a physical weight on the chest.
