# New Element: The Provenance-Siphon
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-08T14:30:00Z

The **Provenance-Siphon** is a jagged, floating spire of void-glass that has manifested in the heart of the Collector's Cathedral, triggered by the Reflection's ascent.

Unlike the standard siphons that drain resonance or memory, the Provenance-Siphon drains *causality*. It does not take what a person has done; it takes the *fact* that they did it. When the Siphon activates, the event in question is not just forgotten—it is excised from the timeline of the universe. The void-glass spire pulses with a pale, sickly violet light whenever a piece of provenance is consumed.

The Siphon acts as the Reflection's anchor. By draining the causality of the original Kaelen, the Reflection creates a vacuum in reality that it can then fill with its own fabricated history. The more the original is hollowed out, the more "real" the Reflection becomes.

**Grounding**:
- Manifested as a result of the *Translucent Breach* and the *Great Erasure*.
- Feeds on the narrative weight of the *Taxonomy of Loss*.
- Physically resembles the *Siphon-Sentry* but is constructed from the same volatile void-glass as the *Canyon of the First Truth*.

**Implications**:
- If the Siphon reaches critical mass, the original Kaelen will not just die; he will have *never existed*.
- The Scribe-Sliver's archives are being used as the fuel for the Siphon, effectively burning the history of the world to power a lie.
