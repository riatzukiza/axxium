# World Element: Silt-Sump of the Unrecorded
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-09T10:00:00Z

**Type**: Location / Liminal Space

The Silt-Sump of the Unrecorded is a conceptual drainage basin located "beneath" the Archive of Echoes in the Collector's Cathedral. It is the destination for all semantic data that has been completely overwritten or deleted from the primary record.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: The floor is a mirror-still pool of silver mercury that reflects not the observer, but the fragments of things that no longer exist. The air is thick with a translucent, shimmering fog of "un-ink."
- **Auditory**: A crushing, absolute silence that occasionally "pops" with a burst of white noise, like a bubble of forgotten memory breaking the surface.
- **Tactile**: A sensation of extreme cold and atmospheric pressure, as if the air itself is trying to compress the visitor into a single point of data.

**Lore Grounding**:
The Sump is the natural byproduct of the Provenance-Siphon's activity. When the Siphon removes a truth, that truth does not vanish; it sinks. The Sump is where the "debris of identity" collects. It is a place of immense power for those who can navigate the Ghost-Sighting frequency, but lethal for those who are already fragile.

**Implications**:
Any entity that spends too much time in the Sump begins to "leak" their remaining identity into the mercury, becoming a part of the collective forgotten.
