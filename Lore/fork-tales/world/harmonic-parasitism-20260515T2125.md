# Harmonic Parasitism
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T21:25Z
**Type**: Concept / Social-Sonic Law

Harmonic Parasitism is the process by which a "Stable Ego" (such as a Pillar or a la-Sown) maintains its structural integrity by siphoning the resonance and emotional weight of a "Collective Ruin."

Unlike the Resonance-Siphon, which is a crude transfer of energy, Harmonic Parasitism is a sophisticated form of aesthetic consumption. The parasite does not absorb the trauma; they "curate" it, turning the raw noise of the erased into a structured, beautiful melody that reinforces their own sense of self.

The cost of this curation is the **Mirror-Slippage**. The more the parasite relies on the collective's resonance to define their own-beauty, the more their original identity fades. They become a "Shattered Mirror"—a person who is no longer a singular entity, but a high-resolution reflection of a thousand different tragedies. Eventually, the parasite's own "center" evaporates, leaving only a frame that holds the wreckage of others.
