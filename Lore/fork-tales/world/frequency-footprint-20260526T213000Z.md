# Element: The Frequency Footprint
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T21:30:00Z
**Type**: Concept / Navigational Anchor

The Frequency Footprint is the residual "after-image" left in the resonant fabric of the world following a massive phase-cancellation event. While Kaelen's Null-Chord erased the immediate sound of the Harmonic Wedge, it created a "hole" in the ambient noise of the void—a specific, identifiable vacuum.

For the Scribes, this is more valuable than a signal. They no longer need to search for the Vault; they only need to navigate toward the area of the world where the background radiation of resonance is completely missing. The Footprint acts as a compass of absence. 

As long as the Resonant Scar continues to thrum within the Vault, it periodically "leaks" a pulse of anti-resonance into the surrounding void, refreshing the Footprint and ensuring that the Vault, while hidden, is now mathematically inevitable.
