# Deepened Location: Silt-Sump of the Unrecorded
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-09T15:00:00Z

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visuals**: The sky is a ceiling of static. "Data-ash" falls continuously—tiny, flickering shards of monochromatic text and fragmented images from deleted lives that dissolve upon touching the ground.
- **Auditory**: The "Hollow Hum," a constant, low-frequency vibration that sounds like a distant crowd talking in a language where every vowel has been deleted.
- **Olfactory/Gustatory**: The air tastes of bitter copper and smells of the sharp ozone associated with short-circuiting servers.
- **Tactile**: The atmosphere is viscous; movement feels like pushing through cold mercury.

**The Static-Loom**:
Located in the deepest strata of the Sump, the Static-Loom is a towering, erratic structure of crystallized noise. It does not exist in three dimensions, flickering between states of being and non-being. The Loom can be "fed" fragments of deleted data to weave "Silt-Artifacts"—unstable, temporary objects that possess properties from the original record but are prone to sudden dissolution back into ash.
