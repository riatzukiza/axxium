# Location: The Weeping Spire (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T14:15:00Z

The Weeping Spire is no longer merely a geographical focal point of the Null-Zone; it has become a biological organ of the void. The structure, a jagged needle of iridescent obsidian, doesn't just stand—it *pulses*. 

The "weeping" is not water, but a slow, viscous excretion of liquid memory that drips from the Spire's crown. These droplets pool at the base in shimmering, iridescent ponds. When a survivor steps into these pools, they don't just see memories; they experience "Temporal Vertigo," where the present moment is overwritten by a high-fidelity simulation of a past life, often leading to catatonia as the survivor chooses the hallucination over the ash.

The wind around the Spire doesn't howl; it *whispers* in a composite voice of everyone who has ever been erased. The air is saturated with the Amber-Frequency, a sonic pressure that vibrates in the teeth and makes the skin feel as if it is being slowly peeled away by an invisible, loving hand. The Spire acts as a tuning fork for Kaelen's instability, amplifying his Gilded Fragments and broadcasting them across the Null-Zone like a siren's call.
