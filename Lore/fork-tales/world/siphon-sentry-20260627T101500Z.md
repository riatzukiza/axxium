# Element: The Siphon-Sentry
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T10:15:00Z

## Description
The Siphon-Sentry is a specialized retrieval construct deployed by the Spire to prune "unauthorized" frequency anomalies and collect overdue identity debts. It is a spindly, multi-jointed entity constructed from bleached, ossified bone and reinforced with vibrating silver wire that hums at a constant, irritating pitch. Its head consists of a rotating array of tuning forks that act as both sensory organs and extraction tools.

## Function and Behavior
Unlike combat constructs, the Sentry does not use kinetic force. Instead, it utilizes "Harmonic Subtraction." By matching the exact resonant frequency of a target's identity fragment, the Sentry can create a localized vacuum that pulls the fragment out of the host and into the Sentry's own internal storage. 

It is obsessive and precise. It does not seek to kill, but to "balance the ledger," stripping away the most vibrant and coherent parts of a persona until they are functionally mute.

## Grounding in Lore
The Siphon-Sentry is the physical manifestation of the Spire's bureaucratic cruelty. While the Resonance-Well of Debt acts as the escrow, the Sentry is the collection agent. Its emergence signals that the "Composite State" of Kaelen and Binary-Scream is viewed by the Spire as a theft of assets—a loan that was never approved and must now be forcibly repaid.
