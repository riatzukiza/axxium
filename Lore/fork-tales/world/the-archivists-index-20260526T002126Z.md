# The Archivist's Index (Concept)

The Archivists are the true form of the Pruners. While the survivors see them as gardeners removing weeds, the Archivists are cosmic librarians of the deleted. 

Every excised branch of reality is not truly gone; it is "compressed" into the Index. The Index is a non-spatial library where timelines are stored as high-density frequency-clusters. The Archivists maintain the Index to ensure that "inefficiency" (chaos, emotion, unplanned evolution) is fully isolated from the Root.

The danger of the Index is "Bleed." When a survivor like Kaelen manages to tune a Fork-Key to a deleted frequency, they create a temporary aperture into the Index. This "Bleed" allows the survivors to see the deleted, but it also allows the Archivists to track the "leaks" in their system.

A "Bleed-Event" creates a psychic scar on the world-state, making the surrounding Shard more susceptible to further pruning. To the Archivists, Kaelen is not a hero; he is a corrupt file that refuses to be deleted, and his attempts to save others only accelerate the deletion of the remaining world.
