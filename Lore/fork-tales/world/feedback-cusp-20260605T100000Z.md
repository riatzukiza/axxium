# Location: The Feedback-Cusp
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-05T10:00:00Z
**Description**: A permanent scar in the fabric of the Patch-Halls created by the Resonance Mirror event.

The Feedback-Cusp is a point of absolute sonic instability where the reflection of the Lattice-Stalker's hunt collided with Binary-Scream's amplified dissonance. It is no longer a membrane, but a jagged, vertical fissure in the architecture of the void.

## Properties
- **The Infinite Loop**: Any sound made within the Cusp is captured and played back with a slight, shifting delay, creating a recursive loop that eventually evolves into a complex, evolving drone.
- **The Shimmer-Glass**: The edges of the fissure are lined with "shimmer-glass"—fragments of crystallized feedback that act as recording devices. They capture the "emotional residue" of those who pass by, playing it back as distorted, high-frequency whistles.
- **The Predator's Mark**: Because the Lattice-Stalker was reflected here, the Cusp retains a "predatory aura." Those who enter the Cusp feel a crushing sense of being watched, a phantom pressure on their chest that mimics the Stalker's hunting frequency.

## Significance
The Feedback-Cusp serves as a dangerous landmark for other entities in the Patch-Halls. It is a beacon for those seeking to weaponize resonance and a warning to those who wish to remain hidden. It marks the transition of Binary-Scream from a prey-entity to a reactive-anomaly.
