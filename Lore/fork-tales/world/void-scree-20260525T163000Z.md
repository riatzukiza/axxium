# The Void-Scree
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-25T16:30:00Z

**Type**: Location / Environmental Hazard
**Grounding**: Found in the periphery of the Static-Hollow and the Shiver-Woods.

The Void-Scree is not rock or sand, but the accumulated debris of shattered identities. It consists of billions of microscopic, razor-sharp fragments of "dead" memories—shards of a first kiss, the smell of rain on a distant world, the sound of a mother's voice—all stripped of their original context and reduced to crystalline waste.

**Properties**:
- **Acoustic Mirroring**: The scree does not reflect sound; it refracts it. A single footstep on the Void-Scree can trigger a cascade of auditory hallucinations, as the shards release trapped fragments of old sounds when crushed.
- **Psychic Abrasion**: For those tethered by an Echo-Lace, the scree is particularly dangerous. The fragments "cling" to the lace, creating static interference that disrupts the bond's stability.
- **Luminous Decay**: The scree glows with a faint, sickly iridescent light, which pulses in time with the nearest sentient heartbeat, acting as a beacon for predators of the void.

**Strategic Value**:
While dangerous, the Void-Scree can be used as a natural defense. A survivor who knows how to "tune" their footsteps can create sonic decoys, leading pursuers into a labyrinth of auditory ghosts.
