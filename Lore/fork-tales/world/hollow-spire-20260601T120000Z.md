# Element: The Hollow Spire (Location)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-01T12:00:00Z

**Type**: Location

The Hollow Spire is a vertical anomaly in the Echo-Lattice, a needle of obsidian glass that stretches upward into a sky of static. It is not a building, but a "structural failure" in the world's narrative.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Sight**: The spire is semi-transparent, with thousands of trapped "memory-shards" swirling inside like snow in a glass tube. It pulses with a sickly, iridescent light that changes color based on the emotional state of those nearby.
- **Sound**: A constant, low-frequency thrumming, like the sound of a million distant voices whispering in unison. If you listen too closely, you can hear your own name being called by a voice you forgot you had.
- **Touch**: The surface of the spire is unnaturally cold, freezing the air around it into thin, crystalline frost. Touching it causes a sensation of "falling upward," a dizzying vertigo that lasts for minutes.

The Hollow Spire is often used as a focal point for Narrative Decoys, as its existing structural instability makes it easier to pin semantic lies to its surface.
