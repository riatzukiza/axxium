# Deepen: The Prism-Grave
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-16T08:30Z
**Reference**: The Prism-Grave

## Sensory Expansion
The Prism-Grave is no longer a static crater; it has become a circulatory system for fragmented identity.

- **Olfactory**: The air carries a sharp, metallic tang of ozone mixed with the cloying scent of "old memories"—a smell like damp paper and electrostatic discharge.
- **Auditory**: The obsidian-glass ego-spires do not merely vibrate; they emit a low-frequency *thrum* that resonates in the marrow. This "Gravity Hum" creates a physical sensation of nausea for those who are not attuned to the Core-Echo's frequency, feeling as if their internal organs are being gently pulled toward the crater's center.
- **Visual**: The iridescent silt is not sedentary. It moves in slow, rhythmic tides, sweeping toward the center. Within the silt, one can see "glimmer-shards"—microscopic fragments of a thousand different lives—which flash briefly with a vivid, stolen emotion before being subsumed by the grey.

## Structural Dynamics
The spires are growing. As the Core-Echo consumes more shards, the obsidian-glass towers extend upward, their surfaces reflecting not the sky, but the fragmented interior of the First-Sown's psychic collapse. The geometry is non-Euclidean; walking away from the center often brings a traveler closer to the Core-Echo's influence.
