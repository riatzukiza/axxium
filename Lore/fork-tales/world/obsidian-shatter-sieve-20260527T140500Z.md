# Element: The Obsidian-Shatter-Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T14:05:00Z
**Type**: Artifact / Concept

The Obsidian-Shatter-Sieve is not a physical object in the traditional sense, but a residual spatial anomaly created when the reflected coherence of a Debt-Bearer is violently rejected by the Resonance-Well of Debt. It manifests as a hovering cloud of razor-thin obsidian shards, each shard acting as a miniature, distorted lens.

## Sensory Details
- **Visual**: A swarm of black glass that doesn't reflect light, but instead "swallows" the colors around it, leaving a grayscale void in its wake.
- **Auditory**: A constant, high-pitched crystalline ringing that modulates based on the proximity of high-coherence beings.
- **Tactile**: A feeling of intense, dry cold that seems to draw the moisture and warmth directly out of the observer's breath.

## Implications
The Sieve acts as a passive filter for the Spire's influence. It can strip away the "noise" of a person's current identity to reveal the raw, distilled grief beneath—the very "fuel" the Spire seeks. Those who stand too close to the Sieve find their memories becoming transparent, as if their life is being rewritten as a series of gaps and omissions.

## Grounding
It is the direct consequence of Kaelen's failed attempt to return coherence to Binary-Scream. The shattered obsidian mirror didn't simply disappear; it expanded, becoming a permanent scar on the landscape of the Resonance-Well, transforming the site from a place of potential reclamation into a place of active filtration.
