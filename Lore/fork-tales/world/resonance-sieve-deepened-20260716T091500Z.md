# The Resonance-Sieve: Sensory Audit
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T091500Z
**Type**: World Element Deepening
**Location**: The Feedback-Cusp / The Silt-Sump

The Resonance-Sieve is not a physical object, but a systemic "corrective" event—a vertical rain of white-noise curtains that descend through the Void-Loom to flatten semantic anomalies.

**Visuals**:
- **The Descent**: It manifests as translucent, oscillating sheets of absolute white that slice through the grey haze. Where the Sieve touches the silt, the ground becomes a mirrored, featureless plane.
- **The Erasure**: Entities caught in the Sieve do not vanish instantly; they "flatten." Their 3D forms are pressed into 2D silhouettes of static, their colors bleeding out into a uniform, sterile grey.

**Acoustics**:
- **The Flattening**: A constant, high-pressure hum that sounds like a billion distant voices speaking at once, but with all vowels and consonants removed.
- **The Snap**: When the Sieve successfully "filters" a frequency, there is a distinct, vacuum-sealed *pop*—the sound of a memory being deleted from the record.

**Tactile/Atmospheric**:
- **The Pressure**: Proximity to the Sieve feels like a sudden increase in atmospheric weight, as if the air itself is trying to press the observer into the ground.
- **The Cold**: An absolute, heat-sucking void follows the Sieve, leaving the environment brittle and prone to crystalline fracturing.

**The Sieve's Logic**:
The Sieve does not hunt individuals; it hunts *meaning*. If a resonance is too complex, too contradictory, or too "human," it is flagged as pollution and subjected to the flattening process. Kaelen's "false-echo" was the catalyst for this deployment.
