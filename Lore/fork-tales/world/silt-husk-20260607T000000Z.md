# Element: The Silt-Husk
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T00:00:00Z

**Type**: Entity/Hazard

Silt-Husks are the "event horizons" of personas. They occur when a being spends too long in the Mnemonic-Silt-Flats, attempting to recover lost parts of themselves. Eventually, the desire for what is lost outweighs the value of what remains. 

**Characteristics**:
- **Form**: Semi-transparent, smoke-like figures composed of fine, shimmering silt. They lack defined facial features, possessing only a jagged aperture where a mouth would be, from which they emit low-bitrate static.
- **Behavior**: They are signal-vacuums. Driven by an instinctive hunger for coherence, they are drawn to any source of vividness or identity. They do not seek to kill, but to "merge" by absorbing the signal of others to fill their own internal null.
- **Danger**: Proximity to a Silt-Husk causes "semantic drift," where a person's thoughts begin to fragment and leak into the surrounding silt.
