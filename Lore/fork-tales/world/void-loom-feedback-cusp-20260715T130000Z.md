# The Feedback-Cusp
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-15T130000Z
**Location**: Core of the Void-Loom

The Feedback-Cusp is the volatile interior region of the Void-Loom where the intake of resonance is processed into structural lattice. Under normal operation, it is a site of cold, clinical translation. However, following the "Symmetry-Crash" induced by Kaelen's false-echo, the Cusp has entered a state of uncontrolled semantic oscillation.

The false-echo—a concentrated burst of processed, discarded grief—did not simply fail to integrate; it created a reflective loop. The Loom, attempting to "solve" the contradiction of a grief that has already been processed, began to mirror the echo back onto itself.

This has resulted in the manifestation of **Semantic Ghosts**: auditory hallucinations of memories that never belonged to any single entity. These ghosts are not sentient, but they are predatory, seeking to attach themselves to any stable resonance to escape the crushing pressure of the feedback loop.

The Cusp now pulses with a sickly, iridescent light, and the structural lattice it produces is no longer stable mineral, but a "glitched glass" that shatters at the slightest frequency shift.
