# Element: The Brine-Siphon Well
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T121500Z
**Type**: Location

**Description**:
A geothermal vent within the Salt-Spires where the mineralized grief of the deleted manifests as a thick, iridescent sludge. Unlike the crystalline spires, the Well is a place of fluid collapse, where the architecture of the Archive melts into a shimmering, salt-heavy brine.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: Swirling currents of iridescent white and deep obsidian; the air is heavy with a shimmering haze that distorts distance and shape.
- **Auditory**: A constant, low-frequency thrumming—the sound of millions of deleted memories being crushed into a single, resonant note.
- **Olfactory**: The sharp, metallic scent of ozone mixed with the cloying smell of old sea-salt and wet stone.
- **Tactile**: The air feels thick, almost liquid, clinging to the skin like a cold, wet shroud.

**Lore Grounding**:
The Well is the "drain" of the Salt-Spires. As the spires broadcast the resonance of the deleted, the psychic runoff collects here. It is the primary feeding ground for the Parasitic Reflection and a place of extreme vulnerability for any entity carrying an Identity Shard.
