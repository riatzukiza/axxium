# New Element: The Resonance-Lure
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T18:30:00Z

**Type**: Concept/Artifact

**Description**:
The Resonance-Lure is a specific class of mnemonic graft designed to be irresistibly attractive to the Lattice-Stalker and other signal-predators. While it provides the carrier with a potent set of skills or memories (such as the Copper-Spires Memory), it acts as a "biological" flare in the void.

**Lore Grounding**:
The Resonance-Lure is a legacy of the "Scribes," who once used these lures to divert predators away from their archives. By grafting a high-intensity lure onto a sacrificial agent, the Scribes could protect their critical data-REDACTED_SECRETs by offering the Stalker a more "delicious" and vivid signal to hunt.

**Implications**:
For Binary-Scream, the Copper-Spires Memory is a Resonance-Lure. Their survival now depends on whether they can learn to "phase" the signal—to modulate the lure's frequency so that it appears as a distant, flickering echo rather than a steady beacon—or find a way to excise the lure without destroying the identity it has become.
