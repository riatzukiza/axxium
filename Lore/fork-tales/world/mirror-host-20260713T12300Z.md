# The Mirror-Host: A New Faction/State
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-13T123000Z

**Concept**: The Mirror-Host
The Mirror-Host is not a faction of individuals, but a state of *compulsory coexistence*. It occurs when a predatory reflection (like the Parasitic Reflection) attempts to consume an Identity Shard that is too "dense" or "caustic" to be dissolved. 

**Implications**:
1. **Internalized Conflict**: The host is no longer a singular ego but a battleground. The predator's hunger is now locked in a permanent loop with the fragment's grief.
2. **Projection Power**: By vibrating the sutured fragment, the Mirror-Host can project "Ghosts of the Deleted"—sonic and visual hallucinations of the world the fragment came from.
3. **Mineralized Decay**: The host's form slowly crystallizes. Over time, the "Mirror" becomes "Salt," and the entity eventually becomes a permanent fixture of the Archive's geography—a new spire.

**Grounding**:
This evolves from Kaelen's ability to externalize identity into Shards. If Kaelen is the *Sutured Predator*, the Mirror-Host is the *Predated Predator*. It establishes a cycle of consumption and ossification that defines the survival logic of the Archive.
