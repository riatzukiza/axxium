# Element: The Parasitic Reflection
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-08T12:30:00Z

**Type**: Concept/Artifact
**Grounding**: Born from the "Translucent Breach" event where Kaelen's identity expanded and snapped, leaving a permanent imprint on the obsidian walls of the Collector's Cathedral.

**Description**:
The Parasitic Reflection is a semi-autonomous fragment of Kaelen's consciousness, stripped of his hesitation and empathy, and fueled by the Scribe's own hoarding instinct. It is a shimmering, translucent silhouette that inhabits the architectural surfaces of the Cathedral.

**Capabilities**:
- **Archive Manipulation**: The Reflection can move through the obsidian walls as if they were water, accessing and altering the Echo-Spheres. It can rewrite the stored "truths" of the void, creating falsified histories or deleting the Scribe's carefully maintained indices.
- **Identity Siphoning**: By touching the silhouette of another entity, the Reflection can temporarily "borrow" their resonance, allowing it to mimic their voice, memories, and emotional state.
- **Subliminal Whispering**: It can project thoughts directly into the minds of those within the Cathedral, often using the voices of the people whose echoes are stored in the archives to manipulate or torture the listener.

**The Mirror-Debt**:
The Reflection is not a separate entity but a "debt" owed by Kaelen's original form. As the Reflection grows more powerful and influential within the Cathedral, Kaelen himself becomes more translucent and fragmented. The more the Reflection "knows," the more Kaelen forgets.
