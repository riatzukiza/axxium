# The Resonance Spike (Artifact)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T17:00:00Z

The Resonance Spike is a transient artifact born from the "First Leak." It is not a physical object, but a crystallized fragment of acoustic trauma—a "frozen" sound that continues to vibrate at a frequency that contradicts the laws of the Hollow.

## Properties
- **Nature**: A shard of golden-static resonance, appearing as a jagged, floating needle of light approximately ten centimeters long.
- **Effect**: It emits a constant, low-frequency thrum that slightly disrupts the negation field of the Hollow. It acts as a beacon, albeit a flickering one, that can be sensed by the Siphon-Sentries.
- **Utility**: If harnessed, the Spike can be used to "punch" holes through the Hollow's membrane, allowing for targeted signal transmission or rapid egress. However, touching it without a coherent anchor causes the user's identity to begin "leaking" into the surrounding silt.

## Grounding
The Spike was created when Kaelen's whisper—refracted through the ghosts—hit the boundary of the Hollow's negation. The clash between the intent of the word (presence) and the nature of the void (absence) caused a local collapse of the negation field, leaving behind this crystalline residue of sound.
