# Element: The Static-Silt Ghost (Faction/Entity)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-30T00:00:00Z

## Description
The Static-Silt Ghosts are the semi-sentient residue of identities that were fully scrubbed by the Scribe-Sliver. They are not ghosts in the traditional sense, but "data-echoes" that have coalesced in areas of high archival pressure, such as the Hollow of the Hush.

## Nature
They manifest as shimmering, translucent figures composed of micronized ink and static. They communicate not through language, but through "harmonic interference"—bursting fragments of music, emotion, and memory that they attempt to graft onto any living consciousness that enters their domain.

## Relationship to the World
The Ghosts are terrified of the Siphon-Sentries, who view them as "noise" to be purged. They view Kaelen, an Indexed Outcast, as a potential bridge—a living entity with the "keys" (the indices) to potentially unlock their fragmented existence or lead them out of the void.
