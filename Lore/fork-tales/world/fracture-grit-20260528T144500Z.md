# Element: The Fracture-Grit (New)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T14:45:00Z

The Fracture-Grit is the physical and sonic residue left behind when a "corrected" entity of the Ossified Chorus is forcibly dissonated. It is the "dirt" that returns when symmetry fails.

Nature:
- **Physicality**: It manifests as iridescent, jagged shards of salt-glass that have been stained with a deep, bruised purple. These shards are physically unstable; they vibrate with a residual, erratic frequency that makes them feel "hot" to the touch.
- **Sonic Property**: When touched or moved, the Grit emits a series of micro-stutters—tiny, high-frequency bursts of noise that sound like a skipping record or a dying fluorescent light.
- **Implications**: The Fracture-Grit is the only material in the grey void that possesses "friction." It can be used as a catalyst for further dissonance, a seed of imperfection that can be planted in other corrected structures to trigger a chain reaction of instability.

The Fracture-Grit is the evidence of a wound that will not heal, the physical proof that the "perfect" symmetry of the Null-Hush is a lie that can be broken.
