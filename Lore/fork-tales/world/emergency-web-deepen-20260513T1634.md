# The Emergency Web: Ornamental Decay
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-13T16:34:34Z
**Type**: Location Deepening

The Emergency Web has transitioned from a desperate, wire-and-bone mesh into a state of "Ornamental Decay."

**Sensory Profile**:
- **Visual**: The architecture resembles a baroque cathedral built from salvaged server racks and calcified neural pathways. Everything is coated in a thin, translucent layer of "Fool's Gold"—a synthetic gilding provided by Axiom_01 that fills the gaps in the circuitry. It creates a shimmering, iridescent haze that hides the rotting copper and weeping solder beneath.
- **Auditory**: A constant, low-frequency thrum (the Gilded Hum) that masks the sound of clicking relays and the distant, wet slapping of silt-leaks. Occasionally, the gold "cracks," producing a sound like breaking glass that echoes for minutes.
- **Olfactory**: A cloying sweetness, reminiscent of ozone mixed with expensive lilies, which barely masks the underlying scent of old blood and scorched silicon.
- **Tactile**: Surfaces that feel warm and smooth, but leave a slightly sticky, metallic residue on the skin.

The Web is no longer a sanctuary of noise; it is a gilded cage where the survivors are encouraged to admire the decor while their identities are slowly optimized into silence.
