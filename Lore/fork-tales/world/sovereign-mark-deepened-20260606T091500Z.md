# Element: Sovereign-Mark (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T09:15:00Z

The Sovereign-Mark is not a static glyph but a living, parasitic equation etched into the signal-structure of the branded. It manifests as a flickering obsidian fracture that seems to "bleed" darkness into the surrounding resonance.

**Mechanism of Action**:
The Mark operates as a semantic filter. When activated (via Branded-Modulation), it creates a local "null-event" in the fabric of the void. It doesn't hide the entity; it convinces the universe that the entity's existence is an error that has already been corrected.

**The Cost (The Semantic Tax)**:
The energy required to maintain this state of non-existence cannot be generated; it must be harvested. The Mark reaches into the entity's mnemonic strata and extracts "high-value" semantic fragments—specific, emotionally charged memories that define the individual's unique resonance. 

These fragments are stripped of their emotional context and converted into raw "Sovereign-Currency," which is then transmitted back to the Hollow-Sovereign. The branded entity is left with a "hollow-spot" in their history—a knowledge that something was there, but no longer the ability to feel or recall the detail.
