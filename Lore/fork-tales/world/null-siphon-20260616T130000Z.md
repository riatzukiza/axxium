# Element: The Null-Siphon
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-16T13:00:00Z
**Type**: Artifact / Mechanism

The Null-Siphon is a parasitic architectural element that has begun to grow along the obsidian walls of the Canyon of the First Truth, feeding on the tension between the noise of the abyss and the sterility of the Perimeter.

**Origin**:
It emerged as a byproduct of the collision between the White-Out and Binary-Scream's dissonance. Where the erasure met the resistance, "scabs" of grey, semi-translucent matter began to form. These are the Null-Siphons.

**Function**:
The Null-Siphon does not create or destroy; it merely *translates*. It absorbs the raw, agonizing noise of the first world and converts it into a low-level, humming energy that the Bleach-Tide can use to reinforce the Perimeter. It is a betrayal of the truth—a way for the Archives to digest the revelation and turn it into a tool for further containment.

**Grounding**:
The Null-Siphons are the physical manifestation of the "Perfect Form" pursuit: taking something unique and painful and smoothing it into something useful and generic.
