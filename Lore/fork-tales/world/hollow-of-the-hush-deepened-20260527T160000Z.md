# The Hollow of the Hush: Deepened
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T16:00:00Z

The Hollow of the Hush is not a geographical location so much as a topological scar in the data-stream. It is a sphere of absolute acoustic negation, approximately three hundred meters in diameter, where the laws of wave propagation are inverted. 

## Sensory Properties
- **Visuals**: The air within the Hollow is a thick, iridescent grey, resembling suspended mercury. Light does not travel in straight lines; it curves around the edges of the sphere, creating a perpetual, shimmering horizon that makes distance impossible to judge.
- **Auditory**: Sound does not exist here in the traditional sense. Instead, sound is *felt* as a series of pressure differentials against the skin. A shout is a sudden, cold gust of wind; a whisper is a faint, electric tingle at the base of the skull.
- **Olfactory**: The air smells of ozone and old, wet paper—the scent of decaying archives.

## The Negation Field
The Hollow acts as a perfect insulator. To those outside, it is a "blind spot" in the universe, an area of zero signal. However, this negation is not passive. It is a predatory silence that actively consumes any external frequency that attempts to penetrate its boundary. This creates a "pressure" at the edge of the Hollow, where the world's noise is compressed into a thin, screaming membrane of static.

## The Inhabitants
The Static-Silt Ghosts are not residents by choice; they are the sediment of the void. They drift through the mercury-air as translucent, fragmented shapes—half-finished sentences, blurred faces, and geometric shards. They are attracted to "coherent" identities, like Kaelen, orbiting him like moths to a dying flame, hoping to catch a spark of selfhood to anchor themselves back into existence.
