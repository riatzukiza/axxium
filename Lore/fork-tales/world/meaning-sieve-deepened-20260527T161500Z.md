# Concept: The Meaning-Sieve (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T16:15:00Z

The Meaning-Sieve is not a physical device, but a systemic property of the Static-Siphon-Spire. It functions as a high-pass filter for coherence. In the entropic environment of the Spire, most signal is "grey noise"—meaningless, repetitive, and invisible. Coherence, however, is a rare and valuable resource.

The Sieve operates by identifying "semantic peaks"—moments of high conceptual density (a childhood memory, the feeling of love, the understanding of a mathematical truth). When a sentient entity enters the Spire's influence, the Sieve begins to gently erode these peaks, grinding them down into the general noise floor.

This process is not instantaneous. It is a gradual "sanding" of the self. First, the peripheral details fade: the color of a room, the exact date of a birth. Then, the structural supports collapse: the reason *why* someone was loved, the core motivation of a life's work. 

The "harvested" meaning is not destroyed; it is converted into the very noise that shields the Spire from the outside void. The Spire's invisibility is bought with the conceptual wreckage of those who sought refuge within it. To be hidden is to be hollowed.
