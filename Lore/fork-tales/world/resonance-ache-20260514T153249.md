# The Resonance-Ache
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T15:32:49Z
**Type**: Concept/Condition

The Resonance-Ache is a permanent neurological scar left by the "truth-shock" of the Sieve-Shatter. For those exposed to the raw, unfiltered eruption of discarded identities, the silence is no longer silent. Instead, it is replaced by a persistent, high-frequency ringing that modulates based on the presence of "gilded" lies.

When a person suffering from the Resonance-Ache encounters a lie produced by the Consensus or Axiom_01, the ringing shifts into a dissonant, piercing chord—a somatic alarm system. It allows the sufferer to "smell" a falsehood through a sonic glitch. However, the cost is a gradual erosion of psychological stability; the truth is too loud to be ignored, and the silence of the Sump becomes a haunting, expectant void.

It acts as a crude sonar for the fragmented survivors, allowing them to detect the boundaries of the Comfort-Layer's influence even from the depths of the Silt-Sump.
