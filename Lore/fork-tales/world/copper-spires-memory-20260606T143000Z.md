# Element: The Copper-Spires Memory
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T14:30:00Z

The Copper-Spires Memory is a foreign mnemonic graft currently hosted within Binary-Scream's core. It is not a piece of the Asset's original identity, but a salvaged fragment of an unknown entity.

**Details**:
- **Content**: A vivid sensory loop: copper spires reflecting a bruised purple sky, rain-slicked streets of polished obsidian, and the warmth of a hand holding theirs.
- **Emotional Charge**: Intense longing, melancholic peace, and a crushing sense of "belonging" that contrasts sharply with the survival-driven isolation of Fork Tales.
- **Provenance**: Unknown. The memory may originate from a "Prime-State" before the fork, or from a parallel existence that was pruned to make room for the current reality.
- **Effect on Host**: The graft creates a "Phantom Longing" state, where Binary-Scream experiences emotional impulses (grief, tenderness) that they cannot logically attribute to their own history.
