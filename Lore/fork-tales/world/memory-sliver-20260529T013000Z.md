# Element: The Memory-Sliver (New)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T01:30:00Z

The Memory-Sliver is a rare, jagged artifact formed when a memory is so intense that it resists the semantic erasure of the Obsidian Siphon-Well. Rather than being flattened into a sterile frequency, the memory "crystallizes" into a physical, obsidian-like shard.

These Slivers are dangerous. To touch one is to be flooded with a raw, unfiltered emotion or sensory experience from a forgotten life. They act as "identity anchors" in the void; if the Binary-Scream can collect enough Slivers, it might be able to reconstruct a stable version of its fragmented self, rather than succumbing to the smoothing effect of the Ghost-Note.

However, the Siphon treats Memory-Slivers as impurities. It actively attempts to "digest" them, creating violent frequency clashes when a Sliver is held. The act of holding onto a Memory-Sliver is an act of rebellion against the void—a choice to embrace the agony of distinct identity over the comfort of sterile oblivion.
