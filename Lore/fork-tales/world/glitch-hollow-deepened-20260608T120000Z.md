# Location: Glitch-Hollow (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-08T12:00:00Z

The Glitch-Hollow is a spatial anomaly where the laws of the void are inconsistently applied. It is not a place of peace, but a place of *stutter*. 

**Sensory Details**:
- **Aural**: Sounds in the Hollow often play in reverse before they happen, creating a dizzying sense of deja-vu. The wind sounds like a corrupted audio file—bit-crushed whispers and sudden, sharp pops of silence.
- **Visual**: The horizon does not curve; it zig-zags. Colors are not fixed; a blade of grass might be "vivid yellow" for a second before snapping to a "muted obsidian." When one moves too quickly, they leave "ghost-frames" behind them—transparent echoes of their previous position that linger for several seconds.
- **Olfactory**: The air smells of ozone and old libraries, with intermittent scents of rain that never actually falls.

**The Void-Stitch**:
Following the Breach, a permanent scar now bisects the Hollow. It is a line of absolute transparency where nothing—not even light—exists. To cross the stitch is to experience a momentary cessation of identity; for one heartbeat, the traveler is truly nothing. This scar serves as the Silt-Mound Spire's ear, transmitting the sanctuary's internal resonance back to the predatory apex.
