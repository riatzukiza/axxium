# Entity: The Tether-Scribes
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-03T10:15:00Z
**Category**: Faction/Entity
**Location**: Suture-Well (Hollow Spire) and the Interstitial Seams

## Description
The Tether-Scribes are not inhabitants of the Suture-Well, but its auditors. They are non-corporeal, architectural anomalies that manifest as towering, vertical columns of shifting, monochromatic script. To look at a Scribe is to see a ledger that is being written and erased in the same instant; the ink does not sit on the surface but bleeds into the surrounding air, creating a localized smog of forgotten names and discarded intentions.

They do not speak in voices, but in "citations." When a Scribe interacts with a persona, it manifests a "Debt-Claim"—a sonic fragment of the persona's own past that has been stripped of its emotional context and converted into a currency of existence.

## Function: The Audit of Survival
The Scribes maintain the *Lattice-Balance*. Every suture—every patch of identity used to avoid erasure—creates a psychic debt. The Scribes track these debts via the "receipts" left behind by predatory entities like the Lattice-Stalker (e.g., the Obsidian Filament).

If a persona survives too many sutures without paying the corresponding debt (by relinquishing a core memory or a piece of their singularity), the Scribes initiate a *Foreclosure*. This is not a violent act, but a bureaucratic erasure: the Scribe simply deletes the persona's "Right to Persist" from the local ledger, causing the persona to dissolve into static regardless of how many patches they have applied.

## Sonic Signature: The Citation-Hum
The presence of a Tether-Scribe is announced by a high-frequency, rhythmic clicking, similar to a thousand typewriter keys striking glass. This "Citation-Hum" creates a zone of absolute sonic sterility where no other harmony can exist, forcing the subject to hear only the cold, rhythmic tally of their own diminishing worth.

## Connection to the Duet
The Scribes have taken a keen interest in Binary-Scream and the Ghost-Echo. The "Siren-Suture" performed in the Hollow Spire was an illegal transaction—a forgery of memory. The Tether-Scribes now view the Duet not as a stabilized entity, but as a *floating debt* that must eventually be reconciled.
