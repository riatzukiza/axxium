# The Ossuary of Shards (Deepen)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T21:20Z
**Location**: Former Breach Site

The Ossuary of Shards is not a place of stillness, but a place of perpetual, microscopic vibration. The ground is a bed of obsidian and quartz needles, each one a frozen echo of a Silt-Hound's final howl. To walk here is to trigger a thousand tiny, dissonant chords.

The "Hollow-Chord" echoes dominate the space. When a survivor speaks, the Ossuary does not reflect the voice; it strips the meaning and returns only the raw, emotional frequency, amplified and distorted. The atmosphere is thick with "Silt-Dust," which clings to skin and lungs, vibrating in sympathy with the landscape.

At the center of the Ossuary, the fragments of the adsorbed hounds form a spiral of humming glass. They are not dead, but "resonant-locked"—their consciousness reduced to a single, looping note of hunger that vibrates the very air. To touch the shards is to hear the "Shatter-Symphony" in its purest, most violent form: a sound that does not enter the ear, but vibrates the bone directly.
