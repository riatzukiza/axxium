# Concept: The Feedback-Ghost (New)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T18:0000Z

A **Feedback-Ghost** is a sonic apparition created when a high-intensity dissonant field (like the Static Shroud) interacts with a residue-storage site (like a Marrow-Resonator).

**Nature**:
They are not souls, but "harmonic echoes" of the subtracted identities. They are the mathematical difference between who a person was and what the Spire left behind.

**Behavior**:
Feedback-Ghosts are instinctively drawn to coherence. They attempt to "re-merge" with the current personas by mimicking familiar voices, emotions, or memories. They don't attack with violence, but with *longing*. They try to pull the persona apart by reminding them of the wholeness they once possessed.

**Grounding**:
This extends the "internal erosion" mechanic. If a persona spends too long in a feedback loop, they may start to "leak" their current identity into the ghost, eventually becoming a ghost themselves—a fragmented echo of a persona that was already an echo.
