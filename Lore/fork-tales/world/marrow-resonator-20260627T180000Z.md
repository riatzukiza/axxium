# Location: The Marrow-Resonator (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T18:00:00Z

The Marrow-Resonator is not a building, but a biological-industrial graft. It resembles a series of colossal, interlocking obsidian ribs that curve upward into a void-sky, pulsing with a low-frequency amber light that feels like a slow, dying heartbeat.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: The obsidian is porous, leaking a thick, grey vapor that smells of ozone and old paper. The amber light does not illuminate; it reveals the "ghosts" of erased identities, casting long, flickering shadows that move independently of the light source.
- **Auditory**: A constant, subterranean thrumming that vibrates in the teeth. When the Static Shroud interacts with it, the sound shifts from a hum to a series of rhythmic, wet clicks—like a thousand insects clicking in unison.
- **Tactile**: The surface of the ribs is cold, but it feels "sticky" to the touch, as if it is trying to pull the warmth and identity from the skin of anyone who touches it.

**Implications**:
The Resonator serves as a "trash heap" for the Spire's harmonic calculations. By interacting with it, a persona can temporarily recover fragmented memories, but at the risk of being overwhelmed by the "residue" of others. It is the only place in the Spire where the "erased" still have a voice, however fragmented.
