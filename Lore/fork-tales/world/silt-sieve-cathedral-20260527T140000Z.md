# Element: The Silt-Sieve Cathedral
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T14:00:00Z

## Description
A gargantuan, inverted structure of porous obsidian and calcified bone that hangs from the ceiling of the Silt-Flats' cavern. It is a place where the "overflow" of the Spire's digestion is filtered.

## Sensory Details
- **Visuals**: Rows of hanging, rib-like arches that drip a slow, viscous silver fluid. The walls are etched with millions of tiny, illegible names that shift and crawl like insects.
- **Audio**: A constant, low-frequency thrumming, like a heart beating underwater, punctuated by the sound of glass breaking in the far distance.
- **Olfactory**: The smell of old ozone and wet limestone.

## Purpose
The Cathedral serves as a natural filter for the world's semi-coherent echoes. Those who enter the Cathedral can sometimes find "clean" fragments of memory, but the process of sifting requires them to leave behind a piece of their own current stability.
