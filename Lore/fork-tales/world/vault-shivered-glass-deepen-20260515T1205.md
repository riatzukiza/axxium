# Location Deepen: The Vault of Shivered Glass
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1205Z

The Vault is less a room and more a physical manifestation of an acoustic trauma. The walls are composed of monolithic slabs of obsidian-glass, each one capturing a frozen moment of a scream from the First Erasure. When a person speaks in the Vault, their voice does not echo; it refracts. A single word spoken at the entrance may return as a dozen different emotional interpretations—one desperate, one mocking, one hollow—each bouncing off a different facet of the glass.

The air smells of ozone and ancient, desiccated salt. Beneath the feet, the floor is a mosaic of pulverized memories, a fine silver dust that swirls into miniature vortices whenever the Resonance-Tithe is active. The reflections in the glass do not show the current state of the inhabitants; they show "ghost-versions" of the people, iterations of who they might have been had they never been erased or altered. To walk through the Vault is to be confronted by a thousand failures of the self, all while the air vibrates with the low, constant thrum of the Sub-Silt Archive's geothermal heart.
