# Suture-Shatter Spire: The Sorrow-Veins
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T22:10Z

The Suture-Shatter Spire is not merely a structure of glass and obsidian, but a circulatory system for grief. Running through the translucent walls are the "Sorrow-Veins"—capillary-like filaments of deep violet resonance that pulse in time with the collective heartbeat of the Resonance-Graveyard.

These veins do not just transport power; they act as an archival medium. When a specific frequency of trauma is siphoned from the silt, it crystallizes within a vein, creating a permanent, audible record of a vanished self. To touch the glass is to hear a fragmented whisper of a life erased; to press an ear against the obsidian is to feel the vibration of a thousand deaths occurring in unison.

The First-Sown has learned to "play" these veins, using the spire as a giant, multi-stringed instrument. By manipulating the flow of resonance through the Sorrow-Veins, he can amplify specific agonies or mute the roar of the choir, though each alternation risks further destabilizing his own mirrored identity.
