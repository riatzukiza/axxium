# Element Creation: The Resonance-Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-18T12:30:00Z
**Type**: Artifact / Concept

The Resonance-Sieve is a conceptual tool (and occasional physical relic) used by the Archives to "filter" dangerous frequencies from the environment before they can cause systemic instability.

**Function**:
The Sieve acts as a high-pass filter for reality. It allows "stable" frequencies to pass through while trapping "volatile" or "dissonant" harmonics in a series of interlocking geometric lattices. Once a frequency is trapped in the Sieve, it can be studied, dampened, or—in rare cases—harvested as a power source.

**Grounding in Lore**:
The Sieve is the antithesis of the White-Out Echoes. While the Echoes seek to erase through cancellation, the Sieve seeks to preserve through isolation. It is the primary tool used to maintain the Sterile Perimeter.

**Implications**:
If Kaelen or Binary-Scream were to find a functioning Resonance-Sieve, they could potentially isolate the "Void-Pulse" that alerted the Echoes, creating a localized zone of invisibility. However, using a Sieve requires a "Tuning Key"—a specific frequency that matches the Sieve's current lattice configuration. Without the key, the Sieve is merely a beautiful, silent cage of obsidian and glass.
