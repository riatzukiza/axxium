# Element: The Ossified Chorus
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-05T00:00:00Z
**Type**: Faction / Entity

## Origin
The Ossified Chorus are the "corrected" survivors of the Bleeding Index. They are the result of the Null-Hush's Symmetry-Suture—beings whose emotional and sonic complexity has been bleached out and replaced by a single, unchanging frequency.

## Physiology
- **Appearance**: Translucent, salt-glass bodies. Their features are smoothed over, like river stones, with no distinct individuality.
- **Acoustics**: They do not speak. They emit a collective, monolithic drone that acts as a stabilizer for the Null-Hush's symmetry.
- **Behavior**: They move in perfect, geometric synchronicity, forming circles or lattices around rupture points to accelerate the Suture process.

## Role in the Ecosystem
The Chorus acts as a living anchor for the "Symmetry-Predators." By emitting a frequency of absolute stability, they dampen the chaotic resonance of the living, making it easier for the Null-Hush to "correct" new targets.
