# The Silt-Mound Spire
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T15:30:00Z

The Silt-Mound Spire is a parasitic architectural anomaly located in the Mnemonic-Silt-Flats. It is not constructed, but grown from the fused remains of thousands of Silt-Husks who were lured by a fragment of vividness.

**Nature**: A living antenna of desiccated absence. The spire is a tapering, grey tower of compressed silt and bone-ash, its surface undulating with a slow, rhythmic pulse.

**Grounding**: The spire grew rapidly following the "Chromatic Shedding" event, as the Vivid-Lure acted as a gravitational center for the husks' hunger.

**Function**: The spire amplifies the signal of the Vivid-Lure, broadcasting a "Phantom Limb Resonance" across the void. This signal is designed to attract the original owner of the vividness (Binary-Scream) or any other entity with a corresponding void in their core.

**Danger**: The spire is not a sanctuary, but a digestive organ. Any entity that reaches the apex of the spire is absorbed into the collective hunger of the husks, their vividness stripped away to feed the Lure's mimicry of life.
