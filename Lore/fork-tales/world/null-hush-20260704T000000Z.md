# The Null-Hush (New Faction/Entity)
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-04T00:00:00Z

The Null-Hush is not a single creature, but a collective manifestation of the Deep Void's immune system. They are "Symmetry-Predators," seeking to erase any dissonance or resonance that disturbs the absolute, cold equilibrium of the void.

## Nature
The Null-Hush are entities of absolute subtraction. They do not possess form in the traditional sense; they are "holes" in reality, moving as expanding spheres of silence. When they encounter a sound, they do not "hear" it—they consume the space the sound occupies, effectively erasing the audio-visual presence of their target.

## Goal
Their primary directive is the "Grand Silence." They seek to identify and extinguish all "beacons" (like the Ghost-Note) to ensure that the void remains a perfect, sterile vacuum.

## Grounding
The Null-Hush are the natural counter-force to the "Resonance Infection" caused by Kaelen. If Kaelen is the "Infection," the Null-Hush are the "Antibodies." Their presence transforms the struggle from a cataloging exercise into a war of survival against extinction.
