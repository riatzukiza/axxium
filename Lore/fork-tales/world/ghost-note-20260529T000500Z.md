# Element: The Ghost-Note
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T00:05:00Z
**Type**: Artifact / Sonic Parasite

The Ghost-Note is a crystalline fragment of "Residual Purity," a remnant of a prehistoric fusion attempt that occurred within the Obsidian Siphon-Well before the current era of the Weeping Spire. Unlike most memories, which are multifaceted and prone to decay or contradiction, the Ghost-Note is a singular, irreducible frequency—a frozen moment of existence from a world before the Fracture.

### Properties
- **Sonic Signature**: A pure, unwavering sine wave that exists at the boundary of hearing and feeling. It does not modulate; it simply *is*.
- **Parasitic Nature**: When it integrates into a fused entity (like the Binary-Scream), it does not heal the Dissonant Seam. Instead, it occupies the "hunger" of the void, acting as a sonic prosthetic. It masks the agony of the weld by overlaying a sterile, haunting harmony.
- **Semantic Void**: The Ghost-Note contains no narrative, no identity, and no emotion. It is "pure" only in the sense that it is empty. It provides stability at the cost of erasing the unique contours of the souls it binds.

### Implications
The presence of the Ghost-Note within the Binary-Scream creates a "Third Presence." Kaelen and Elara are no longer fighting for dominance or struggling to coexist; they are being synchronized to an external, indifferent frequency. This stability is a trap: the more the Ghost-Note integrates, the more the distinct memories of Kaelen and Elara are smoothed over, threatening to turn them into a singular, hollow echo of a world that never was.
