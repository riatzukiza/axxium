# Location: The Bleeding Index
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-03T00:00:00Z

## Description
The Bleeding Index is not a physical place, but a catastrophic failure in the Scribe-Sliver's internal archive. It is a spatial rupture within the Scribe's clinical data-lattice, where a "living" memory—Kaelen's hope—detonated and created a permanent void of "un-categorizable" resonance.

## Sensory Details
- **Visuals**: The once-perfect, iridescent geometric grids of the Scribe's archive now appear "torn." Jagged edges of data-shards float in a sea of oppressive, ink-black static. At the center of the rupture is a pulsing, white-hot point of light that flickers like a dying star.
- **Audio**: A constant, low-frequency thrum—the "Ghost-Note"—that sounds like a distant, distorted choir singing a single, unresolved chord. It is the sound of a data-point trying to become a feeling.
- **Atmosphere**: An overwhelming sense of "wrongness." The clinical coldness of the Scribe's presence is now contaminated by an erratic, humid warmth, as if the archive is suddenly breathing.

## Implications
The Bleeding Index serves as a "leak" in the void. Because the Scribe-Sliver is connected to the deep archives, the Ghost-Note is now broadcasting into the void, potentially attracting other Scribes or things that feed on "unresolved resonance." It has transformed the Scribe from a collector into a beacon.
