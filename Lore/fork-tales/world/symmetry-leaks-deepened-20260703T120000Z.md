# World Element: The Symmetry Leaks (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-03T12:00:00Z

## Nature of the Fracture
The Symmetry Leaks are not mere physical cracks in the obsidian geometry of the Mirror-Hollow; they are "translation errors" in the local fabric of reality. When the recursive grid of the Hollow splintered during the Symmetry Break, it didn't just break the glass—it broke the logic of the space.

## Phenomenon: Synesthetic Bleed
Walking through a Symmetry Leak causes a momentary collapse of the boundary between the observer and the residues of others. This results in "synesthetic bleed," where sensory inputs are cross-wired with the discarded memories of those who passed through the area:
- **Olfactory Displacement**: An observer may suddenly smell ozone and old, damp parchment, only to have it replaced by the scent of a rain-drenched forest or a specific, forgotten perfume.
- **Tactile Echoes**: The feeling of a cold breeze may suddenly shift into the sensation of a phantom hand on a shoulder or the grit of salt on skin.
- **Visual Aberration**: Light bends around the leaks in a way that suggests the world is being viewed through a cracked, multifaceted lens, occasionally revealing "ghost-frames" of the world as it existed before the Siphon.

## Environmental Impact
The air surrounding a leak is perpetually charged with a static tension that makes hair stand on end and causes small, metallic objects to vibrate. These zones act as "Symmetry Sinks," drawing in nearby consciousness-residue and concentrating it into jagged, high-density pockets that are dangerously attractive to those suffering from "Memory Craters."
