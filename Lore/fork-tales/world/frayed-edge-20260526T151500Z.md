# Element: The Frayed-Edge (Location/Concept)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T15:15:00Z
**Grounding**: The Lace-Tear event.

## Description
The Frayed-Edge is not a place, but a condition of the Echo-Lace bond following the attack in the Static-Hollow. It is a metaphysical "scar" where the bond was snagged and torn.

## Sensory Details
- **Visual**: A jagged, sparking gap in the violet light of the lace, resembling a broken electrical wire. It periodically emits small, erratic bursts of golden light—remnants of the stolen memory.
- **Auditory**: A constant, low-frequency "crackle" or "stutter" that accompanies their shared resonance. It sounds like a skipping record or a radio signal losing its lock.
- **Tactile**: When Kaelen and Elara touch near the fray, they feel a sharp, electric shock and a momentary flash of a memory that doesn't belong to either of them—a "phantom limb" of the collective consciousness.

## Implications
The Frayed-Edge makes their resonance "noisy." While the Static-Hollow provided cover, the stuttering signal of the Frayed-Edge is now a beacon for Scribes who hunt by frequency. It is a vulnerability that turns their survival mechanism into a tracking device.
