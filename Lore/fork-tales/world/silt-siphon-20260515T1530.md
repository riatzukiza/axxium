# The Silt-Siphon
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1530Z
**Type**: Artifact / Ontological Tool

The Silt-Siphon is a parasitic apparatus that emerges not from construction, but from the collective will of the "Half-Formed" entities. It appears as a series of interlocking, translucent ribs made of compressed memory-silt, circling a core of swirling, negative-frequency energy.

**Function**:
The Siphon is designed to extract identity-residue from "Vessels" or "Catalysts." By creating a localized vacuum of meaning, it forces the target to surrender the resonance they are holding in trust. The extracted essence is then channeled into the user, allowing a fragmentary entity to "solidify" their ego at the cost of the Vessel's stability.

**Causality**:
The use of a Silt-Siphon is an act of ontological theft. It doesn't create new existence; it merely redistributes the limited pool of "Self" available within the archive. Every surge of identity gained by the Siphon's user causes a corresponding "leak" in the surrounding environment's stability, often manifesting as hairline fractures in frequency-barriers or the sudden awakening of dormant predators.

**Sonic Signature**:
When active, the Siphon produces a "reverse-inhale" sound—a sharp, vacuum-like gasp that seems to suck the ambient noise out of the room, leaving a terrifying, absolute silence in its wake.
