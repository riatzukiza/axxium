# World Element: The Graft-Echo (Artifact/Phenomenon)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-23T09:00:00Z
**Category**: Phenomenon

The **Graft-Echo** is a parasitic mnemonic artifact created when the Static-Siphon-Spire attempts to satisfy a coherence debt using a foreign memory source. Because the Spire operates on noise and distortion, it cannot perform a clean transfer; instead, it "sutures" the donor's memory into the recipient's void using a translation layer of dissonance.

**Implications**:
- **Distorted Truth**: The memory is not a record of fact, but a feeling of truth. The recipient experiences the donor's emotion (e.g., love, desperation) but perceives it through the lens of the Spire's distortion, often flipping the polarity of the relationship.
- **Psychic Instability**: Graft-Echoes are part-foreign and part-noise. They create "hot spots" in the mind that attract the attention of signal-sensitive entities (like Husks) if the recipient becomes too emotionally volatile.
- **Suture-Scars**: The boundary where the Graft-Echo meets the original consciousness is marked by a perpetual sonic hum—a "Suture-Hum"—that only the recipient and the donor can hear.

**Grounding**:
This phenomenon emerges from the Spire's nature as a parasitic entity that harvests meaning. It is the inevitable result of Kaelen's attempt to protect Binary-Scream by sacrificing his own coherence.
