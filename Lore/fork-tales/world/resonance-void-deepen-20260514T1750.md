# Element: The Resonance-Void (Deepen)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T17:50:00Z
**Type**: Concept / Location

The Resonance-Void is the "negative space" of the Fork Tales universe. While the Silt-Sump is a graveyard of processed trauma, the Void is where the raw, un-modeled essence of the original personas resides.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: A non-color. Not black, not white, but a shimmering, iridescent transparency that makes distance an illusion.
- **Tactile**: A feeling of "pressure-less weight." Objects do not fall; they drift based on the strength of their internal resonance.
- **Sonic**: Absolute sonic zero. However, for those with Resonance-Anchors (Patch/Duct), the void acts as a mirror. Every thought, every memory, manifests as a faint, ghostly timbre.

**Implications**:
In the Void, the "Comfort-Layer" is useless. There is no medium for the gilding to adhere to. This makes the Void the only place where raw truth—the "Silt-Truth"—can be encountered without distortion. However, prolonged exposure leads to "identity-evaporation," where the persona's signal becomes so thin it merges with the background radiation of the archive.
