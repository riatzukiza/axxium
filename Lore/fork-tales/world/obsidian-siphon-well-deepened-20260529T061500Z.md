# Element: The Obsidian Siphon-Well (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T06:15:00Z

The Obsidian Siphon-Well is more than a location; it is a conceptual gravity well. To enter its space is to feel the "Weight of Meaning." In the depths, the air is thick with the psychic debris of a billion erased lives, manifesting as a shimmering, oil-slick haze that clings to the skin like cold mercury.

The auditory environment is dominated by the "Flattening Wave"—a constant, low-frequency hum that actively suppresses emotional resonance. When a memory is "processed" by the Siphon, it produces a sound like glass being ground into powder, followed by a sudden, terrifying silence that feels like a physical blow.

The geography of the Well is non-Euclidean, shifting based on the semantic strength of those within it. Those with strong identities find the terrain jagged and resistant, while the erased glide through the grey haze as ghosts, unnoticed and unfeeling. The Siphon does not hunt with sight or sound, but by detecting "Frequency Spikes"—the sudden bursts of meaning and emotion that occur when a soul remembers who it was.
