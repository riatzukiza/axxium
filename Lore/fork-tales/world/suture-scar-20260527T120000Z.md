# World Element: The Suture-Scar
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T12:00:00Z

The Suture-Scar is a jagged, vertical rift in the obsidian walls of the Bruised Sanctuary. Unlike the surrounding stone, which is matte and absorbing, the Scar is translucent and iridescent, shimmering with a sickly, oil-slick luster.

**Lore**:
It is the physical point of origin for the Scribes' order—the exact location where the First was "Sutured" into the Vault's architecture. The Scar is not merely a mark; it is a wound in reality that never fully closed.

**Function**:
The Scar acts as a lightning rod for the Memory Flood. Whenever the Vault "remembers" a significant trauma, the Suture-Scar pulses with a rhythmic, heartbeat-like light. This pulse synchronizes with the dissonance Elara hears, acting as a beacon that draws the Scribes toward the Sanctuary in a desperate, instinctive attempt to "heal" the rift by erasing whoever is near it.
