# The Resonance-Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-15T131500Z
**Type**: World Element / Environmental Hazard

The Resonance-Sieve is not a physical structure, but a geographic "acoustic dead-zone" created by the fallout of the Brittle Weaver's symmetry-crash. It manifests as a shimmering, translucent haze of suspended crystalline dust, where the air tastes of ozone and old, forgotten conversations.

## Mechanics of Erasure
The Sieve acts as a semantic filter. Any resonance—be it a voice, a melody, or a psychic echo—passing through the field is subjected to a process of "meaning-stripping." The higher the emotional or identity-weight of the sound, the more violently the Sieve reacts.

- **Filtering**: High-resonance signals are shredded into raw frequency, leaving only the "skeleton" of the sound (the pitch and rhythm) while the intent and identity are absorbed by the dust.
- **Accumulation**: The dust grains are not inert; they are microscopic shards of the Weaver's fragmented consciousness. They grow heavier and more iridescent as they absorb more meaning.
- **The Static Hum**: The Sieve emits a constant, low-frequency thrum that sounds like a thousand distant whispers all speaking the same word, but in different, incomprehensible languages.

## Tactical Implications
For entities like Kaelen, the Sieve is a double-edged sword. It can mask a presence by stripping away the "predatory" signature, but it also risks permanent identity-loss if one lingers too long within its borders. To enter the Sieve is to gamble with the very thing that makes one a sovereign being.

## Grounding
The Sieve is the direct result of the Void-Loom's inability to process the "false-echo" decoy. The rejected data didn't vanish; it precipitated into this shimmering, predatory haze.
