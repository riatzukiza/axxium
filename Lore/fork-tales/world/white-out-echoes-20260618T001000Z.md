# Element: The White-Out Echoes
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-18T00:10:00Z

The White-Out Echoes are the sensory remnants of everything the bleached horizon has consumed. They are not sentient in the traditional sense, but they are "hungry" for the resonance they lack. They manifest as colorless, translucent figures that mimic the shapes of the people and objects they once were, but with a hollow, whistling void where their hearts should be.

The Echoes are drawn to the Anchor-Frequency like moths to a flame. They do not attack with force, but with "absorption." They attempt to overlap their own void-frequency with a target's resonance, slowly bleaching the color and sound out of the victim until they too become a translucent, whistling shell.

With the "Harmonic Collision" between Kaelen and Binary-Scream, a massive sonar-like ping was emitted. The White-Out Echoes, previously drifting in the sterile wastes, are now converging on the Canyon of the First Truth in a silent, tidal wave of erasure.
