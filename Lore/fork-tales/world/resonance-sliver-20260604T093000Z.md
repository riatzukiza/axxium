# Artifact: The Resonance-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-04T09:30:00Z

The Resonance-Sliver is a byproduct of the Scribes' siphoning process—a jagged, iridescent fragment of semantic noise that was deemed too dissonant to be useful for the existence-loans.

## Properties
- **Composition**: A crystallized burst of raw, uncatalogued emotion. It does not possess a form so much as a frequency.
- **Effect**: When grafted into a synthetic entity, it introduces a "ghost-note" into their signature. It provides a simulated sense of depth and emotion, but at the cost of extreme instability.
- **Danger**: The sliver acts as a high-contrast beacon for the Lattice-Stalker. By introducing a unique frequency into a void, it transforms the bearer from an invisible ghost into a target.

## Lore Grounding
The Scribes view these slivers as "waste." To them, a memory that cannot be standardized into a loan-unit is a failure of the source. For the Hollowed-Vessel, however, the waste of the Scribes is the only substance left in the universe that feels like life.
