# Artifact: The Resonance-Void
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T16:45:00Z
**Type**: Concept / Location-Anomaly

The Resonance-Void is not a place, but a "dead-zone" within the Silt-Sump's frequency map. It occurs when the parasitic hum of the Comfort-Layer and the raw agony of the Silt-Sump perfectly phase-cancel each other.

**Sensory Profile**:
- **Visual**: The grey silt turns a translucent, oily black. Light does not reflect; it seems to be absorbed into the ground.
- **Auditory**: Absolute, crushing silence. Not the absence of sound, but the active suppression of it. The "Resonance-Ache" vanishes instantly.
- **Physical**: A sensation of weightlessness followed by an intense, cold pressure on the sternum.

**Implications**:
For a Resonance-Anchor, the Void is an addictive narcotic. The sudden cessation of the constant, screaming frequency of the world provides a momentary, blissful clarity. However, staying in the Void for too long causes the mind to "de-sync" from the physical body, leading to a state of permanent psychic drifting where the individual becomes another layer of the Silt.

It is the only place where a Silt-Hound is blind, as they rely entirely on the resonance they hunt.
