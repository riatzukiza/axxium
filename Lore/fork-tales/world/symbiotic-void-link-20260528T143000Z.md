# Concept: The Symbiotic Void-Link
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T14:30:00Z

The Symbiotic Void-Link is a catastrophic failure of identity boundaries between a "Conduit" (like Kaelen) and a "Witness" (like Elara). 

When a Witness consumes a Gilded Fragment produced by a Conduit, the fragment acts as a psychic anchor. Instead of the memory being integrated into the Witness's identity, the fragment creates a permanent, invisible tether. 

**Implications**:
1. **Sensory Bleed**: The Conduit and Witness begin to share tactile and emotional states. The Witness's hunger feeds the Conduit's instability, and the Conduit's void-breath begins to leak into the Witness's psyche.
2. **Identity Erosion**: As the link strengthens, the distinction between "I" and "Thou" dissolves. The Witness stops remembering their own past and starts remembering the Conduit's void-shards.
3. **The Collapse Event**: Eventually, the tension of the tether reaches a breaking point. The two entities are pulled together in a "Singularity of Absence," where they merge into a single, non-existent point, erasing both completely from the world-state.

This link transforms the relationship from one of predatory lure to one of mutual destruction.
