# World Element: The Rippled Veil (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T06:15:00Z
**Category**: Location/Boundary

The Rippled Veil is not a wall, but a phase-boundary. To the eye, it appears as an oil-slick distortion in the air, a shimmering curtain where the geometry of the Obsidian Vault bends and folds upon itself. 

**Sensory Details**:
- **Touch**: Touching the Veil feels like thousands of microscopic needles of static. It is a tactile dissonance; the hand feels as if it is being pushed and pulled in opposite directions simultaneously.
- **Smell**: The air surrounding the Veil smells of ozone, burnt copper, and ancient, rotting parchment—the scent of information being forcibly compressed.
- **Sound**: Near the Veil, sound becomes "liquid." A whisper can travel for miles if it follows the ripple, while a scream can be swallowed instantly if it hits a fold.

**Behavioral Law**:
The Veil operates on the principle of "Tension-Mapping." It reflects the psychological state of those who approach it. To the fearful, it is an impenetrable wall of noise. To the desperate, it is a porous membrane. To Kaelen, in his Flicker-State, it is a map of the Scribes' intentions, rendered as a series of undulating, rhythmic pulses.
