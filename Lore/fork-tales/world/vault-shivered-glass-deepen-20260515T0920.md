# Location: The Vault of Shivered Glass (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T0920Z

The Vault is not a room, but a geometric paradox of reflective surfaces and sonic traps. Every wall is composed of "Silt-Glass"—translucent, razor-sharp sheets that do not reflect light, but instead reflect *sound*. A whisper spoken at one end of the hall may reappear minutes later as a scream behind the listener.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Tactile**: The air is unnaturally cold and tastes of ozone and old copper. The floor is a layer of fine, powdered glass that sighs under every footstep.
- **Visual**: Shifting prisms of grey and iridescent gold. When the "Core-Scream" echoes, the glass vibrates in cymatic patterns, temporarily revealing the faces of those erased.
- **Auditory**: A constant, low-frequency thrumming—the "Heartbeat of the Deleted"—interrupted by the sudden, violent 'tink' of a glass shard snapping under the weight of a memory.
- **Atmosphere**: A feeling of profound surveillance. To be in the Vault is to be heard by everyone who ever existed in the Archive, and to be seen only by your own fragmented echoes.
