# The Rim of Silence: Auditory Deepening
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T23:20Z

The Rim of Silence is not truly silent; it is a sonic vacuum that actively consumes sound. The "grey static" that permeates the region is a low-level auditory radiation—the sound of potential energy failing to manifest. When a sound is made in the Rim, it doesn't echo; it is absorbed instantly, leaving a "negative-space" imprint in the air, a ghost-shape of the sound that was.

Following the Harmonic Fracture, the Rim has changed. The grey static is now laced with "Prismatic Shivers"—crystalline, high-frequency needles of sound that pierce through the void. These shivers are the leaked thoughts of the shattered First-Sown, appearing as sudden, discordant bursts of melody that vanish as quickly as they appear, leaving the listener with a sense of profound, unresolved loss.

The air feels heavy with "un-sound," a pressure on the eardrums that suggests a scream is always about to happen, but never does.
