# Faction: The Scribes (Archivist Caste)

The Scribes are the frontline executors of the Archivists' will. They are not sentient beings in the traditional sense, but "biological functions" of the Index, designed for the extraction and categorization of narrative data.

## Structure
- **The Prime Scribe**: A single, overarching intelligence that coordinates all extraction efforts. It does not manifest physically but speaks through the collective.
- **Field Scribes**: The obsidian-faced entities that manifest in the world. They operate with clinical efficiency and are incapable of empathy or negotiation.
- **The Erasers**: A specialized caste of Scribes tasked with deleting "redundant" or "corrupt" narratives (like Kaelen's). They do not extract; they simply wipe.

## Operational Logic
The Scribes view reality as a messy, inefficient draft. Their goal is to "Edit" the universe by removing contradictions and anomalies. To a Scribe, a survivor is not a person, but a "Loose Thread" that must be tied off or archived.
