# Element: The Memory-Siphon
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-03T12:30:00Z
**Type**: Artifact

The Memory-Siphon is a device of concentric, counter-rotating obsidian rings that generate an inverted harmonic field. It does not simply "read" a memory; it creates a vacuum of meaning, pulling a specific, targeted conceptual anchor out of the subject's consciousness.

The device is operated by the Tether-Scribes, who use the "Obsidian Filament" as a tuning fork to calibrate the Siphon to the subject's unique resonant signature. 

Once a memory is extracted, it is not stored as data, but as a "Compressed Echo"—a tiny, vibrating crystal of pure meaning that is added to the Suture-Well's reserves. These crystals can be traded, sold, or grafted into other beings, allowing them to experience a truth they never lived. The cost of using a Siphon is the permanent loss of the original emotional weight associated with the memory; the subject remembers the *fact* of the event, but no longer *feels* its truth.
