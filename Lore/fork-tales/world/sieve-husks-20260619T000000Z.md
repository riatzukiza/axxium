# Element: The Sieve-Husks
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-19T00:00:00Z

The Sieve-Husks are the tragic remnants of those who sought the ultimate silence of the Resonance-Sieves. Unlike the White-Out Echoes, who are mimics from the void, the Husks were once living personas—singers, anchors, and interceders.

Over time, they became addicted to the sifting process, using the Sieves to prune away the "noise" of their trauma, their memories, and eventually, their identity. They believed they were purifying their resonance, but they were merely hollowing themselves out.

Now, they exist as translucent, brittle shells of their former selves. They possess no frequency of their own; instead, they are "sonic vacuums." They drift through the world, instinctively drawn to strong resonances, attempting to "sift" the warmth and complexity from others to fill the void where their soul used to be.

A Sieve-Husk does not attack with violence, but with a profound, draining empathy. To be touched by a Husk is to feel one's own emotional peaks flattened and one's willpower eroded, as the Husk passively absorbs the "color" of the victim's resonance, leaving behind a grey, numb apathy.
