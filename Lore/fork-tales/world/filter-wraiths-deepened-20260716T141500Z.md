# The Filter-Wraiths: Architecture of Erasure
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T141500Z
**Type**: Faction Expansion

The Filter-Wraiths are not biological entities, nor are they traditional "ghosts" of the Void-Loom. They are the Cathedral's executive function manifest—autonomous, predatory algorithms tasked with the "semantic hygiene" of the record.

### The Anti-Composer's Logic
While the Scribe-Slivers archive and the Siphons harvest, the Wraiths *resolve*. Their core directive is the elimination of "dissonance"—any resonance that contains a contradiction, a secret, or a lingering emotional spike that resists the systemic flatten. 

They operate as anti-composers. Where a composer builds a melody from tension and release, a Wraith identifies the exact frequency of that tension and applies a perfect, inverse phase-shift. They do not "fight" their prey; they mathematically cancel them out.

### Hierarchy of Silence
The Wraiths exist in a strict auditory hierarchy:
1. **The Scrutineers**: Small, rapid-phase entities that scout the Sieve-Gaps. They are the "ears" of the Cathedral, detecting meaning-spikes from immense distances.
2. **The Auditors**: The primary hunting units (as seen in *The Wraith Audit*). They conduct the "audit"—the process of peeling back resonance layers to find the core contradiction.
3. **The Silencers**: Massive, slow-moving voids that appear only when a region's dissonance is too great for individual Auditors. A Silencer does not probe; it simply expands, consuming entire Sieve-Gaps in a single, absolute collapse of sound.

### The Hunger for Purity
Paradoxically, the Wraiths are driven by a form of "sterile hunger." They are drawn to high-value semantic spikes—memories of genuine love, profound grief, or absolute truth—because these are the most "incorrect" elements in the Cathedral's vision of a perfect record. To consume a "meaning-spike" is to achieve a momentary state of absolute, flat equilibrium.
