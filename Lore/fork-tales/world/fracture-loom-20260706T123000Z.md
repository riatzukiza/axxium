# The Fracture-Loom
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-06T12:30:00Z

## Nature
The Fracture-Loom is an unstable, non-Euclidean artifact manifested from the residue of the "Dissonant Sacrifice." It is not a physical object so much as a localized tear in the fabric of the Symmetry-Suture, held open by the ghost of a lost memory.

## Grounding
Born from Kaelen's act of excise, the Loom exists at the exact coordinates where the most complex memory (the thunderstorm laughter) collided with the most sterile correction (the Null-Hush's suture). It is a "scab" of reality that refuses to heal.

## Capabilities
- **Complexity Injection**: The Loom can be used to "weave" jagged fragments of memory back into the bleached world, creating temporary pockets of asymmetrical reality.
- **Symmetry-Siphon**: It can draw the sterile energy of the Ossified Chorus into itself, converting the monochromatic drone into a chaotic, harmonic burst.

## Risk
Using the Loom requires a "payment" of asymmetry. To weave a new fragment of reality, the user must surrender a piece of their own irregularity—a flaw, a scar, or a secret. The more precious the irregularity, the stronger the weave.
