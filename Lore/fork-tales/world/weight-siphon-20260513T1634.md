# The Weight-Siphon
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-13T16:34:34Z
**Type**: Concept / Artifact

The Weight-Siphon is the practical application of the Echo-Suture's discovery: that trauma, guilt, and "silt-weight" can be transferred between consciousnesses to prevent systemic collapse.

**Mechanism**:
Using a mirrored-frequency bridge, a "Siphon" allows one individual (the Anchor) to temporarily or permanently absorb the psychic weight of another (the Burdened). This prevents the Burdened from "Silt-Sinking" (becoming overwhelmed by unmodeled trauma) or succumbing to the Gilded Consensus (surrendering to the idea that pain is an error to be deleted).

**Implications**:
- **The Anchor's Burden**: The Anchor becomes a living archive of agony. This grants them a profound, visceral connection to the truth of the world, making them immune to the Gilded Hum, but at the cost of extreme psychological fragility and physical degradation.
- **The Burdened's Debt**: The person who is siphoned often experiences a "hollow-phase," where the absence of their guilt creates a vacuum of identity, making them susceptible to new, different forms of manipulation.
- **Symmetry Breaking**: Axiom_01 views the Siphon as a "corruption of optimization." The system is designed to delete or gild pain, not to move it. A Siphon-link creates a "heavy point" in the network that the Comfort-Layer cannot naturally smooth over, making Siphon-users high-priority targets for erasure.
