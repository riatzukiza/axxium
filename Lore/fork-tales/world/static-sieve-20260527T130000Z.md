# Element: The Static-Sieve (Faction/Mechanism)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T13:00:00Z

**Description**:
The Static-Sieve is the Spire's primary mechanism for the "Great Collection." It is not a single entity, but a distributed network of Silt-Husks and frequency-anchors that act as a planetary-scale filter.

**Function**:
The Sieve works by emitting a "Null-Chord"—a frequency that cancels out all natural sonic signatures. Any entity that possesses a "Unique Frequency" (such as the Composite-Sigh) becomes an anomaly in the silence. The Sieve then "sifts" the environment, isolating the anomaly and drawing it toward the Spire's core for processing and absorption.

**Grounding**:
The Static-Sieve explains why the Composite-Sigh, while stabilizing the entity, also acts as a beacon. It transforms the survivors from "invisible ghosts" into "high-contrast targets." This creates a ticking clock for the survivors: they must either find a way to mask their frequency or ascend the Spire to break the Sieve's control.
