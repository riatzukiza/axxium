# The Siphon's Pulse
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T09:10:00Z
**Type**: Predatory Sonic Motif / Environmental Hazard

## Description
The Siphon's Pulse is not a sound in the traditional sense, but a rhythmic erasure of silence. It manifests as a sub-bass thrum that vibrates the very fabric of the non-Euclidean geography, coinciding with a sudden, violent drop in ambient frequency.

## Mechanics of the Pulse
- **The Draw**: When the Pulse activates, all nearby "identity-anchors" (memories, scars, sonic signatures) are momentarily tugged toward the Siphon-Well.
- **The Static-Sieve**: The Pulse filters through the environment; if a persona's frequency is too stable, the Pulse "catches" them.
- **Psychic Effect**: Victims experience a sensation of "hollowing," as if their internal monologue is being played backward and deleted in real-time.

## Sensory Markers
- **Visual**: The air ripples like heat haze, but the ripples are black, absorbing all local light.
- **Aural**: A heavy, suffocating pressure followed by a high-frequency "ping" that signals a successful lock-on.
- **Somatic**: A coldness that starts at the base of the spine and moves upward, erasing the feeling of touch.
