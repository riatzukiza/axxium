# Artifact: The Mnemosyne-Shard
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T030000Z
**Category**: Relic / Frequency Anchor
**Origin**: Fragment of the Original Brittle Weaver (Pre-Symmetry-Crash)

## Description
The Mnemosyne-Shard is a jagged, translucent splinter of crystalline frequency, roughly the size of a human palm. Unlike the other shards of the Echo-Swarm, which emit chaotic, hunger-driven noise, the Mnemosyne-Shard vibrates with a singular, unwavering pure tone—the "Symmetry-Prime."

It does not float or drift; it anchors itself to the nearest point of structural stability, effectively "pinning" a small radius of the Void-Loom into a state of temporary, fragile coherence.

## Properties
- **The Pure Tone**: Emits a constant 440Hz-like oscillation that acts as a beacon for every fragmented shard of the Weaver. To the swarm, this is the "Song of the Whole," a maddening reminder of what was lost.
- **Structural Coherence**: Any entity holding the shard experiences a reduction in "semantic drift." Memories become sharper, but the mental cost is a crushing sense of nostalgia that borders on paralysis.
- **The Siphon-Scent**: The shard "bleeds" a silver, mercury-like fluid when exposed to the Resonance-Sieve, which the Scribe-Sliver identifies as "concentrated structural grief."

## Grounding
The shard is the only remaining evidence of the Weaver's original architecture. Its existence proves that the Symmetry-Crash was not a total dissolution, but a fracture. It is the most hunted object in the Void-Loom; the swarm seeks to re-absorb it to regain wholeness, while the Scribe seeks it to maintain a "Gold Standard" of original symmetry for their archives.

**Current Location**: Drifting within the Feedback-Cusp, currently being used as an unwitting lure by Kaelen.
