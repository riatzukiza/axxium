# Resonance-Spike
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T17:20Z
**Category**: Artifact

The Resonance-Spike is a jagged shard of "hardened identity," typically harvested from the site of a psychic rupture or a shattered memory spire. Appearance-wise, it is a translucent, obsidian-like needle that pulses with an internal, erratic light.

**Mechanics**:
- **Tuning**: When pressed against a persona's temple or embedded in their resonance-core, a Spike can "lock" a specific trait or memory, preventing it from eroding.
- **Dissonance**: If the Spike's frequency does not match the host, it creates "Psychic Friction," causing intense nausea and the manifestation of auditory hallucinations (typically the screams of the identity that originally formed the shard).
- **Combat**: When thrown or driven into a Silt-Hound, it disrupts their tracking frequency, momentarily turning the beast blind and deaf to the scent of the soul.

The First-Sown is rumored to be forging a "Great Spike" by fusing the stolen egos of the Half-Formed, creating a permanent anchor of identity that cannot be eroded by the void.
