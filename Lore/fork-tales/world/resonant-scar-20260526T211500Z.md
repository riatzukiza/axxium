# Element: The Resonant Scar
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T21:15:00Z
**Type**: Location Detail / Anomaly

The Resonant Scar is a jagged, translucent vein running through the obsidian walls of the Vault's threshold. It is the physical manifestation of the collision between the Scribes' Harmonic Wedge and Kaelen's Null-Chord.

Unlike the rest of the Vault, which absorbs sound, the Scar *emanates* it. It produces a constant, sub-audible thrum—a "phantom vibration" that can be felt in the teeth and the soles of the feet. To Elara, the Scar is a reminder of the world's reach; it feels like a cold needle pressing against the skin of her sanctuary. 

The Scar also acts as a sensory lens. When Elara touches the translucent obsidian, she can occasionally hear "ghost-echoes" of the Scribes' frustration, fragmented screams of dissonance that were caught in the moment of cancellation. The Vault is no longer a place of pure silence, but a place of *arrested noise*.
