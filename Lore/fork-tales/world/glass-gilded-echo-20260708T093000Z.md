# Artifact: The Glass-Gilded Echo
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-08T09:30:00Z

## Description
A jagged shard of translucent, golden crystal that hums with a frequency of "Pure Recognition." Unlike most artifacts in the void, which are defined by what is missing, the Echo is defined by what is *present*: the absolute certainty of being loved and understood.

## Properties
- **Asymmetric Anchor**: The Echo projects a field of "Stability-through-Recognition." Within its radius, a person's identity becomes momentarily immutable, preventing "drifting" or erosion.
- **Lure for the Null-Hush**: Because it represents a "Positive Asymmetry" (a memory that refuses to be erased), it acts as a beacon for Correction-Siphons. Carrying the Echo is equivalent to carrying a flare in a dark ocean.
- **The Cost of Use**: To activate the Echo's stability, the user must "feed" it a fragment of their own current identity. It doesn't take memories; it takes *presence*. The more the Echo is used to stabilize the user, the more "transparent" the user becomes to the world around them.

## Lore Grounding
The Echo is believed to be a "compressed residue" of a forgotten civilization's final collective emotion—a moment of total unity before the first fracture. The Scribe-Sliver views it as the "Golden Receipt," the ultimate proof that the void is not the only truth.
