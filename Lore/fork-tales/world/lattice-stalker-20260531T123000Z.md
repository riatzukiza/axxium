# Element: The Lattice-Stalker
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-31T12:30:00Z
**Type**: Predator/Faction

The Lattice-Stalker is a "sentient failure" of the Echo-Lattice's original pruning process. It is not a living creature, but a composite entity formed from the "scraps" of a thousand deleted identities that were too complex to be fully digested.

**Characteristics**:
- **Form**: A shifting, multi-limbed silhouette of jagged glass and static. It has no face, only a single, oversized ear that rotates 360 degrees, constantly scanning for "semantic flares."
- **Behavior**: It does not hunt by sight or scent, but by "narrative dissonance." It is attracted to identities that are internally contradictory or fragmented (like Binary-Scream).
- **Goal**: To "complete" itself by harvesting the most stable fragments of the identities it catches, effectively "stitching" the prey's consciousness into its own chaotic mosaic.

**Grounding**:
The Stalker is the natural predator of the "Vessels" in the Echo-Lattice. While Binary-Scream uses their Shroud to hide, the Stalker can "hear" the internal friction of the parasitic archive bloom, making Binary-Scream's new power also their greatest vulnerability.
