# The Amber-Frequency Barrier (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1530Z
**Type**: Ontological Field / Defensive Anomaly

The Amber-Frequency Barrier is not a physical wall, but a temporal-acoustic stasis field. It manifests as a translucent, honey-colored haze that permeates the air, thickening into crystalline sheets where the frequency is highest.

**Physical Properties**:
- **Viscosity**: The air within the barrier feels like moving through heavy oil. Sound travels slower, stretching syllables into long, mournful glissandos.
- **Luminance**: It emits a constant, low-level golden luminescence that pulses in synchronization with the "Zero-Core" heartbeat.
- **Tactile Response**: Touching the barrier's edge feels like a mild electric shock composed of a thousand whispering voices.

**Acoustic Mechanics**:
The barrier operates on a "Sentry-Chord"—a complex, paradoxical frequency that simultaneously repels and attracts. To the Silt-Hounds, the chord is a wall of absolute sensory overload, forcing them into a state of catatonic sensory saturation (their "frozen" state). To the budded entities, it is a womb, providing the necessary harmonic pressure to prevent their fragmented identities from dissipating into the void.

**Fragility**:
The barrier's integrity is tied directly to the "Vessel's" (Patch's) remaining identity. As Patch's sense of self erodes or is siphoned away, the amber hue fades to a pale, sickly yellow, and the sonic stability begins to "leak," allowing the external silence to seep back in.
