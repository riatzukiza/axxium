# Element: The Ash-Wind
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T20:00:00Z
**Type**: Environmental Phenomenon / Medium of Communication

The Ash-Wind is a rhythmic, pulsing tide of grey ash that flows across the Null-Zone. It is not wind in the meteorological sense, but a psychic current composed of the "residual frequencies" of erased individuals. 

## Properties
- **Emotional Gravity**: The wind flows toward areas of high emotional intensity, particularly the Void-Aperture (Elara).
- **Mnemonic Resonance**: Touching the Ash-Wind allows a person to experience "fragments" of erased memories, though this usually results in overwhelming grief if the subject is not anchored.
- **Structural Instability**: Where the Ash-Wind is strongest, the geography of the Null-Zone becomes fluid, shifting between obsidian ruins and a grey void.

## Interaction
The Ash-Wind serves as a bridge between the remaining Scribes and the "Ghost-Scribes"—the composite entities formed by the accumulation of erased history. It is the only medium through which the Scribes can now perceive the true scale of their failures.
