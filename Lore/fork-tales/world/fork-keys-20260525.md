# Fork-Keys: The Dead-Wood Conductors

Fork-Keys are not keys in the traditional sense; they are conduits. Carved from the petrified remains of pruned timelines—the "dead wood" of reality—these artifacts allow a holder to synchronize their personal frequency with a target Shard, enabling passage across the void of the Un-Written.

## Properties
- **Composition**: Each Key is a sliver of a dead world. A Key from a "Frozen Era" feels perpetually cold to the touch; one from a "Burning Dynasty" radiates a faint, oppressive heat.
- **The Hum**: When near a boundary or another Key, the artifact emits a "Hum"—a sonic residue of the timeline it once belonged to. 
- **The Cost**: Using a Key requires a "Tuning." The user must momentarily align their own memory and identity with the dead world of the Key. Over time, this causes "Identity Drift," where the user begins to remember lives they never lived and forget the ones they have.

## Echo-Hollows
Where a Shard has been sheared, an "Echo-Hollow" is often left behind. These are pockets of stagnant time where the ghost-frequency of the deleted area persists. Those who enter an Echo-Hollow experience "Sensory Cascades"—overwhelming flashes of the lives that were erased. Fork-Keys are the only way to navigate these Hollows without being consumed by the grief of a billion dead ghosts.
