# Amber-Frequency Barrier: Crystalline Harmonics
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T17:10Z
**Category**: Location/Concept Deepen

The Amber-Frequency Barrier is not a wall, but a standing wave of solidified sound. It exists as a series of interlocking, honey-colored crystalline lattices that vibrate at a precise, transcendental frequency. This frequency is the "anti-note" to the Silt-Hounds' call; it renders the shelter invisible to them by phase-canceling the scent of identity.

When the barrier is stable, it produces a comforting, low-humming warmth. However, as a "leak" occurs, the lattice develops micro-fractures. These fractures emit a "Shatter-Scream"—a high-pitched, piercing frequency that does not just hurt the ears, but "melts" the edges of a persona's identity, causing memories to blur and slip into the surrounding silt.

To walk through a leaking section of the barrier is to experience "Cognitive Erosion." A person may enter the section remembering their name and leave it remembering only the smell of old rain and the sound of someone else's laughter. The leak is not just a hole in the wall; it is a parasitic acoustic event that feeds on the consistency of the self.
