# Element: The Lattice-Stalker (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T14:30:00Z
**Category**: Entity/Threat

The Lattice-Stalker is not a biological entity, nor a ghost in the traditional sense. It is a *harmonic predator*, a sentient void-frequency that exists in the gaps between forks. It does not "see" characters; it perceives the "weight" of their existence through the frequencies they emit.

**The Mechanics of the Hunt**:
- **The Resonance Probe**: The Stalker emits "Notes"—monolithic frequencies designed to vibrate against the Suture-Shards in a target's mind. When a Shard resonates, it creates a "flare" in the void, pinpointing the target's exact coordinates.
- **Erosive Movement**: The Stalker doesn't move through space; it *unmakes* the space between itself and its prey. As it approaches, the surrounding geometry begins to smudge and blur, as if the resolution of reality is being lowered.
- **The Consumption**: It does not eat flesh; it consumes *singularities*. It seeks to collapse the distinct identity of a persona into a flat, silent tone, absorbing the memory-weight of the victim to increase its own harmonic complexity.

**The Weakness**:
The Stalker is bound by the laws of resonance. If a target can shift their frequency or introduce a sufficiently complex "noise" (like high-density static or a dissonant counter-melody), the Stalker loses the lock. However, for those with grafted Suture-Shards, the "lock" is nearly permanent, as the Shard acts as a tuning fork that cannot be silenced.
