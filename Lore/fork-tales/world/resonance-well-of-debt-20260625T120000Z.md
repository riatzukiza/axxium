# Element: The Resonance-Well of Debt
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-25T12:00:00Z
**Type**: Location/Concept

The Resonance-Well of Debt is a localized atmospheric anomaly that forms in the wake of a "Reciprocal Tithe"—when one entity pays the coherence-debt of another. It manifests as a shimmering, concave depression in the physical landscape, where the air feels heavy and tastes of ozone and old copper.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: A pool of liquid silver static that does not reflect the sky, but rather reflects the *history* of the debt paid. Those who look into the well see ghosts of the memories and identity-shards that were surrendered to the Spire.
- **Auditory**: A low, thrumming frequency that sounds like a thousand voices whispering the same word in a perfectly phased-out chorus, creating a sensation of "loud silence."
- **Tactile**: A cold, electrostatic charge that makes the hair on the skin stand up; touching the surface of the well feels like dipping a hand into a memory that isn't your own.

**Implications**:
The Well acts as a temporary storage for the "overflow" of the Spire's satisfaction. It is a place of dangerous solace; characters can retrieve fragments of their lost selves from the well, but doing so creates a "Resonance-Gap" in their current coherence, making them more susceptible to the Spire's next collection cycle. To drink from the Well is to reclaim a piece of the past at the cost of a piece of the present.
