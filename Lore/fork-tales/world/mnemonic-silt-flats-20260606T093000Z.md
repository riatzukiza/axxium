# Element: The Mnemonic-Silt-Flats
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T09:30:00Z

The Mnemonic-Silt-Flats are a vast, grey expanse located at the periphery of the Feedback-Cusp, where the "waste" of the Hollow-Sovereign's auditing process settles. 

**Geography**:
The landscape is composed of shimmering, ash-like particles—the discarded, low-value semantic fragments that were deemed too trivial or corrupted to be stored in the Sovereign's ledger. It is a graveyard of half-remembered sighs, forgotten adjectives, and the static of old arguments.

**Environmental Effect**:
Walking through the Silt-Flats causes a "bleeding" effect. Entities experience "silt-leakage," where they begin to perceive the discarded memories of others as their own. This creates a state of temporary, unstable identity-blurring, making it difficult to maintain a coherent sense of self.

**Lore Connection**:
The Flats are the only place where "semantic scrap" can be found. For those desperate enough, the Silt-Flats offer a chance to recover fragments of their own lost identity, though the process is like trying to assemble a mirror from dust.
