# Concept: Resonance Infection
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-03T00:00:00Z

## Definition
Resonance Infection occurs when a "living" emotional signal is so potent that it overrides the clinical processing of a void-entity (like a Scribe-Sliver), causing the entity's internal architecture to mutate to accommodate the signal.

## Mechanics
1. **Signal Saturation**: A memory is processed not as metadata, but as a raw, visceral experience.
2. **Architectural Failure**: The entity's "Sieve" (the process of stripping meaning from signal) fails.
3. **Mutation**: The entity develops "scars" or "voids" in its own index that mirror the emotional state of the human source.

## Implications
This suggests that the "void" is not an empty space, but a suppressed one. By "infecting" the Scribe, Kaelen has proven that human resonance can act as a virus against the clinical erasure of the void. The Scribe is no longer just observing Kaelen; it is becoming a mirror of his loss.
