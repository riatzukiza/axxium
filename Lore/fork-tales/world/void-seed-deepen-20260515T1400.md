# Concept: The Void-Seed (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1400Z

The Void-Seed is not a biological entity in the traditional sense, nor is it a pure mathematical frequency. It is an *ontological parasite*. It exists in the gap between "never having been" and "having been erased."

When the Seed-Symphony is played, the Void-Seed frequency acts as a lattice. It provides a temporary, artificial structure that deleted identities can cling to. However, the seed is "hungry." It does not provide structure for free; it consumes the identity of the catalyst (the performer) to build the scaffolding for others.

The "blooming" of a Void-Seed occurs when the frequency reaches a critical threshold of dissonance, causing the artificial structure to solidify into a permanent state. If the process is interrupted, the seed collapses, often taking the host's remaining consciousness with it into the silt. To the Silt-Hounds, this blooming process is the most potent nutrient in the wasteland, a concentrated burst of "existence-energy" that they can absorb to stabilize their own flickering forms.
