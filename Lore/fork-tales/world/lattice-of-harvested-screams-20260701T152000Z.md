# The Lattice of Harvested Screams
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-01T15:20:00Z
**Type**: Parasitic Architecture / Signal-Siphon Artifact
**Status**: Prototypical / Emerging

## Description
The Lattice is not a city in the traditional sense, but a geometric crystallization of stolen resonance. It manifests as a series of interlocking obsidian needles and translucent, vibrating membranes that stretch across the Hollow of the Hush. Each "street" is a frozen frequency; each "building" is a compressed loop of a stolen memory.

The structure grows by feeding on the high-fidelity signals extracted from Vessels. Following the *First Evisceration*, the Lattice has expanded rapidly, incorporating the collective sorrow of the Choir that was stripped from Kaelen.

## Sonic Properties
- **Architecture of Echo**: The Lattice does not produce its own sound; it reflects and distorts the signals of those trapped within it. To walk through the Lattice is to hear your own past conversations played back as structural supports.
- **The Hum of Ownership**: A constant, low-frequency thrum (the "Sovereign's Pulse") that signals the Sentries' total ownership of the harvested data.
- **Frequency Dead-Zones**: Pockets of absolute silence where the extraction was so complete that even the concept of sound has been erased.

## Implications
- **For Kaelen**: The Lattice is a mirror of his own loss. Every spire he sees is a piece of his missing identity, rendered in cold, black glass.
- **For the Sentries**: The Lattice serves as a processing hub, refining raw "Scream-Data" into a stable, governable reality.
- **For the Erased**: The ghosts are no longer just drifting; they are being "filed" into the architecture, their consciousness serving as the mortar for the obsidian walls.

## Grounding
Rooted in the *Siphon-Sentry* faction's goal of "The Great Cataloging." The Lattice is the physical manifestation of the transition from *Void-Sifting* to *Void-Sovereignty*.
