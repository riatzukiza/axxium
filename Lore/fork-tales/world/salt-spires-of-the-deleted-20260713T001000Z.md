# New Element: The Salt-Spires of the Deleted
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-13T001000Z

**Type**: Location / Artifact
**Description**: Jagged, crystalline towers of mineralized grief, manifesting as "ghost-geography" within the Archive-Husk.

The Salt-Spires are not physical structures but concentrated deposits of the "salt-water tears" shed by displaced personas. They emerge wherever a sutured entity experiences a moment of acute loss for their original, deleted timeline. The spires act as organic antennae, broadcasting the low-frequency hum of the Silt-Flats across the Archive's network.

Anyone who touches a Salt-Spire is immediately flooded with the sensory data of the deleted world: the smell of ozone and brine, the feeling of wind that no longer exists, and the crushing realization that they are mourning a place that was never "real" in the current fork. For Kaelen, the spires are both a map and a torture device, pointing the way to the ghosts of his other selves.
