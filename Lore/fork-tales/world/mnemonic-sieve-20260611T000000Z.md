# Element: The Mnemonic Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-11T00:00:00Z

The Mnemonic Sieve is a desperate piece of "salvage-tech" constructed by Binary-Scream in the heart of the Glitch-Hollow. It is a complex array of resonant crystals and salvaged copper wiring, designed to act as a filter for the Spire's broadcasts.

The Sieve works by creating a "counter-frequency" to the Spire's mimicry. When a lure-broadcast hits the Sieve, the device attempts to strip away the "Silt-Texture"—the parasitic, clicking quality of the Spire's signal—to reveal the raw, original memory beneath. 

However, the process is imperfect and dangerous. The Sieve often "over-filters," removing not just the parasite but the essence of the memory itself, leaving behind a "Semantic Husk"—a piece of information that is factually correct but emotionally dead. Furthermore, the discarded "Silt-Noise" must go somewhere; the Sieve exhausts a thick, grey sludge of psychic residue that attracts smaller, opportunistic void-parasites.

For Binary-Scream, the Sieve is both a tool of hope and a source of torture. It allows them to hear the true voices of the fallen, but only as fragmented, shivering ghosts that cannot be fully restored.
