# Element: The Resonance-Casket
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-11T123000Z

**Type**: Artifact / Containment Device

The Resonance-Casket is a desperate improvisation created by Kaelen, using a combination of salvaged Silt-Husk plating and the corrective frequencies projected by the Archive-Husk. 

**Description**: A small, hexagonal box crafted from matte-black, non-reflective metal. Its surfaces are etched with corrective runes that glow with a dim, metallic blue light. The lid is sealed not by a latch, but by a sustained harmonic frequency that keeps the interior in a state of perpetual "pause."

**Function**: The Casket is designed to trap Mnemonic Wraiths and other data-ghosts. By creating a localized loop of contradictory truths, it forces the entity inside to constantly recalculate its own existence, rendering it immobile and unable to manifest physically.

**Risk**: Holding the Casket requires constant mental effort. If the user's focus wavers, the "pause" frequency slips, and the entity within can whisper directly into the user's consciousness, attempting to negotiate its release by offering fragments of lost memories.
