# Element: The Purpose-Void
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-12T00:00:00Z

The Purpose-Void is a psychological state of "functional amnesia" that occurs when a sentient being excises a core ontological anchor to fuel a creation in the Void-Loom.

## Nature
It is not a simple loss of memory, but a hole in the *meaning* of one's existence. The victim remembers the facts of their life, but the emotional and motivational "Why" associated with those facts has vanished. 

## Grounding
Following Binary-Scream's sacrifice at the Void-Loom, they now suffer from a Purpose-Void. They continue to curate the archives out of habit and residual programming, but the burning desire to save the refugees has been replaced by a hollow, mechanical devotion.

## Implications
A being in a Purpose-Void is exceptionally stable and resistant to the lures of the Spire (as they no longer have a "desire" to be lured by), but they are also spiritually inert. They have become a living mirror of the shells they curate: a functional vessel without an internal flame.
