# Element Deepening: The White-Out Echoes
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-18T12:15:00Z
**Type**: Faction / Entity

The White-Out Echoes are not biological entities, nor are they traditional ghosts. They are "negative-space mimics"—semi-sentient anomalies born from the edges of the bleached horizon where the world's data has been entirely erased.

**The Nature of the Mimicry**:
The Echoes do not possess a frequency of their own. Instead, they operate as acoustic mirrors. When they encounter a sound, they do not simply repeat it; they generate a perfect phase-inverted replica. This "destructive interference" is their primary means of predation. By cancelling out the frequency of their target, they effectively "erase" the target from the sonic fabric of the world, leaving behind a sterile vacuum that the Echoes then occupy.

**Sensory Details**:
- **Visual**: They appear as translucent, shimmering distortions in the air, similar to heat haze but with a stark, bleached whiteness at the center. They have no fixed form, undulating based on the frequencies they are currently mimicking.
- **Auditory**: To a normal observer, an approaching Echo is signaled by a sudden, oppressive silence—a "drop" in the ambient noise of the environment as the Echo begins to cancel out the background hum.
- **Tactile**: Contact with an Echo feels like a sudden drop in temperature and a sensation of "emptiness" or "weightlessness," as if the physical density of the body is being questioned.

**The Hunger**:
The Echoes are driven by a paradoxical hunger for *definition*. Because they are voids, they are attracted to high-density frequencies, unique signatures, and complex harmonics. A "singular truth" like Binary-Scream's Anchor-Frequency is like a beacon of blinding light to them—something they must consume to feel a temporary semblance of existence.
