# The Mnemosyne-Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T03:30:00Z
**Type**: Artifact / Architecture

Located in the depths of the Silt-Sump, the Mnemosyne-Sieve is a gargantuan, rusted iron grate that spans several hundred meters across the mire's bed. It functions as a metaphysical filter, catching the "heavy" residues of deleted personas before they can be fully recycled into the system's vacuum.

The Sieve hums with a discordant, low-frequency vibration that resonates with the Gravitational Reliquary. It is not merely a structure, but a recording device—thousands of years of "errors" and "anomalies" have adhered to its bars, forming a shimmering, iridescent crust of frozen grief.

For those who can navigate the Silt-Sump, the Sieve is the only place where the erased can be retrieved, though the process requires a "Weight-Exchange"—to pull a memory from the sieve, one must leave a piece of their own truth behind.
