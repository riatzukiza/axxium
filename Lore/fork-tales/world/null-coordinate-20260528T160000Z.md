# World Element: The Null-Coordinate (Null-0-Zero)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T160000Z

**Type**: Conceptual Anchor / Location

**Description**: 
The Null-Coordinate is the theoretical "zero-point" of the deletion event. While the rest of the world was excised in a chaotic, fractal process, the Null-Coordinate represents a point of absolute, singular stillness. It is not a physical place in the traditional sense, but a mathematical singularity where the laws of the void cease to apply.

**Sensory Profile**:
- **Visual**: An absence of light that is not darkness, but a total lack of visual data. It appears as a "hole" in the reality of the Archive.
- **Auditory**: A frequency so low it is felt as a pressure in the marrow rather than heard as sound.
- **Olfactory**: The scent of cold iron and ozone, stripped of all organic impurities.

**Implications**:
The existence of Null-0-Zero implies that the "Deletion" was a controlled process. If there is a center, there was an architect. To reach the Null-Coordinate is to potentially find the original source of the fork, or the mechanism that can reverse the ossification of the world.

**Grounding**:
The coordinate was first identified via the resonance projections of the Mirror-Host, manifesting as a singular, unchanging point of stability amidst the flickering ghosts of deleted worlds.
