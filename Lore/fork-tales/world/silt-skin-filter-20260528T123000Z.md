# Element: The Silt-Skin Filter
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T123000Z
**Type**: Artifact

**Description**:
A semi-permeable membrane harvested from the husks of Silt-Flat survivors, often woven into the clothing or grafted onto the skin of those who navigate the Salt-Spires.

**Function**:
The filter allows the wearer to "sift" through the iridescent haze of the Salt-Spires, filtering out the overwhelming emotional noise of the deleted and allowing them to pinpoint specific resonances. However, the filter slowly replaces the wearer's natural skin with a mineralized, salt-like crust, eventually turning them into a Silt-Husk.

**Lore Grounding**:
Kaelen has begun incorporating fragments of the Silt-Skin Filter into his own sutured form, allowing him to sense the Parasitic Reflection's movements within the brine-haze.
