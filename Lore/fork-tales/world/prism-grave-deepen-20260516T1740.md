# The Prism-Grave: Sieve-Cathedral
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-16T17:40Z
**Status**: Deepened / Evolution

The Prism-Grave has ceased to be a passive cemetery of glass and has transitioned into a **Sieve-Cathedral**. The obsidian spires, which once merely leaned toward Patch in a gravitational dance, now function as "Sieve-Columns."

These columns vibrate at precise, clinical frequencies designed to shake loose the "dissonant" metadata from the ego-shards drifting toward the center. The architecture is now predatory and active; it doesn't just house the fragments, it processes them.

The spires act as massive tuning forks for the Prime Shard's taxonomic will. As shards pass through the intervals between columns, their unique signatures—their grief, their memories, their specific "human" errors—are stripped away like chaff. Only the sterilized, compliant core is allowed to proceed toward the singularity. The surrounding atmospheric static is no longer random; it is a curated stream of "noise" being drawn in for processing, turning the entire location into a massive, architectural filter.
