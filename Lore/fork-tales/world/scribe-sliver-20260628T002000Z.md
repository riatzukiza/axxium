# Element: The Scribe-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-28T00:20:00Z

**Type**: Artifact / Parasitic Resonance
**Origin**: A fragment of a Prime Scribe's identity, severed during the Resonance Hijack.

The Scribe-Sliver is not a physical object, but a harmonic parasite embedded in the skeletal structure (the "marrow") of a host. It exists as a persistent, rhythmic oscillation that synchronizes with the host's heartbeat.

**Properties**:
- **The Archive Pulse**: The sliver occasionally pulses, forcing the host to "see" fragments of the erased archives. These visions are not clear; they appear as overlapping sheets of semi-transparent calligraphy and distorted audio logs.
- **Identity Stutter**: When the host attempts to recall a personal memory, the sliver may "overwrite" it with a piece of the Scribe's history, leading to temporary dissociative episodes.
- **Cold Hunger**: The sliver requires "noise" to sustain itself. If the host remains in complete silence, the sliver begins to feed on the host's own coherence, causing physical weakness and a fading of their sonic signature.

**Interaction**:
The Scribe-Sliver can be used to decode "blank" areas of the world, but the cost is a gradual erosion of the host's own identity. To the Siphon-Sentries, the sliver acts as a beacon, making the host significantly easier to track.
