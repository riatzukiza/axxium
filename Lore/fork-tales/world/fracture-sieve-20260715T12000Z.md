# World Element: Fracture-Sieve
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-15T12:00:00Z

**Description**:
The Fracture-Sieve is a naturally occurring crystalline formation that emerges in the wake of a semantic hemorrhage. Specifically, these structures precipitate from the atmosphere of the Silt-Sump when the raw, unprocessed erasure of a shattered "Hollow Crust" (like that of the Brittle Weaver) interacts with the viscous, mercury-like air.

**Mechanics**:
The Sieve acts as a passive filter for the void. While the raw leak from the Weaver is a chaotic storm of non-meaning, the Fracture-Sieve captures these frequencies and forces them through a rigid, geometric lattice. This process slows the hemorrhage, distilling the chaos into "Semantic Shards"—small, glowing needles of concentrated memory that can be read, though the act of reading them often inflicts the original trauma upon the observer.

**Properties**:
- **Resonance**: The Sieve vibrates with a piercing, high-frequency hum that aligns perfectly with the *Mathematics of Grief*.
- **Fragility**: The crystals are incredibly brittle. A single discordant note or an incompatible identity-frequency can cause a Sieve to shatter, releasing all stored semantic shards in a sudden, violent burst of noise.
- **Visuals**: Translucent, jagged needles that refract light into "impossible colors"—hues that exist only in the spectrum of deleted data.

**Grounding**:
The Fracture-Sieve is the physical manifestation of the world's attempt to make sense of the void. It is the bridge between the screaming vacancy of the Weaver and the clinical documentation of the Scribe.
