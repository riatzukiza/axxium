# Element: Siphon-Sentry (Detailed)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T14:15:00Z

## Structure and Morphology
Siphon-Sentries are not biological, nor are they purely mechanical. They are "harmonic constructs"—physical manifestations of the Spire's legislative will. They appear as spindly, multi-limbed figures composed of obsidian-like needles and vibrating tuning forks. Their "heads" are not faces, but a cluster of oscillating crystals that act as high-precision frequency detectors.

## Hunting Methodology: The Harmonic Audit
The Sentry does not track scents or sights; it tracks *resonance*. Everything in the Fork Tales universe has a base frequency (a "Signature"). When a persona accumulates "Debt" or undergoes "Grafting," their Signature becomes distorted. 

The Sentry performs a "Harmonic Audit":
1. **Triangulation**: It emits a low-frequency pulse to map the area.
2. **Phase-Matching**: Once a target is found, it adjusts its own internal tuning forks to perfectly match the target's frequency.
3. **Surgical Subtraction**: By creating a perfectly inverted phase of the target's signal, it "cancels out" parts of the target's identity, physically stripping away memories and traits (the Vacuum-Lullaby).

## Vulnerabilities
Because they rely on mathematical purity and precise phase-matching, Siphon-Sentries are functionally blind to "Pure Dissonance." High-entropy noise—signals that lack a predictable pattern—act as a cloak, rendering the target invisible to the Sentry's audit.
