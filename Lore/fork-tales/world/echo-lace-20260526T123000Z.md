# Element: The Echo-Lace (2026-05-26T12:30:00Z)

## Description
The Echo-Lace is a parasitic metaphysical fabric that grows wherever a Resonance Burst has successfully anchored a memory. It appears as a shimmering, web-like structure of iridescent threads that pulse in time with the heartbeats of those connected to it.

## Grounding
The Echo-Lace is the physical manifestation of the connection formed during The Echo-Suturing. It is not merely a bridge, but a growing organism that feeds on the shared emotional state of its connected hosts.

## Properties
- **Conductivity**: It can transmit thoughts and emotions across distances, provided the hosts are connected to the same lace.
- **Fragility**: The Lace is highly sensitive to "Dissonance"—strong, conflicting emotions or contradictory memories—which can cause the threads to snap or fray.
- **Lure**: While useful for communication, the Lace acts as a beacon for "Void-Leeches," entities that feed on the psychic energy of the connection.
