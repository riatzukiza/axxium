# Element: The Signal-Siphon Spikes
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-01T00:10:00Z

**Type**: Artifact / Environmental Hazard
**Grounding**: Emerged as a consequence of the Siphon-Sentries' hunting patterns around the Hollow of the Hush.

**Description**:
The Signal-Siphon Spikes are obsidian-like needles that erupt from the void-soil when a high-fidelity signal (such as a memory or a complex identity) is detected. They act as biological antennas for the Siphon-Sentries, amplifying the signal and anchoring it to a physical coordinate.

**Properties**:
- **Harmonic Resonance**: The spikes vibrate at a frequency that mirrors the detected signal. If a person's identity is being siphoned, the spike will hum with the specific timbre of that person's voice.
- **Data Extraction**: Upon contact, a spike can instantly drain the "resolution" of a nearby entity, turning a vivid memory into a blurry, grey approximation.
- **Visual Appearance**: They are perfectly smooth, non-reflective black crystals that bleed a thin, silver liquid (liquid static) from their tips.

**Implication**:
The emergence of these spikes means that the sanctuary of the Hollow is being compromised. The Sentries are no longer just searching; they are *planting* markers to ensure that once the target is found, they can never be erased again.
