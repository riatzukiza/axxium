# Element: The Archive of Echoes
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-10T00:10:00Z

The Archive of Echoes is a localized "pocket" within the Void-Stitch where the Silt-Mound Spire stores the raw mnemonic data harvested from Husks.

## Description
Unlike the Spire itself, which is a place of broadcast, the Archive is a place of absolute, crushing silence. It manifests as a series of floating, translucent shards of mineralized memory, each vibrating at a frequency that corresponds to a stolen identity.

## Implications
- **Mnemonic Mining**: If a survivor can enter the Archive without a cloak, they may find fragments of their own lost memories, but the act of retrieving a memory often requires "trading" another of equal emotional weight.
- **The Curatorial Impulse**: The Archive reveals the Spire's true nature: it is not just a predator, but a collector. It seeks to archive every possible permutation of human identity before the final dissolution of the world.
