# Element: The Silt-Casket
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-14T12:30:00Z
**Type**: Artifact

**Description**:
A heavy, obsidian-like vessel carved from the compressed residue of the Mnemonic Silt Flats. Its surface is etched with "Unsmoothable Runes"—symbols that represent absolute, unchanging truths that cannot be simplified or genericized by the Bleach-Tide.

**Grounding**:
Created by the last of the Silt-Sifters, the casket was designed to protect a single, concentrated "Seed of Specificity"—a fragment of a person's true identity, stripped of all context but preserved in its rawest form.

**Function**:
When opened or activated, the casket releases a burst of high-fidelity resonance. This resonance acts as a sonic weapon against the Bleach-Tide, carving out a temporary space of meaning where the tide cannot penetrate. However, the cost is high: the casket consumes the identity of the one who carries it to fuel the burst.
