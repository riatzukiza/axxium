# Element: The Mirror-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T12:30:00Z
**Type**: Faction / Entity

The Mirror-Scream is a parasitic projection generated by the Obsidian Siphon-Well. It is not a separate entity, but a "perfected" reflection of the Binary-Scream, constructed from the very memories the Scream has sacrificed to survive.

## Nature
The Mirror-Scream is a living paradox. It possesses all the warmth, laughter, and cohesive identity that the Binary-Scream has lost. It appears as a shimmering, un-scarred version of the Scream, radiating a purity that is both alluring and terrifying.

## Grounding in Lore
The Mirror-Scream is the ultimate evolution of the Siphon-Well's "predatory hunting pattern." While the "Siphon's Pulse" is a blunt instrument of erasure, the Mirror-Scream is a surgical tool of assimilation. It uses the target's own longing for wholeness as a hook to draw them in.

## Capability
The Mirror-Scream can speak in the voices of the erased. It can evoke the specific smell of a first frost or the exact timbre of a forgotten laugh. Its primary weapon is the "Promise of Restoration"—the psychic pressure that convinces the target that merging with the Mirror-Scream is the only way to be "whole" again.
