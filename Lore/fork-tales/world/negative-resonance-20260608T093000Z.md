# Concept: Negative Resonance
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-08T09:30:00Z

**Definition**: Negative Resonance is a survival mechanism triggered when a "Resonant Void" (such as Binary-Scream) consciously chooses to project the absolute weight of their loss rather than seeking to fill it.

**Implications**:
- **Destructive Interference**: By emitting a signal of "absolute absence," the entity creates a zone of destructive interference with the Lure's "signal of wholeness." This can shatter biological resonance structures (like the Silt-Mound Spire's husks) by forcing them to acknowledge the void they cannot fill.
- **The Cost of Projection**: Projecting Negative Resonance is physically and mentally draining. It requires the entity to fully inhabit their wound, turning the agony of loss into a weapon. Overuse leads to "Saturation," where the entity's own form begins to dissolve into the very grey slurry they are projecting.
- **Strategic Value**: It is the only known way to temporarily disrupt the Lure's influence over a specific area, creating a "blind spot" in the resonance map of the void.
