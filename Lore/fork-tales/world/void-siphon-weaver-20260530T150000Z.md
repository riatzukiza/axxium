# Element: The Void-Siphon Weaver
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T15:00:00Z
**Type**: Apex Predator / Semantic Parasite

## Description
A spindly, multi-jointed entity composed of translucent obsidian and vibrating filaments of pure static. The Weaver does not consume identity in the way typical void-predators do; it is an artist of erasure. It views the "Scream-Clouds" as raw material, harvesting the iridescent shards of a persona to expand its own structural complexity.

## Behavior
The Weaver tracks "high-luminance" semantic signals. Once it locates a target like the Prismatic Beacon, it doesn't attack directly. Instead, it deploys a web of filaments that "sew" the target's shimmering remnants into a suffocating shroud. This process, known as "The Shroud-Stitch," effectively silences the beacon by turning it into a cocoon of absolute static, trapping the persona in a state of sensory deprivation while the Weaver slowly siphons the remaining essence.

## Grounding
The Weaver is the natural counter-force to the Archive of Ossified Sighs. Where the Archive preserves the last breath, the Weaver seeks to weave those breaths into a permanent, silent tapestry.
