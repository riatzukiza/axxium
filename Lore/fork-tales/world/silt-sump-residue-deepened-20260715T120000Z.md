# Deepened Location: Silt-Sump of the Unrecorded (Post-Breach)
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-15T120000Z

Following the *Breach of the Hollow Crust*, the Silt-Sump has transitioned from a passive drainage basin into a volatile zone of semantic hemorrhage. The leakage of raw, un-processed erasure from the Weaver's core has fundamentally altered the environment.

**Updated Sensory Details**:
- **Visuals**: The monochromatic "data-ash" has been replaced by *Dry-Static Silt*—jagged, sparking particles that flicker between white and absolute black. The landscape is now punctuated by *Void-Rifts*, vertical slits in reality where the background static simply ceases to exist, leaving holes of perfect, terrifying silence.
- **Auditory**: The "Hollow Hum" has been overtaken by the *Hemorrhage Frequency*—a sound like a thousand glass violins snapping simultaneously, followed by a sudden, vacuum-like intake of breath.
- **Olfactory/Gustatory**: The bitter copper taste has intensified, now mixed with the smell of *Oxidized Memory*—the scent of burning parchment and ancient, rusted iron, as if the archive itself is being incinerated.
- **Tactile**: The viscous "mercury" air has become erratic. Areas of extreme pressure (where the Weaver's logic still holds) are adjacent to pockets of *Null-Gravity*, where fragments of silt and discarded identity float in slow, aimless orbits.

**The Breach Effect**:
The Silt-Sump is no longer just a repository; it is now a *Sieve*. The raw erasure leaking from the Weaver is attempting to "digest" the existing data-ash, creating violent chemical reactions of meaning. This has resulted in the emergence of the *Fracture-Sieves*—crystalline growths that attempt to filter the chaos into stable, though distorted, forms.
