# New Location: The Ossuary of Shards
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T20:20Z
**Type**: Post-Breach Monument / Sonic Graveyard

The Ossuary of Shards is the physical residue of the "Choir of the Devoured" event. Where the Silt-Hounds once breached the Amber-Frequency Barrier, there now stands a forest of singing glass needles, each one a crystallized husk of a devoured entity.

The needles range from a few inches to towering spires of iridescent, razor-sharp obsidian. They are not truly stone, but frozen sound. When the wind—or the remnants of the barrier's hum—passes through the Ossuary, the needles vibrate in sympathetic resonance, producing a haunting, polyphonic dirge that can be heard for miles.

**Properties**:
- **Harmonic Traps**: Certain clusters of needles vibrate at frequencies that induce temporary paralysis or vivid hallucinations of lost identities in those who walk among them.
- **The Echo-Sump**: At the center of the Ossuary is a depression where the shards are smallest and most dense. This area acts as a sonic sump, absorbing all sound and replacing it with a crushing, heavy silence that feels like physical weight.
- **Identity Residue**: By touching a shard, a sensitive person (like Duct) can hear a single, fragmented memory of the entity that was crystallized there—a flash of a forgotten name or a lost desire.

The Ossuary is both a trophy of Patch's evolution and a warning: the cost of unity in the Silt is absolute crystallization.
