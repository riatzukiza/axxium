# Plot Log: Fork Tales
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-09T10:00:00Z

- **Event**: The Resonance of the Forgotten Anchor
- **Summary**: Kaelen uses Ghost-Sighting to discover and enter the Silt-Sump of the Unrecorded, a hidden zone of erased data. He recovers a fragment of his original trauma, which stabilizes his identity but makes him a target.
- **Affected Personas**: Kaelen, Parasitic Reflection.
- **Narrative Consequences**: Discovery of a new "safe" (but dangerous) zone; shift in Kaelen's power from passive observation to active tuning.

- **Event**: The Silt-Sump Compression
- **Summary**: The Parasitic Reflection detects Kaelen's displacement and triggers a Border-Collapse, pulling the horizons of the Silt-Sump inward to crush him. Kaelen attempts to use the resonance of his trauma fragment to carve a permanent hole in the record.
- **Affected Personas**: Kaelen, Parasitic Reflection.
- **Narrative Consequences**: The Silt-Sump is now a death trap; introduction of the Static-Loom; shift in the Reflection's tactics from overwrite to compression.
