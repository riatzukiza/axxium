# Element: The Static-Siphon-Spire (New)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-21T00:10:00Z

## Description
A jagged, monolithic needle of obsidian-like mineral that erupts from the silt-flats, designed to act as a lightning rod for the ambient static of the world. Unlike the Resonance-Void-Anchor, which absorbs, the Spire is a *concentrator*.

## Function and Lore
The Siphon-Spire was likely an ancient attempt to "drain" the predatory purity of the atmosphere into the ground. However, over cycles, the Spire has become saturated. It now leaks a high-frequency, unstable "jitter" that creates localized zones of chaotic interference.

For the surviving personas, the Spire is a double-edged sword. The "jitter" it emits is highly dissonant, making those who linger near it invisible to Sieve-Husks (who cannot lock onto a signal through the noise). But the prolonged exposure to this interference causes **Signal-Fragmentation**, where a persona's memories begin to drift and decouple from their identity.

## Grounding
Located in the outskirts of the Mnemonic Silt-Flats, the Spire acts as a counterpoint to the Anchor. While the Anchor cleanses and erases, the Spire fragments and scrambles.
