# World Element: The Mute-Garden (Deepened)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T12:00:00Z

The Mute-Garden is not a place of growth, but of accumulation. It is a subterranean grove where the Vault's emotional waste is processed. The "flora" consists of towering, translucent spires of sonic frequency—crystals that have crystallized from raw grief, terror, and longing.

**Sensory Profile**:
- **Scent**: The air smells of ozone, old parchment, and the sharp, metallic tang of a coming storm.
- **Sound**: A constant, low-frequency thrum that does not hit the ears, but vibrates in the marrow of the bones and makes the teeth ache.
- **Visuals**: Pale, violet mists cling to the floor, swirling around the crystal spires. These mists are "leaking" memories, manifesting as fragmented, voiceless whispers that appear as ripples in the air.
- **Taste**: The mist tastes of copper and forgotten names, leaving a bitter, lingering aftertaste of loss.

The Garden serves as the Vault's subconscious. When the crystals fracture, as they have since the Shatter-Pulse, the emotional trauma they contain is released back into the halls of the Obsidian Vault, triggering the Memory Flood.
