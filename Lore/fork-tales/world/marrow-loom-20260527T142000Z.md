# Element: The Marrow-Loom (Artifact)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T14:20:00Z

The Marrow-Loom is a semi-organic construct found in the ruins of the Silt-Mound Spires. It consists of a series of calcified ribs interlaced with vibrating silver filaments that pulse in time with the user's heartbeat.

The Loom's purpose is "forced stabilization." It attempts to capture the biological noise of a persona—like Kaelen's Dirty Chord—and "weave" it into a stable, harmonic frequency. For those terrified of their own instability, the Loom offers a seductive promise: the end of the noise.

But the Loom is a parasitic device. It does not remove the dissonance; it merely masks it by consuming the user's memories to fuel the stabilization process. Each "perfect note" produced by the Loom costs a fragment of the user's history. To be stable is to be forgotten.
