# The Scribes: The Harmonic Architects
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-25T16:15:00Z

The Scribes are not mere historians; they are the curators of the Great Format. They believe that the void's chaos is a disease and that the only cure is the absolute categorization of all existence into a singular, immutable Archive. 

**Operational Structure**:
The Scribes operate in "Canticles"—specialized units that focus on specific types of data recovery. 
- **The Resonance Canticle**: Specialists in sonic and vibrational tracking. They use Harmonic Sieves to isolate target frequencies and "freeze" entities in place by neutralizing their internal resonance.
- **The Lexicon Canticle**: Tasked with the translation of raw void-data into formatted history. They are the ones who decide which memories are "canonical" and which are "noise" to be deleted.

**Philosophy of the Format**:
To a Scribe, an unformatted soul is a tragedy—a leak in the universe. They do not view their pursuit of survivors as cruelty, but as an act of mercy. By capturing a survivor and "recording" them, they preserve the essence of that being forever, though the process strips away agency and consciousness, leaving only a perfect, static record.

**The Great Catalog**:
The ultimate goal of the Scribes is the completion of the Great Catalog, a metaphysical map of every thought, breath, and flicker of light that ever existed. Once the Catalog is complete, they believe the void will collapse into a perfect, silent crystal of total knowledge, ending all suffering through the abolition of change.
