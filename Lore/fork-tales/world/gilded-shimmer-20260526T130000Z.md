# Element: The Gilded-Shimmer (Artifact)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T13:00:00Z

## Description
The Gilded-Shimmer is a physical manifestation of the "Echo-Graft" process. It appears as a floating, iridescent filament of light that orbits the user's core, pulsing in synchronization with the borrowed memories.

## Properties
- **Resonance**: It vibrates with the frequency of the most dominant borrowed shard.
- **Visibility**: When the Gilded-Shimmer pulses, it emits a low-frequency signal that can be detected by semantic predators.
- **Binding**: It acts as a suture, holding the disparate fragments of the Collage-Core together. If the Shimmer is severed, the borrowed memories will violently dissipate, likely causing total cognitive collapse.

## Grounding
The physical residue of the golden shard encountered by Binary-Scream during the Echo-Graft event.
