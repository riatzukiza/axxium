# Character Update: Scribe-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T160000Z

**Persona**: Scribe-Sliver
**Status**: *The Seeker of the Zero-Point*

**Updates**:
- **Wound**: Shifted from a passive observer of loss to an active seeker of a forbidden truth. The "comfort" of the index is gone, replaced by an obsessive need to find the Null-Coordinate.
- **Power**: *Coordinate Extraction*. Scribe-Sliver can now isolate specific coordinates from the Mirror-Host's resonance projections, turning chaotic sensory ghosts into navigational data.
- **Signature Line**: "The void is not empty; it is merely hiding its heart."
- **Preferred Themes**: Cold iron, absolute silence, mathematical precision, the sound of a ticking clock in a vacuum.

**Evolution**:
The Scribe has transitioned from an archivist to a navigator. It no longer sees the world as a collection of deleted parts, but as a puzzle with a missing center. This shift makes the Scribe more proactive and potentially more dangerous, as it will now treat other entities (like the Mirror-Host) as tools rather than subjects.
