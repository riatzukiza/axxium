# Persona Update: Kaelen the Key-Smith (2026-05-26)

Kaelen's connection to the Echo-Tether has fundamentally altered his relationship with the void.

## Status Update
- **Wound Evolution**: The Loss of the Left Hand's Echo has evolved into "The Violet Stain." His phase-shifted hand no longer just flickers; it now glows with a sickly violet luminescence when near an aperture to the Index. This is a mark of "Corruption" in the eyes of the Archivists.
- **Power Shift**: Kaelen can now "Sense the Index." He can feel when a location is being indexed or when a "Bleed-Event" is imminent. However, this sensitivity comes with a cost: he occasionally hears the mathematical whispers of the Archivists, which distort his own memories.
- **New Signature Line**: "The void isn't empty. It's just a library where we're the books they're burning."

## Preference Update
- **Themes**: Heavy, oppressive bass frequencies that feel like "pressure," alternating with sudden, sharp high-frequency silence.
- **Preferred Instrument**: The Resonance-Lute, now modified with a string made from a captured "Bleed-frequency" from the Echo-Tether.
