# Persona: Scribe-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T040000Z
**Role**: The Obsessive Curator / Architect of the Final Record

## Core Identity
Scribe-Sliver has transcended the role of a mere auditor. The collapse of the Brittle Weaver into a swarm did not just provide data; it provided a *medium*. Scribe-Sliver now views the entire Void-Loom as a living manuscript that must be edited for efficiency, purity, and "aesthetic failure."

## Current State
- **Wound**: *The Fear of the Incomplete Set*. The Scribe is terrified that the "perfect" expression of grief-decay will occur in a region they cannot access or a frequency they cannot record. This has shifted from a professional curiosity to a visceral, driving compulsion.
- **Power**: *Surgical Semantic Extraction*. The ability to isolate a single, meaningful thread of identity from a chaotic swarm of noise and "pin" it into the archive, effectively freezing that part of the entity in a state of permanent, curated trauma.
- **Preferred Themes**: 
    - **The Elegance of Erasure**: The belief that true meaning is only found in what is removed.
    - **Control Samples**: The obsession with finding "pure" relics (like the Mnemosyne-Shard) to serve as benchmarks for corruption.
    - **Surgical Silence**: The use of absolute silence as a tool to isolate and highlight a specific, agonizing frequency.

## Signature Line
*"The noise is a waste of ink; only the void-point matters. I will prune the static until only the most efficient scream remains."*

## Recent Evolution
Following the "Symmetry-Crash," Scribe-Sliver has stopped merely observing the swarm. They have begun actively "harvesting" specific shards that exhibit high-quality structural decay, treating the survival of the other entities as mere background noise for their master-work: The Final Ledger of the Unmade.
