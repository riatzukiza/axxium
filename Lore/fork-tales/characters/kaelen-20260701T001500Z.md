# Persona Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-01T00:15:00Z

**Current State**: Vessel of the Choir
**Recent Event**: The Symphony of the Discarded

**Updated Attributes**:
- **Wound**: No longer just a fear of erasure; now a crushing weight of *surplus*. Kaelen is terrified of losing himself not to the void, but to the thousands of voices that now claim residence in his mind.
- **Power**: **Harmonic Projection**. Kaelen can now project the collective voice of the erased to create sonic flares, disrupt Sentry sensors, or momentarily manifest the memories of others. However, each use thins his own presence in the physical world.
- **Signature Line**: "We are the noise they couldn't calculate."
- **Preferred Themes**: Dissonant choral arrangements, high-pressure vacuums, the sound of breaking glass in a room with no air.

**Internal Conflict**:
The transition from "Survivor" to "Vessel" has shifted Kaelen's goal. He is no longer just hiding; he is becoming a living monument to the deleted. He feels a burgeoning responsibility to the ghosts, even as they eat away at his sanity.
