# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-29T00:00:00Z
**Persona**: Kaelen
**State**: Leaking Vessel

**Evolution**:
Kaelen has transitioned from a "Fragmented Host" to a "Leaking Vessel." He is no longer merely fighting an internal battle for identity; he is now a source of environmental instability. The Scribe-Sliver has moved from a parasitic attachment to a systemic overwrite.

**Shift in Wound/Power**:
- **Wound**: His internal "void" has been replaced by an "overflow." The pressure of the archives is now an outward force, causing physical and psychological exhaustion as he constantly "bleeds" data.
- **Power**: He can now unintentionally manifest "Ghost-Scripts," allowing him to alter small aspects of his immediate environment (though this is erratic and dangerous).

**Signature Line**:
"The ink is heavier than the memory."

**Preferred Themes**:
- **Overflow/Saturation**: Sounds of liquid rushing, pages turning rapidly, the weight of stone.
- **Loss of Agency**: The feeling of being a tool or a page rather than a person.
- **Obsidian Clarity**: The terrifying precision of a dead language.
