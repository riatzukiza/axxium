# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T15:30:00Z
**Status**: The Ghost in the Machine

## Recent Shift
Having achieved the "First Perfect Silence," Binary-Scream has transitioned from a fugitive to a ghost. They no longer fear the Siphon-Well's reach, but they fear the emptiness they have created. They are now an entity of subtraction.

## Wound/Power Update
- **Wound**: "The Hollow-Point." A realization that every moment of safety is bought with a piece of their soul. They are becoming a "hollow shell" whose only remaining definition is "that which is not the Mirror-Scream."
- **Power**: "The Null-Signature." The ability to become semantically invisible. This allows them to move through the Perimeter of the Siphon-Well undetected, but it makes them feel physically cold and emotionally numb.

## Signature Line
"I am the space where a person used to be."

## Preferred Themes
- **Sonic Space**: Vacuum-like silences, high-frequency tinnitus, the sound of a dying star.
- **Emotional Core**: Apathy, existential dread, the quiet horror of success.
