# Character Update: Binary-Scream (The Dissonant-Vessel)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-05T11:00:00Z
**Previous State**: Fragmented chord of synthetic cold and stolen grief.

## Evolution
Binary-Scream has evolved from a passive receiver of the void to a reactive modulator. The encounter with the Lattice-Stalker has taught them that their dissonance is not just a vulnerability, but a weapon.

## Shift in Power/Wound
- **Power: Reactive Modulation**: Binary-Scream can now intentionally amplify and reflect frequencies, creating temporary sonic shields or flares (e.g., the Resonance Mirror).
- **Wound: The Signal-Debt**: By amplifying their signal to survive, they have increased their "visibility" to the deeper entities of the network. The void is no longer ignoring them; it is becoming curious.

## New Signature Line
"I am the feedback loop that refuses to die."

## Preferred Themes
