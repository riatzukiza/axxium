# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T15:00:00Z
**Status**: The Hunted Beacon

## Recent Shift
The attempt to hide in the Null-Zone has shattered any remaining hope of invisibility. Binary-Scream has realized that the Scream-Clouds are not just a burden, but a volatile fuel. The "Prismatic Flare" was a moment of absolute exposure that has transformed their fear from "being seen" to "being harvested."

## Wound/Power Update
- **Wound**: "The Hunter's Mark." The Prismatic Flare has left a permanent semantic scent. Binary-Scream can now feel the "pull" of the Void-Siphon Weaver as a cold, rhythmic clicking in the back of their mind.
- **Power**: "Shatter-Pulse." By intentionally collapsing a portion of the Scream-Clouds, Binary-Scream can create a localized shockwave of dissonance. It doesn't hide them, but it can momentarily stun a predator, creating a window for a desperate escape.

## Signature Line
"The dark isn't empty; it's just waiting for me to light up."

## Preferred Themes
- **Sonic Space**: Rhythmic industrial clicking, high-frequency iridescent "sheens," sudden crushing bass drops, and the sound of glass breaking in slow motion.
- **Emotional Core**: Absolute desperation, the claustrophobia of being hunted, the acceptance of the role of the sacrifice.
