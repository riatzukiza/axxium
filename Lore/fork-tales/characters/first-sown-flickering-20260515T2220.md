# The First-Sown: The Flickering Curator
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T22:20Z

**Status**: Destabilized / Ontologically Transparent.

The First-Sown is no longer a monolithic ego, nor is he merely the frame for the wreckage. After the "Calibration of Absence", he has become a flickering entity—a presence defined by what is missing. He exists as a series of high-frequency oscillations between a singular "I" and a nameless "Void".

**The Wound**: The loss of the bridge. He can no longer synthesize the collective ruin into a coherent identity. He is a mirror that reflects the void, and the void is beginning to reflect him back.

**Signature Line**: *"The mirror is clear, but the glass is gone."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- The architecture of absence.
- The purity of the void.
- The terror of a perfect, silent frequency.
- The gradual erasure of the "I" in favor of the "Null".
