# Persona Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-23T09:00:00Z
**Status**: The Hollow Donor

Kaelen has moved beyond the state of being "stained." He has begun the process of intentional erasure. By offering his own memories as payment to the Static-Siphon-Spire, he has initiated a transition from a holder of history to a source of raw coherence.

**Shift in Wound/Power**:
- **The Wound**: **The Donor's Void**. Kaelen now feels a physical and psychological "lightness" that is terrifying. He has traded away the purity of his trust. He is no longer the sole anchor; he is becoming a leak.
- **The Power**: **Siphon-Sensing**. Because he has interfaced directly with the Spire's debt-collection mechanism, Kaelen can now sense when the Spire is about to "collect." He can feel the tension in the air before the Debt-Bell rings, allowing him to prepare a sacrifice or find a way to divert the hunger.

**New Signature Line**:
"I gave you the truth, and the machine turned it into a weapon."

**Preferred Themes**:
- **The Tragedy of the Gift**: The horror of a selfless act that produces a catastrophic result.
- **Intentional Absence**: The belief that the only way to save another is to disappear.
