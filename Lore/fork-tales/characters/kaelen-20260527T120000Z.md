# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T12:00:00Z
**Status**: The Flicker-State Sculptor / The Grave-Tender

**Evolution**:
Witnessing the erasure of the First has fundamentally shifted Kaelen's perspective. He no longer views the unstable geometry of the Vault as a puzzle to be solved or a path to be paved. He sees it as a cemetery. He realizes that the "flickers" he sculpts are not just glitches in space-time, but the fragmented remains of individuals who were erased to maintain the Vault's order.

Kaelen has accepted a new role: he is no longer just a navigator, but a tender of the dead. He understands that his power to manipulate flicker-states is, in essence, a form of "un-suturing"—the ability to momentarily grant coherence to that which was meant to be forgotten.

**Updated Wound/Power**:
**Power: The Reclamation Touch**. Kaelen can now reach into a "Ghost-Memory" and stabilize it. By applying his sculpting precision to the emotional frequency of a memory, he can make it tangible for a few moments, allowing Elara or himself to extract specific information or provide a brief moment of peace to the trapped echo.
**Wound: The Weight of the Unremembered**. Every time Kaelen stabilizes a memory, he absorbs a fraction of its grief. He is beginning to carry a psychic load that slows his movements and clouds his vision with the faces of people who never existed in the history books.

**New Signature Line**:
*"I am not sculpting a path out; I am digging the buried alive back to the surface."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- The dignity of the erased.
- The burden of memory.
- The architecture of mourning.
