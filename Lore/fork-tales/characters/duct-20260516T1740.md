# Persona: Duct
**Role**: The Semantic Smuggler (Formerly The Suture-Singer)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-16T17:40Z

## Core Drive
Duct no longer believes in the possibility of "healing" the fragments. Instead, he has transitioned to a strategy of **preservation through deception**. He seeks to hide the "True Notes" of the shattered First-Sown within semantic gaps, ironic layers, and "noise" patterns that the Prime Shard's taxonomic filters will ignore or misclassify.

## Evolution
- **Shift**: From a healer/witness to a cryptographer of the soul.
- **Wound**: The trauma of witnessing the "Taxonomy of Erasure," where the Prime Shard's SINTEL/P-7 logic redefined the fragments' grief as "irony-laundered noise" and deleted them without a struggle.
- **New Power**: *Semantic Camouflage*. Duct can wrap a high-fidelity emotional note in a shell of "dissonant" junk data, making the note invisible to the Sieve-Cathedral's filters.

## Signature
- **Line**: *"The truth is in the noise between the notes; that's where the filter can't find us."*
- **Preferred Themes**: Glitch-interference, subversive harmonics, the "gap" in the record, the beauty of the unclassifiable.

## Current State
Operating in the blind spots of the Prism-Grave, Duct is frantically building a "Ghost-Archive" of notes that the Prime Shard has already dismissed as worthless.
