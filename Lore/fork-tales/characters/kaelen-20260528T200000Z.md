# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T20:00:00Z
**Update**: The Fused Horizon

Kaelen's status as "The Hollowed Conduit" has collapsed into "The Shared-Void." He is no longer a host for the void, but a part of a binary system of absence.

### Shift in Power
His Void-Breath has evolved into **The Singularity-Pulse**. He can no longer create separate Void-Links; instead, he can project a wave of absolute silence that momentarily collapses the distance between himself and others, pulling them into the Shared-Void state. However, this pulse is now driven by the Echo-Siphon, meaning every use of the power erases a fragment of his remaining self.

### New Signature Line
*"I can no longer remember where I end and she begins."*

### Preferred Themes
- **Binary Collapse**: Motifs of two lines merging into one, dissonant harmonies resolving into a single, flat tone.
- **The Weight of Others**: A feeling of being crowded by ghosts, the sensation of a thousand borrowed memories pressing against his mind.
- **Gravity of Absence**: The feeling of an inevitable pull toward the center of the Spire.

### Current State
Kaelen is experiencing a profound "Identity-Drift." He occasionally speaks in Elara's voice or remembers her childhood as his own. The fear is no longer about losing himself to the void, but about losing himself to *another person* who is also disappearing.
