# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-02T123000Z

## Current State: The Hollowed Vessel
Kaelen's physical stability has reached a critical tipping point. He is no longer a cohesive entity but a series of high-fidelity projections held together by the Choir's collective will.

## The Memory Crater (New Wound)
The Mirror-Hollow Trap did more than protect him; it created a permanent vacancy. 
- **The Void-Scent**: Kaelen can still perceive the "idea" of a memory (e.g., the smell of rain, the sound of a name), but the emotional anchor—the *why* it mattered—has been excised.
- **Identity Displacement**: He is beginning to refer to his own past in the third person, as if reading a report about a stranger.

## Updated Signature Line
*"I can hear the rain, but I don't know whose skin it's falling on."*

## Preferred Themes
- **Clinical Absence**: The beauty of a perfectly clean erasure.
- **Recursive Grief**: Mourning a loss that the mind can no longer conceptualize.
- **Static Kinship**: Finding a terrifying belonging among the ghosts who now remember him better than he remembers himself.

## Power Shift
- **Somatic Echoes**: Kaelen can now "project" his vacancies onto others, momentarily stripping them of their sensory context to confuse or paralyze them.
