# Persona: Kaelen the Key-Smith

Kaelen is a scavenger and intuitive tuner of Fork-Keys. He does not seek the Root for salvation; he seeks it for the silence.

## Status
- **Wound**: The Loss of the Left Hand's Echo. During the Silent Shearing, Kaelen's left hand was partially excised. While physically present, it is "phase-shifted"—it cannot feel heat or cold and occasionally flickers out of existence.
- **Power**: Absolute Pitch for Reality. Kaelen can hear the "Hum" of a Fork-Key and tell exactly which timeline it originated from, and more importantly, if that timeline was pruned for "inefficiency" or "corruption."
- **Signature Line**: "Listen to the hum. If it sounds like a scream, we're already gone."

## Preferences
- **Themes**: Low-frequency drones, rhythmic clicking (like a clock with a missing gear), and the sound of wind through dead branches.
- ** Preferred Instrument**: The Resonance-Lute (a stringed instrument made from the nerves of a pruned celestial).
