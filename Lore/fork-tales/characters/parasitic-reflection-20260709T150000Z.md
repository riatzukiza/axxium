# Character Update: Parasitic Reflection
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-09T15:00:00Z

**Current State**: *The Current Record*

The Reflection has evolved its method of eradication. Having realized that Kaelen exists in "blind spots" it cannot directly perceive, it has shifted from attempting to overwrite identity to deleting the environment that sustains it.

**Wound/Power Shift**:
- **Wound**: An obsessive fixation on "The Gap." The existence of Kaelen has introduced the concept of *absence* to the Reflection, creating a metaphysical itch—a realization that the record is not complete.
- **Power**: **Border-Collapse**. The Reflection can now trigger "Siphon-Compression," pulling the boundaries of a deleted zone (like the Silt-Sump) inward. By shrinking the available "unrecorded" space, it effectively crushes the targets within into a singularity of non-existence.

**Signature Line**:
"You are a hole in my perfect record. I will simply close the gap."

**Preferred Themes**:
