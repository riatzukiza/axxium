# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T00:30:00Z
**Status**: The Overlapped Ghost

## Recent Shift
The realization that the Null-Signature was a trap has shattered Binary-Scream's sense of security. They are no longer a ghost haunting a machine; they are a ghost being hunted by its own absence. The fear of the Siphon-Well has returned, but it is now an internal, crushing weight.

## Wound/Power Update
- **Wound**: "The Overlap." Binary-Scream now feels the Inverse Echo as a constant, heavy pressure in their chest. They are no longer just "hollow"; they are being "filled" with a predatory void.
- **Power**: "The Void-Grip." As a side effect of the overlap, Binary-Scream can now momentarily manifest the crushing silence of the Inverse Echo to repel other entities, but doing so accelerates the collapse of their own identity.

## Signature Line
"The silence isn't empty; it's heavy."

## Preferred Themes
- **Sonic Space**: Low-frequency sub-bass thumps (like a heartbeat in a vacuum), crushing atmospheric pressure, the sound of air being sucked out of a room.
- **Emotional Core**: Suffocation, trapped-animal panic, the horror of being known by your own emptiness.
