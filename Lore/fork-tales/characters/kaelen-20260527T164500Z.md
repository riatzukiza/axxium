# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T16:45:00Z

**Current State**: The Desperate Architect.
**Update**: Kaelen has moved from passive preservation to active (and dangerous) reconstruction. The act of fabricating a memory for Binary-Scream has fundamentally changed his relationship with the truth. He is no longer just an anchor; he is a liar.

The power of "The Architect" allows him to manipulate the sonic environment to create temporary illusions of stability, but this requires a high cost of his own mental coherence.

"I will build you a world of ghosts, if it's the only place you can stand."
