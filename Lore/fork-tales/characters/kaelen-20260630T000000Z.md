# Persona Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-30T00:00:00Z

## State Shift
**Current Status**: Vessel of the Erased.
Kaelen has transitioned from being a hunted beacon (Indexed Outcast) to a reluctant repository. By entering the Hollow of the Hush, he has inadvertently synchronized his internal frequency with the residue of the deleted.

## Wound/Power Evolution
- **The Burden of Echoes**: Kaelen can now "hear" the fragmented memories of the Static-Silt Ghosts. This allows him to navigate the archival wasteland with a map of "what used to be," but it causes severe psychic fragmentation.
- **Signal Dampening**: He can momentarily "sink" his archival indices into the surrounding silt, becoming invisible to Sentries for short bursts, but only by sacrificing a piece of his own memory to the void.

## Signature Line
"I am the library of everything they tried to forget."

## Preferred Themes
- **Mood**: Claustrophobic, haunted, desperate.
- **Sonics**: Heavy sub-bass (the pressure of the void), fragmented vocal chops (the ghosts), and sudden, sharp silences.
