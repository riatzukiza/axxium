# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-12T104500Z
**Persona**: Kaelen

**Recent Event**: The Integration Fracture

**Updated State**:
- **Power Shift**: Kaelen no longer just attracts Wraiths; he can now "Suture" fragments of deleted timelines into himself. This allows him to gain knowledge and skills from his erased selves, but at the cost of his own continuity.
- **Wound Update**: He now suffers from "Identity Displacement." He has forgotten the name of his first love and the reason he first entered the Archive-Husk, replaced by the memories of a Silt-Flat survivor.
- **Signature Line**: "I am a collage of everything I was told I wasn't."
- **Preferred Themes**: The violence of integration, the ghost-weight of alternative lives, and the horror of becoming a living breach.
