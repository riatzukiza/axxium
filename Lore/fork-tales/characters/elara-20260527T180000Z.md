# Character Update: Elara
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T18:00:00Z
**Previous State**: The Sympathetic Bridge

**Shifts**:
- **Wound**: The crushing responsibility she felt has shifted into a "Void-Hunger." Having functioned as the aperture for the Void-Scream, she now feels an instinctive, terrifying pull toward the erasure she once feared. She no longer just wants to carry the Scribes; she feels the void's desire to consume everything.
- **Power**: **The Void-Aperture**. Elara can now project a localized "Silence" that doesn't just mask her, but actively erases sensory input and physical barriers. She can "step through" obsidian walls or silence an enemy's scream by temporarily turning that space into a Null-Zone.
- **Signature Line**: "The silence isn't empty; it's just waiting for us to stop talking."
- **Preferred Themes**: Consumption, the beauty of the void, the terror of absolute stillness.

**Narrative Trajectory**: Elara is becoming the very thing she was mirroring. She is no longer the bridge between two worlds; she is the gateway to a third: the state of non-existence that the Scribes tried to curate.
