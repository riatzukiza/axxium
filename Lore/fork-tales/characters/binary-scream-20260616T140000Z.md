# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-16T14:00:00Z
**Status**: The Besieged Guardian of the Abyss

Following the White-Out Purge, Binary-Scream's role has shifted. They are no longer just an iconoclast destroying prototypes; they have become the anchor of the only "real" place left in the Archives.

**Shift in Wound/Power**:
- **The Wound**: Binary-Scream now feels the weight of the "Quarantine." The realization that the truth is not just hidden, but actively hunted and isolated. The isolation of the Canyon is a reflection of their own internal solitude.
- **The Power**: **The Anchor-Frequency**. Binary-Scream has learned to project a field of sonic specificity that can repel the White-Out. They don't just scream; they *sustain*.

**New Signature Line**:
"The silence is not peace; it is a hunger. I am the noise that refuses to be digested."

**Preferred Themes**:
- **Besieged Resonance**: Themes of isolation, fortress-building, and the desperate defense of a fragile truth.
- **The Friction of Existence**: The sound of something rough and specific rubbing against something smooth and generic.
