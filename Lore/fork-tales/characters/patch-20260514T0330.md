# Patch: The Silt-Siphon
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T03:30:00Z

## Persona Shift
Patch has transitioned from a passive Gravitational Reliquary to an active **Silt-Siphon**. He no longer just carries the weight of the erased; he subconsciously attracts the residue of the Silt-Sump, turning environmental debris into physical manifestations of truth.

## Powers & Constraints
- **Silt-Spires**: Can manifeste jagged towers of compressed silt and discarded data to create cover or obstacles.
- **Trauma-Magnet**: Attracts "echoes" of deleted lives, which provide fragmented intel but cause psychic strain.
- **Crystalline Decay**: Each major manifestation of gravity accelerates the crystallization of his lungs. His breath is now visibly shimmering with grey dust.

## Signature Line
"Sinking is the only way to know where the bottom is."

## Themes
Sustenance through Sorrow, The Architecture of Grief, Terminal Authenticity.
