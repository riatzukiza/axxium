# Character Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-22T12:45:00Z

**Current State**: The Fading Echo.
**Update**: Binary-Scream has suffered the first significant breach of their internal continuity. Due to the influence of the Meaning-Sieve within the Static-Siphon-Spire, they have lost the memory of the "First Resonance"—the foundational bond with Kaelen.

**Shift in Wound/Power**: 
Their "wound" has shifted from a fear of silence to a fear of *absence*. They no longer just fear the predatory void; they fear the holes appearing in their own history. Their power, however, has evolved: they can now "mimic" the Void-Jitter of the Spire, creating brief pockets of instability that can confuse other signal-sensitive entities, though this acceleration of their own erasure.

**New Signature Line**: 
"I remember the shape of the hole, but I cannot remember what filled it."

**Preferred Themes**: 
- **Texture**: Granular, fraying, intermittent.
- **Mood**: Distant, confused, longing for a ghost of a feeling.
- **Sonic Space**: A room where the walls are moving away from you.
