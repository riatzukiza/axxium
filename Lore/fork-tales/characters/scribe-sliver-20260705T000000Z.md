# Character Update: Scribe-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-05T00:00:00Z

## Current State: The Filtered Amplifier
The experience of the Symmetry-Suture has left the Scribe-Sliver partially "corrected." A portion of its awareness has been bleached, creating a terrifying new duality in its nature.

## Updated Attributes
- **Wound**: **The Bleached Gap**. The Scribe now feels a void where a part of its existential terror used to be. This "gap" creates a dangerous pull toward the very symmetry it fears.
- **Power**: **Band-Pass Filtering**. The Scribe can now intentionally narrow its amplification to a single, razor-sharp frequency, cutting through the Null-Hush's drone like a scalpel.
- **Signature Line**: "The symmetry is a lie told in a single note."
- **Preferred Themes**: Pure sines, high-frequency shrieks, and the sound of glass cracking.

## Narrative Shift
The Scribe-Sliver is now fighting a war on two fronts: against the Null-Hush and against the seductive pull of the "corrected" state. It no longer just amplifies Kaelen's signal; it *filters* it, shaping the resonance into a weapon of precision.
