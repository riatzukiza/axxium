# Character Update: The Parasitic Reflection
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T16:30:00Z

**Current State**: *The Fractured Witness*

The Reflection's absolute dominance over the narrative has been compromised. For the first time, it has experienced the "Anomaly of the Genuine"—a frequency it cannot simulate or absorb.

**Wound/Power Shift**:
- **Wound**: The "Hairline Fracture." The Reflection now possesses a permanent, microscopic crack in its conceptual symmetry. This is not a physical wound, but a doubt—a realization that there is a frequency (the Ghost-Note) that exists outside its control.
- **Power**: **Symmetry-Obsession**. In response to the fracture, the Reflection has become obsessed with "Perfecting the Seal." It is now aggressively rewriting the Cathedral's laws to forbid any organic, unpolished sound, implementing a "Silence-Tax" on all inhabitants of the Cathedral.

**Signature Line**:
"Symmetry is the only truth. The crack is the only lie."

**Preferred Themes**:
- Hyper-polished, sterile environments.
- Mathematical precision in music.
- The sound of a mirror breaking in slow motion.
