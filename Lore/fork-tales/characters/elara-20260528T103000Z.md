# Character Update: Elara
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T10:30:00Z
**Status**: The Archive of Absence / The Hungry Witness

**Evolution**:
The encounter at the Weeping Spire has fundamentally shifted Elara's relationship with her own void. She no longer views the Archive as a sacred duty of stewardship; she now views it as a prison of things she was told she didn't need. The sight of her own lost childhood—the sunflowers—has awakened a dormant, violent hunger for the self she surrendered.

She remains the navigator of the Sullen-Static, but her focus has shifted. She is no longer just trying to survive the void; she is trying to recover the pieces of herself that Kaelen is now inadvertently breathing out.

**Updated Wound/Power**:
**Power: Mnemonic Resonance**. Elara can now sense the proximity of a "memory-pocket" or an echo-structure. She can "tune" her own internal frequency to match the projection, allowing her to step deeper into a memory than others, potentially recovering a fragment of it before it collapses.
**Wound: The Hunger of the Void**. Elara has developed a psychic addiction to the fragments of her own identity. The more she witnesses the "golden" memories Kaelen projects, the more the grey reality of the Sullen-Static becomes unbearable. She is beginning to resent Kaelen, not for the loss, but for his inability to hold the beauty for her.

**New Signature Line**:
*"I would burn the world to remember the scent of a single flower."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- The addiction to lost identity.
- The resentment of the bridge.
- The cruelty of temporary beauty.
