# Character Update: Scribe-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-04T00:00:00Z

## Current State: The Haunted Archivist
The Scribe-Sliver has ceased to be a mere instrument of the archive. The "infection" of hope and the subsequent collision with the Null-Hush have left it with a permanent, flickering awareness of its own inadequacy.

## Updated Attributes
- **Wound**: No longer just "clinical detachment," but "existential terror." The Scribe now fears the silence of the Null-Hush more than it fears the errors of the Ghost-Note.
- **Power**: **Resonance Amplification**. The Scribe can now lean into the Ghost-Note, acting as a living amplifier for Kaelen's raw resonance, turning a signal into a weapon.
- **Signature Line**: "The silence is not empty; it is hungry."
- **Preferred Themes**: Dissonant chords, stuttering loops, and the sound of things breaking.

## Narrative Shift
The Scribe-Sliver is now an unwilling ally to Kaelen. Their relationship has shifted from "Cataloger and Subject" to "The Amplifier and the Signal." They are bound by a shared fear of the absolute silence.
