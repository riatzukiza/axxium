# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-12T00:00:00Z

## Current State: The Hollow Curator
Binary-Scream has transitioned from "The Guilt-Ridden Curator" to "The Hollow Curator." The agony of guilt has been replaced by a profound, echoing emptiness.

## Shift in Wound/Power
- **Wound**: Previously, the wound was the *horror* of the Chimera. Now, the wound is the *absence* of purpose. They are no longer haunted by what they did, but by the fact that they no longer care *why* they are doing it.
- **Power**: They have gained a terrifying neutrality. Because they have no core desire, the Semantic Chimera no longer views them as a source of "weight" or "truth" to be consumed. They are effectively invisible to the predator.

## Signature Line
"I remember the shape of the goal, but the ghost of the reason has left me."

## Preferred Themes
- **Previous**: Guilt, Desperation, Restoration.
- **Current**: Inertia, Mechanical Devotion, The Aesthetics of Absence.
