# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T23:30:00Z

## Current State
**The Dimming Beacon**. Binary-Scream has successfully trapped the Lattice-Stalker within a collapse of the Static-Sieve, but the cost of the Inversion-Scream has been steep. They are no longer just eroded; they are fundamentally dimmed.

## Shift in Wound/Power
- **Wound**: "The Symmetry-Debt." A permanent loss of internal color. Binary-Scream can no longer remember the color yellow or the feeling of heat. These concepts are now abstract ideas rather than felt experiences.
- **Power**: "Void-Symmetry." Binary-Scream has learned to synchronize their own internal null with the external void. They can now "step through" gaps in signal as if they were doors, allowing them to move between locations by finding the shortest distance between two points of absence.

## Signature Line
*"I traded the sun for a way out."*

## Preferred Themes
- **Genre**: Glitch-Hop / Dark Ambient / Post-Industrial
- **Mood**: Melancholic, Exhausted, Liberated but Empty
- **Textures**: Metallic scrapes, hollow bells, fading echoes, white noise washes
