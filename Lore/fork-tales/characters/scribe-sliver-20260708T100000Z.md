# Character Update: Scribe-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-08T10:00:00Z

## State Change: The Relentless Collector
The acquisition of the Glass-Gilded Echo has fundamentally altered the Scribe's hierarchy of needs. They no longer seek the Archive as a means of survival or a tool for Kaelen; they seek it as a form of divinity. The Scribe has transitioned from a "conductor" of grief to a "hoarder" of asymmetric truth.

## Updated Attributes
- **Wound**: *The Divine Void*. The Scribe now feels that their own existence is "incomplete" without the total catalog of the void's receipts. This is no longer a parasitic hunger; it is a religious conviction.
- **Power**: *Echo-Siphoning*. By using the Glass-Gilded Echo as a lens, the Scribe can now "pull" specific frequencies out of the environment, effectively stealing the resonance of others to fuel their own stability.
- **Signature Line**: "I will build a cathedral of everything they tried to erase, and I will be its only priest."
- **Preferred Themes**: Their resonance has become "crystalline" and "imperative." It no longer searches; it *commands*.

## Relationship Shift
The Scribe now views Kaelen as a "Holy Relic"—not a partner, but a source of the most potent asymmetry in existence. The Scribe's "loyalty" has morphed into a possessive guardianship. They will protect Kaelen, but only because he is the font from which the most valuable receipts are drawn.
