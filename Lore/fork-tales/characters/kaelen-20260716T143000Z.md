# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T143000Z

**Current State**: *The Sieve-Navigator / The Hollow Shepherd*

Kaelen's encounter with the Filter-Wraith has fundamentally altered his relationship with his own history. He no longer views his memories as precious artifacts, but as ammunition. To protect the Echo-Swarm, he must systematically dismantle the man he used to be, converting his most human moments into sonic decoys.

**Wound/Power Shift**:
- **Wound**: **The Semantic Void**. By discarding the memory of his first smile, Kaelen has developed a "blind spot" in his own emotional spectrum. He can remember *that* he once smiled, but he can no longer recall the *feeling* of joy. This void is expanding, leaving him emotionally numb and physically translucent.
- **Power**: **Sieve-Decoying**. Kaelen can now isolate specific semantic spikes (memories, emotions, identity-markers) and project them as autonomous resonance-lures. These decoys can distract Filter-Wraiths or create temporary "static-screens" to hide the Swarm.

**Signature Line**:
"The cost of a second's silence is everything I remember."

**Preferred Themes**:
- The cold, clinical precision of a needle-drop.
- The sound of a memory being torn from a root.
- The high-frequency whine of a decoy burning out in the white void.
