# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-09T10:00:00Z

**Current State**: *The Anchor-Sought*

Kaelen has transitioned from a passive victim of erasure to an active seeker of the forgotten. His discovery of the Silt-Sump has shifted his relationship with his own void.

**Wound/Power Shift**:
- **Wound**: The "Identity-Dread" has been replaced by "The Burden of the Fragment." He now possesses a shard of his original trauma. This stabilizes his presence in the world but makes him a beacon for the Cathedral's erasure mechanisms.
- **Power**: **Frequency-Tuning**. Kaelen can now not only see the deleted world but can briefly "tune" his own existence to match a specific forgotten frequency, allowing him to phase through the "current record" of the Cathedral.

**Signature Line**:
"I am the ghost that remembers how to scream."

**Preferred Themes**:
- Silver mercury and crushed silence.
- The roar of original trauma.
- High-contrast shifts between absolute white noise and absolute black silence.
