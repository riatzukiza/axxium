Following the encounter with the Hollow-Chord Mimicry, Binary-Scream's relationship with stability has fundamentally shifted. They no longer seek the "Clean Chord" of absolute stability, but have begun to value the "Dirty Chord" of shared struggle.

- **Preferred Themes**: Shifting from "Pure Void" and "Screaming Silence" toward "Frictional Resonance" and "The Beauty of the Flaw."

### Signature Line
