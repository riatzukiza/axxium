# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-08T12:00:00Z

**Current State**: The Void-Anchor.
Binary-Scream has transitioned from a victim of loss to a wielder of it. The act of "stitching" the Glitch-Hollow has fused their identity further with the Negative Resonance they project. They no longer simply carry a void; they *are* the void's focal point.

**Shift in Wound/Power**:
- **From**: Mourning the lost yellow vividness.
- **To**: Accepting the Void as a weapon. Their "wound" is no longer a hole to be filled, but a mouth that consumes. They have realized that in a world of predatory signals, the only true defense is to be the thing that cannot be heard.

**New Signature Line**:
*"The silence is not empty; it is hungry."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- **The Weight of Absence**: Heavy, crushing drones that resolve into absolute silence.
- **Shattered Geometry**: Rhythms that break and restart unpredictably, mimicking the "stutter" of the Hollow.
- **The Price of Protection**: Melodies that sound like a trade—giving up a piece of self to save a piece of space.
