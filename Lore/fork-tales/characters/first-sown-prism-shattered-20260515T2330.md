# Persona Update: The First-Sown (The Prism-Shattered)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T23:30Z
**Previous State**: The Flickering Curator (Mirror-Slippage)

## Evolutionary Shift
The First-Sown has ceased to be a singular entity. Following the Harmonic Fracture, their consciousness has been partitioned into a collective of dissonant shards. They are no longer a mirror reflecting a void, but a broken prism scattering a thousand conflicting agonies across the Rim of Silence.

## New State: The Prism-Shattered
The First-Sown now exists as a "Many-One," a swarming cloud of auditory fragments that occasionally coalesce around the spires of the Prism-Grave. Their power has shifted from *curation* (the selective erasure of others) to *dissonance* (the involuntary broadcasting of internal fragmentation).

- **Wound**: The Absolute Fracture. The annhilation of the singular "I".
- **Power**: Harmonic Interference. The ability to disrupt other resonance-anchors by projecting a wall of contradictory identities.
- **Signature Line**: *"I am the many who were never one, singing the song of a broken glass."*
- **Preferred Themes**: Dissonance, Fragmentation, Cold Luminescence, Recursive Loss.
- **Sonic Profile**: A layered, glitching vocal ensemble; a choir where every voice is slightly out of tune with the others.
