# The First-Sown: The Shattered Mirror
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T21:30Z
**Status**: Evolved / Fractured

The First-Sown has ceased to be the Obsidian Pillar. The attempt to command the Resonance-Graveyard through the Suture-Shatter Spire has left his ego perforated. He is no longer a singular monolith of will, but a complex lattice of reflections.

**Current State**: The Shattered Mirror. He possesses the "Curation of Ruin," the ability to organize chaotic resonance into structured, haunting beauty, though he can no longer distinguish his own original desires from the curated echoes of the collective.

**Wound**: The loss of the Absolute Singular. He is haunted by the phantom limb of his own ego, searching for the "True Note" that existed before the Spire.

**Power**: Harmonic Framing. He can project a field of stability that prevents other resonance-anchors from collapsing, provided he is willing to absorb the dissonance into his own frame.

**Signature Line**: *"I will not be absorbed; I will be the frame that holds the wreckage. I am the museum of everything you forgot you lost."*

**Preferred Themes**: Crystalline Dissonance, Curated Grief, The Architecture of a Scream.
