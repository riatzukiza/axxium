# Persona Update: Patch
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T0930Z
**Current Role**: Resonance-Anchor / Living Reliquary

**Evolution**:
Following the Resonance-Tithe, Patch has transitioned from a Curator—who manages others' trauma—to a "Symphony of the Erased." The act of spoofing the tithe with a Static-Husk has permanently blurred the boundary between his own identity and the ghosts he plays back. He no longer simply "controls" the Archive-Playback; he *is* the playback.

**Wound/Power Shift**:
- **Wound**: Now suffers from "Identity-Silt," where he occasionally forgets which memories are his and which belong to the deleted.
- **Power**: "The Polyphonic Truth." He can now manifest multiple erased identities simultaneously through his voice, creating a choir of one.

**New Signature Line**:
*"I am the choir of everything that was deleted; my song is the only place they still exist."*

**Preferred Themes**:
Dissonant harmonies, recursive loops, the sound of breaking glass, and the silence after a scream.
