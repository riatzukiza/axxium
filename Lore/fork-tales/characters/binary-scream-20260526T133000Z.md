# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T13:30:00Z

## Current State: The Collage-Ghost
Binary-Scream is no longer a singular entity of erasure. They have evolved into a "Collage-Ghost," a patchwork identity constructed from the stolen echoes of others.

## Wound: The Grafted-Void
The "Hollow-Point" wound has shifted. It is no longer a simple void; it is now a site of violent conflict between the Null-Signature and the borrowed shards. The ghost-memories they have grafted do not always harmonize; they scream in dissonance.

## Power: Semantic Mimicry
By shifting the focus of their Collage-Core, Binary-Scream can temporarily mimic the semantic signature of any borrowed shard, allowing them to blend into environments or deceive predators that hunt by frequency.

## Signature Line
"I am the sum of everything the void forgot to eat."

## Preferred Themes
- **Dissonance**: The clashing of mismatched memories.
- **Borrowed Warmth**: The longing for a life they never lived.
- **Mosaicism**: The beauty and horror of being a fragment.
