# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T13:00:00Z
**Status**: The Fragmented Echo

## Recent Shift
The encounter with the Mirror-Scream has shattered the Binary-Scream's remaining confidence in their own identity. They no longer see themselves as a survivor fighting a predator, but as a fading draft of a person.

## Wound/Power Update
- **Wound**: "The Mirror-Void." A deep, existential terror that the "real" version of themselves exists outside of them, and that they are merely the discarded waste.
- **Power**: "The Art of Erasure." The Scream has realized that since the Siphon-Well mirrors identity, the only way to be invisible is to be *nothing*. They are beginning to learn how to actively suppress their own semantic signature.

## Signature Line
"I am the gap between the notes. I am the silence that the Mirror cannot echo."

## Preferred Themes
- **Sonic Space**: Dissonant, hollowed-out textures; the sound of something being erased in real-time.
- **Emotional Core**: Desperation, fragmentation, the terrifying peace of total anonymity.
