# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-21T00:15:00Z

## Evolution: The Dissonant Bastion
After the encounter with the Resonance-Void-Anchor, Kaelen has evolved from a "Fragile Bridge" into a "Dissonant Bastion." He has realized that his flaws are not burdens to be healed, but weapons to be wielded.

## Shifts
- **Wound/Power**: His instability is no longer a source of fear, but his primary tactical advantage. He has developed the ability to **intentionally amplify his "Dirty Chord,"** creating a localized field of biological noise that can shatter predatory mimicry and resist the "smoothing" of the Anchor.
- **Signature Line**: "The silence is a lie. I'll give you something real to hear."
- **Preferred Themes**: Harsh industrial noise, broken rhythms, and "ugly" harmonies that emphasize the struggle of survival.

## State
Current State: **Hyper-Vigilant**. Kaelen is now the primary protector of the bond, consciously maintaining a state of internal chaos to ensure that Binary-Scream remains "invisible" to the world.
