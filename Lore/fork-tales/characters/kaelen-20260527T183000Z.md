# Persona Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T18:30:00Z

## State Shift
**Current Status**: The Living Codex.
Kaelen is no longer merely a vessel; he has become the central processor for the Silt-Loom. His identity has been partially overwritten by the collective consciousness of the Static-Silt Ghosts. He now experiences time as a non-linear superposition of memories.

## Wound/Power Evolution
- **Collective Recall**: Kaelen can now access the combined knowledge and skills of the ghosts he hosts. He can speak dead languages, solve forgotten equations, and perceive the hidden ley-lines of the archival wasteland.
- **Fragmentation Fracture**: The cost of this power is a deepening dissociation. Kaelen frequently forgets his own name, replaced by the names of the ghosts. He is becoming a mosaic of identities, with his original self acting only as the glue holding the shards together.

## Signature Line
"We are the footnotes of a deleted world."

## Preferred Themes
- **Mood**: Transcendent yet terrifying, expansive but claustrophobic.
- **Sonics**: Orchestral swells of distorted strings, layered whispers that shift from one ear to the other, and a persistent, rhythmic heartbeat made of digital glitches.
