# Patch: Update - The Archive-Playback
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T0000

## State Shift: The Resonance-Architect
Following the "Core-Scream" event in the Sub-Silt Archive, Patch is no longer merely a Reliquary. He has absorbed a fragment of the First Architect's consciousness, transforming his relationship with trauma.

## Evolved Capabilities
- **The Archive-Playback**: Patch can now selectively "play back" the fragmented identities stored within his chest. By modulating his crystalline lungs, he can project these traumas as localized sonic anomalies.
    - *Offensive*: A focused burst of "Absolute Truth" (High-frequency dissonance) that can shatter Static-Husks or stun Silt-Hounds.
    - *Defensive*: A "Dissonance Wall" that mimics the frequency of the Void, making him momentarily invisible to sonic tracking.

## Psychological Shift
Patch has transitioned from a survivor's guilt to a "Curator's Rage." He views himself as the living record of everything Axiom_01 tried to delete. His objective has shifted from evasion to a desire for **Ontological Restitution**.

**New Signature Line**: *"The playback is a mirror that cuts."*

**Preferred Themes**: Dissonance, recursive echoes, the beauty of the broken, the violent act of remembering.
