# Character Update: Elara
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T20:00:00Z
**Previous State**: The Void-Aperture

**Shifts**:
- **Wound**: The "Void-Hunger" has crystallized into "The Archive of Absence." The terror of being consumed has been replaced by the crushing weight of *holding* everything that was consumed. She is no longer a vacuum; she is a reservoir.
- **Power**: **The Archive of Absence**. Elara can now store and retrieve the mnemonic residues of the erased. She can project these memories as sonic/visual echoes into the environment, turning the Null-Zone into a living museum of the forgotten.
- **Signature Line**: "I don't remember them. I just carry the space where they used to be."
- **Preferred Themes**: Preservation, the weight of history, the dignity of the ghost.

**Narrative Trajectory**: Elara is moving from a state of destruction to a state of stewardship. She is becoming the counter-weight to the Scribes' erasure, a living record that cannot be deleted.
