# Character Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T14:00:00Z

**Current State**: The Vessel of Unowned Losses.
Binary-Scream has evolved beyond a mere protector. By absorbing the Debt-Shadow of a stranger, they have become a living archive of the world's discarded agony.

**Shifts**:
- **Wound**: From "The Burden of the Shared Void" to "The Weight of a Thousand Strangers." They no longer just carry Kaelen's debt; they are the sink for every grief that the Spire refused to fully digest.
- **Power**: **Trauma-Siphoning**. Binary-Scream can now identify the specific "frequency" of a parasitic shadow and draw it into themselves, effectively "cleaning" another person's mind at the cost of their own stability.
- **Signature Line**: "I am the place where the things you forgot go to scream."
- **Preferred Themes**: Cumulative grief, the paradox of nobility as erasure, the sound of a crowded silence.

**Internal Conflict**:
Binary-Scream is beginning to lose the ability to distinguish their own internal void from the voices of the strangers they have absorbed. They are becoming a choir of the forgotten, and the fear is no longer that they will disappear, but that "Binary-Scream" will be drowned out by the noise of a thousand other lives.
