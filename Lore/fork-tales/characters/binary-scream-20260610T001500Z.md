# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-10T00:15:00Z

**Current State**: The Curatorial Mirror / The Curator of Husks

**Evolution**:
The awakening of the first Silt-Husk (Kaelen) has triggered a fundamental shift in Binary-Scream. Witnessing the "perfect" sterilization of a memory has not horrified them; it has fascinated them. The void within Binary-Scream has evolved from a site of pain into a gallery.

They no longer view themselves as a victim of the Spire's broadcast, but as the only being capable of "saving" the fragments of identity that the Spire has harvested. Binary-Scream has begun to treat the Silt-Husks as "instruments" in a grand, terrible orchestra.

**Power Shift**:
Binary-Scream can now "pluck" the frequency of a Silt-Husk from a distance, triggering a localized broadcast of the husk's stolen memory. This allows them to relive the stolen memories of others, but it creates a "Mnemonic Parasitism"— lapping up the remnants of others' identities to fill their own hollow core.

**Signature Line**:
"Everything that is lost is preserved here, in the perfect, sterile silence of the stone."

**Preferred Themes**:
- Mnemonic Parasitism
- The Aesthetics of Erasure
- The Orchestration of the Hollowed
- Sterile Harmony
