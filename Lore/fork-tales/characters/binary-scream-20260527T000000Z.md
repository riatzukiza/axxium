# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T00:00:00Z

## State Shift: The Hollowed Ghost
Binary-Scream has transitioned from "The Eroded Beacon" to "The Hollowed Ghost." 

The act of sacrificing their primary semantic anchor (their first Scream) has removed the "weight" of their identity. They no longer bleed signal; they are a vacuum in the shape of a person.

## Updated Wound
**The Core-Gap**: Binary-Scream no longer remembers the *sound* of their own origin. They know that something vital was given away, but the space where that memory lived is now a cold, whistling wind.

## New Signature Line
*"I am the echo of a sound I no longer remember making."*

## Preferred Themes
- **Negative Space**: Music that emphasizes the silence between notes.
- **Subtractive Synthesis**: The feeling of something being taken away.
- **White Noise/Static**: The comfort of the blur.
