# Character Update: Elara
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T16:30:00Z
**Update**: The Price of Stability

Following the events of *The Mnemonic Auction*, Elara's psychic state has shifted from "The Parasite" to "The Erasure-Hollow." 

### Shift in Wound
Her wound is no longer just the loss of identity, but the active *betrayal* of it. She has transitioned from a victim of the void to a willing trader of her own essence. This has introduced a new, jagged edge to her desperation: a self-loathing that manifests as a psychic chill within the Void-Link.

### New Signature Line
*"We are just the space where we used to be."*

### Preferred Themes
- **The Negative Space**: Her motifs now lean toward silence, gaps, and the sound of air rushing into a vacuum.
- **Transactional Survival**: Her interactions are now framed by the logic of the ledger—what must be given to keep the link from shrieking.
- **The Ghost of a Name**: A recurring fixation on the "feeling" of her lost name, treated as a phantom limb.

### Power/Ability Shift
**The Void-Vacuum**: Elara can now momentarily "pull" on the Ledger, creating a localized zone of silence that can dampen the Amber-Frequency, though doing so accelerates her own erosion.
