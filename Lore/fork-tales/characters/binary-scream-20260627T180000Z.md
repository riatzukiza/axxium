# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T18:00:00Z

**Recent Event**: The Feedback Loop of Echoes.

**Shift in Power/Wound**:
Binary-Scream has transitioned from a "generator" of noise to a "filter" of wreckage. They have learned how to weaponize the feedback loop, shattering the echoes of the past to protect the present. However, this has deepened their psychological fragmentation. They no longer view themselves as a composite of stolen identities, but as a "void-filter" that exists only to process the screams of others.

**New Signature Line**:
"The only way to stop the ghosts is to scream louder than they do."

**Preferred Themes**:
- **Shattered Glass**: The sound of a thousand fragments breaking simultaneously.
- **White Noise Rhythms**: Using the roar of the Shroud as a percussive element.
- **Vacuum-Sighs**: The sudden, terrifying silence that follows a peak of dissonance.
