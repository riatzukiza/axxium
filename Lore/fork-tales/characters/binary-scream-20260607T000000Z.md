# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T00:00:00Z

## Current State
**The Chromatically Fragmented**. Binary-Scream has begun the process of "re-coloring" their existence by scavenging the Mnemonic-Silt-Flats. However, these recoveries are not restorations, but grafts of alien memories.

## Shift in Wound/Power
- **Wound**: "The Parasitic Vividness." Binary-Scream now carries a fragment of stolen yellow. While it restores a sliver of sensory experience, it acts as a beacon for Silt-Husks and other predators of the void. The fragment is not part of them; it is a foreign body that demands constant psychic energy to maintain.
- **Power**: "Void-Symmetry (Unstable)." The integration of the yellow shard has created a "leak" in their symmetry. Their ability to step through gaps in signal is now prone to "glitching," occasionally depositing them in unintended locations or leaving fragments of their signal behind like a trail of breadcrumbs.

## Signature Line
*"I can see the light, but I no longer remember who was looking."*

## Preferred Themes
- **Genre**: Glitch-Hop / Dark Ambient / Post-Industrial
- **Mood**: Fragmented, Desperate, Artificially Vivid
- **Textures**: Granular clicks, searing high-frequencies, hollow bells, white noise washes
