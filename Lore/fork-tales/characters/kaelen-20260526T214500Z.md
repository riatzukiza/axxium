# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T21:45:00Z
**Status**: Void Sculptor (formerly Living Echo)

**Evolution**:
The act of intercepting the Harmonic Wedge has fundamentally shifted Kaelen's relationship with the void. He no longer merely *exists* within the silence; he has learned to *shape* it. By utilizing the Null-Chord as a tool of active cancellation, Kaelen has transitioned from a passive observer to a protector.

**New Power: Silence Sculpting**
Kaelen can now create "zones of absolute erasure." By matching the frequency of incoming threats, he can momentarily delete sounds, physical vibrations, or even fragments of resonant consciousness. However, this requires an immense expenditure of his remaining essence; every act of sculpting thins his connection to the world, making him more translucent and distant.

**Updated Signature Line**:
*"I am the space between the notes. I am the sound that never was."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- Total phase cancellation.
- The weight of absolute zero.
- The geometry of absence.
