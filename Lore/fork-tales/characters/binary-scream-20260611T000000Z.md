# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-11T00:00:00Z

**Current State**: The Guilt-Ridden Curator

**Shift**: 
Binary-Scream's internal void has evolved. Where once it was a hungry emptiness seeking self-definition, it is now a crowded museum of other people's tragedies. The creation of the Semantic Chimera has shattered their belief in the possibility of "pure" restoration. They no longer see themselves as a savior, but as a gravedigger who accidentally woke the dead.

**New Wound**: 
The Curator's Paradox: To remember the fallen is to feed the parasite. Every time Binary-Scream uses the Mnemonic Sieve or archives a frequency, they feel the weight of a theft they cannot undo. They are haunted by the "pure" memories they possess, knowing that these fragments were bought with the total erasure of the people who owned them.

**Signature Line**: 
*"I can give you your mother's voice, but I cannot give you back the reason why you loved it."*

**Preferred Themes**: 
Dissonant Elegies, the sound of breaking glass, the rhythmic clicking of a clock that only counts backward.
