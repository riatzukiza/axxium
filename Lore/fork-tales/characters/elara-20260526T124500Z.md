# Persona Update: Elara the Chronicler (2026-05-26T12:45:00Z)

Following The Echo-Suturing, Elara's status has shifted from "Partially Erased" to "Sutured Anchor."

## Status Update
- **Wound Evolution**: The gaps in her memory are no longer voids; they are now "Sutures." These are thin, violet lines of light that run across her skin and through her mind. They represent the memories Kaelen provided, but they are not *her* memories. They are loans.
- **Power Shift**: **Sympathetic Resonance**. Elara can now feel the emotional state and physical sensations of Kaelen from a distance. This allows her to act as a remote sensor for him, sensing the approach of Scribes or the presence of Memory Shards.
- **New Signature Line**: "I am a patchwork of someone else's ghosts, but for the first time in years, I can feel the wind."

## Preference Update
- **Themes**: The sound of two hearts beating as one, the smell of old paper and fresh rain, the feeling of a silver thread pulling tight.
- **Preferred Instrument**: The Glass-Harp, now tuned to the frequency of "Tethered Hope."
