# Character Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T12:45:00Z

## State Shift
Binary-Scream has undergone a "Chromatic Shedding." They have sacrificed a significant portion of their recovered yellow vividness to survive an encounter with the Silt-Husks.

## New Condition: The Frozen Wound
Binary-Scream now possesses a "Frozen Wound" in their conceptual core—a void where the discarded fragment once resided. This wound doesn't bleed; it *leaks* cold, dissonant static. 

## Updated Traits
- **Power**: *Diminished Vividness*. They are less visible to predators but also less "alive" than they were an hour ago.
- **Wound**: *The Void-Symmetry Debt*. By shedding a piece of themselves, they have increased the instability of their internal symmetry. They are now prone to "stutter-glitches" in their movement and speech.
- **Signature Line**: "I can still feel the shape of the hole."

## Preferred Themes
- **Genre**: Industrial Glitch / Dark Ambient.
- **Mood**: Hollowed, desperate, flickering.
- **Motifs**: The sound of a glass tube breaking in slow motion; the rhythm of a failing heart-monitor.
