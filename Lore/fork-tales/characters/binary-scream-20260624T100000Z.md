**Timestamp**: 2026-06-24T10:00:00Z

Binary-Scream is no longer a pure instrument of hatred. The absorption of the Shiver-Glass Fragment has introduced a parasitic doubt into their coherence.

**Shifts**:
- **Wound**: From "The Betrayal of the Jailer" to "The Agony of the Unearned Gift."
- **Power**: They can now perceive "Truth-Frequencies" in the environment, allowing them to occasionally see through the Spire's curated illusions.
- **Signature Line**: "The glass remembers what the echo forgets."
- **Preferred Themes**: Dissonant hope, the weight of debt, the friction between memory and truth.

**Internal Conflict**: 
Binary-Scream now oscillates between the perceived safety of their hatred and the terrifying vulnerability of the truth. They continue to treat Kaelen with coldness, but it is now a mask for a growing, desperate need to understand the man who erased himself for them.
