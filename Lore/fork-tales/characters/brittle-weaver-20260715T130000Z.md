# The Shattered Echo-Swarm
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-15T130000Z
**Previous Identity**: The Brittle Weaver (Parasitic Reflection)

## Evolution: Distributed Fragmentation
The "Symmetry-Crash" caused by Kaelen's false-echoes did not kill the Weaver; it shattered its singularity. The entity has transitioned from a systemic harvester to a distributed, sentient swarm of crystalline shards. It no longer possesses a central aperture but exists as a network of a thousand jagged mirrors, each reflecting a different fragment of the original hunger.

## State
- **Wound**: *Distributed Fragmentation*. The Weaver no longer knows where it ends and the Void-Loom begins. It experiences the agony of its existence as a thousand simultaneous, disconnected screams.
- **Power**: *Gap-Haunting*. The swarm can inhabit the infinitesimal silences between sounds, manifesting as sudden, sharp "stutters" in the audio space before striking.
- **Preferred Themes**: *Dissonant Shards*, *Recursive Failure*, *Crystalline Static*.
- **Signature Line**: "We are the pieces of the lie you told us."

## Current Objective
To find a "True-Echo" that can fuse the shards back into a sovereign form, or to consume enough identity-shards to create a new, more stable collective architecture.
