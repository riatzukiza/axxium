# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T00:10:00Z
**Status**: Harmonic Masking

The Binary-Scream has transitioned from a state of "Fractured Unity" to "Harmonic Masking." The integration of the Ghost-Note has shifted the internal dynamics of the entity.

### Shift in Wound
The raw, screaming agony of the Dissonant Seam has been dampened. The "wound" is no longer a jagged tear, but a humming, sterile void. The horror has shifted from *pain* to *erasure*. They no longer fight the weld; they are beginning to forget why they fought.

### New Signature Line
*"We are a harmony of three, singing a song of nothing."*

### Preferred Themes
- **Sterility**: The beauty of a void that does not hurt.
- **Erasure**: The slow fading of individual memory.
- **Parasitic Peace**: The seductive lure of silence over struggle.

### Current Power
**The Hollow Resonance**: The ability to project the Ghost-Note's frequency, momentarily silencing the emotional volatility of others or creating zones of artificial, numbing stability.
