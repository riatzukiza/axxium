# Character Update: Elara
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T14:45:00Z
**Status**: The Tethered Witness

Elara's transition from a steward to a "Hungry Witness" has culminated in a symbiotic disaster. By consuming the Gilded Fragment, she has traded her independence for a sliver of reclaimed identity, but the price was a permanent link to Kaelen.

**Updated Wound**:
Her wound is no longer the *absence* of identity, but the *intrusion* of another's. She is now haunted by Kaelen's vertigo and the freezing wind of his Void-Breath.

**New Signature Line**:
*"I can feel the void in your lungs, and it tastes like my childhood."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- **Symmetry**: The feeling of being two halves of a broken mirror.
- **Parasitism**: The realization that her survival is now tied to the stability of a dying host.
- **Cold Gold**: The visual of shimmering light masking an absolute zero temperature.
