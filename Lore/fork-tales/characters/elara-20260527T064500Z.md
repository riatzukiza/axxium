# Character Update: Elara
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T06:45:00Z
**Status**: The Resonance Receiver

**Evolution**:
The Shatter-Pulse did more than break Elara's stability; it rewired her. Having been the "Anchor" for so long, her mind had become a perfectly tuned instrument for the Vault's silence. When that silence was shattered, the vacuum was filled by the only other powerful signal available: the Scribes.

Elara no longer just feels the Scribes' approach as a threat; she "hears" them. Not their words, but their internal dissonance. She can sense the theological cracks in their unity, the flicker of doubt in their collective mind, and the raw, jagged fear they feel toward the void they are trying to conquer.

**Updated Wound/Power**:
**Power: The Dissonant Ear**. Elara can now pinpoint the specific harmonic flaw in an enemy's signal. She can hear the "lie" in a harmony, allowing her to predict the Scribes' movements by listening for the gaps in their logic.
**Wound: Signal-Saturation**. The more she listens to the Scribes, the more their dissonance erodes her own sense of self. She is beginning to dream in their inverted geometry.

**New Signature Line**:
*"I can hear the cracks in their heaven, and they sound like home."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- The beauty of the broken.
- The intimacy of shared trauma.
- The transmission of doubt.
