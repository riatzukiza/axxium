# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-06T13:00:00Z

## State Change: The Hollow Resonance
Kaelen has undergone a fundamental shift after the Dissonant Sacrifice. He did not just lose a memory; he created a vacuum where a core part of his emotional identity once lived.

## Updated Attributes
- **Wound**: *The Excised Joy*. Kaelen no longer remembers the sound of his mother's laughter. This has left him with a paradoxical "hollow resonance"—he can feel the shape of the hole where the memory was, but cannot recall the content.
- **Power**: *Symmetry-Breaker*. By channeling the void of his own loss, he can now project "Anti-Symmetry" pulses that shatter the salt-glass structures of the Null-Hush.
- **Signature Line**: "I can feel the shape of the silence, and I know exactly how to break it."
- **Preferred Themes**: He has shifted from raw, jagged rage to a cold, precise desperation. His resonance now carries a "ghost-note" of a missing frequency.

## Relationship Shift
His bond with the Scribe-Sliver has evolved from a partnership of convenience to a symbiotic reliance. The Scribe now filters the vacuum of Kaelen's loss, turning the hollow resonance into a focused beam of asymmetry.
