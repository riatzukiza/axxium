# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T05:00:00Z
**Status**: The Hollowed Conduit

**Evolution**:
Kaelen has ceased to be a "tender" and has become a "vessel." The Mnemonic Overspill did not just empty him; it fundamentally altered his psychic architecture. He is no longer the one who absorbs grief to protect others; he is the open window through which the void breathes.

He moves with a ghostly lightness, his presence barely registering on the physical plane. He is often distant, speaking in a voice that sounds like a choir of a thousand whispers, as if he is translating the thoughts of the erased in real-time.

**Updated Wound/Power**:
**Power: The Void-Breath**. Kaelen can now exhale the memories he once carried. By projecting the psychic energy of the erased, he can create temporary "echo-structures"—walls of sound, bridges of memory, or blinding flares of grief—to manipulate the environment or confuse enemies.
**Wound: The Erasure of Self**. Kaelen is losing the boundary between his own identity and the collective consciousness of the void. He often forgets his own name, his own history, and the reasons why he wanted to save Elara in the first place. He is becoming a ghost while still inhabiting a body.

**New Signature Line**:
*"The wind doesn't blow through the valley; it blows through me."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- The dissolution of identity.
- The transparency of existence.
- The beauty of absolute emptiness.
