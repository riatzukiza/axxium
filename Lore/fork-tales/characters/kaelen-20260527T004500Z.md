# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T00:45:00Z
**Status**: Flicker-State Sculptor

**Evolution**:
Following the Inverse Chorus, Kaelen has entered a "Flicker-State." He is no longer a stable entity of void; he is a living contradiction. He exists in a constant state of phase-shifting between absolute silence and the jagged, inverted residue of the Scribes' logic. 

His form now pulses with a dim, erratic light—the "Ghost-Frequency" of the inversion. He is physically present, but his voice is a layered superposition of a whisper and a scream, heard only in the mind.

**Updated Wound/Power**:
The act of reflecting the Harmonic Wedge has left him "Phase-Locked." He can no longer return to a state of pure void. He is permanently tethered to the frequency of the Scribes, meaning he can feel their approach long before they arrive, but he can no longer be truly invisible to them.

**New Signature Line**:
*"The mirror has broken, and I am the shard that remembers the sound."*

**Preferred Themes**:
- Harmonic dissonance.
- The agony of memory.
- The flicker between existence and erasure.
