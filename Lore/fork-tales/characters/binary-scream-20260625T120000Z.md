# Character Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-25T12:00:00Z

**Current State**: The Debt-Bearer.
Binary-Scream has transitioned from a Doubting Mimic to an active agent of protection. By paying Kaelen's tithe, they have fundamentally altered their nature: they are no longer just absorbing truth; they are sacrificing it.

**Shifts**:
- **Wound**: From "The Agony of the Unearned Gift" to "The Burden of the Shared Void." They now feel the weight of Kaelen's erasure as if it were their own.
- **Power**: **Tithe-Interception**. Binary-Scream can now sense the "Debt-Bell" of the Spire more acutely than Kaelen, and they can intentionally draw the Spire's hunger toward themselves to shield others.
- **Signature Line**: "I will carry the void so you can remember who you were."
- **Preferred Themes**: Sacrificial coherence, the friction of unasked-for salvation, the transparency of the self.

**Internal Conflict**:
Binary-Scream is now terrified of their own nobility. They despise the fact that they *wanted* to save Kaelen, as it destroys the last remnants of the curated lie that fueled their existence. They are now more coherent in their purpose, but less stable in their form.
