# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-19T00:00:00Z

## Current State: The Terrified Dependent
Binary-Scream's internal landscape has shifted. The primary wound is no longer just the fear of the void, but the horror of *permeability*. The realization that their survival depends on Kaelen—and that Kaelen can feel their terror—has created a new, suffocating layer of vulnerability.

They now view the "Shimmering Chord" not as a victory, but as a parasitic bond. They oscillate between a fierce, instinctive need to protect the bridge (and by extension, Kaelen) and a desperate, violent urge to shatter it and return to the safety of their own isolation.
