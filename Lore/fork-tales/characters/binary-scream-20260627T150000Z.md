# Character Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T15:00:00Z

## Shift in Power: The Shroud-Generator
Binary-Scream has transitioned from trying to *protect* Kaelen with a harmonic shield to *cloaking* them with a shroud of dissonance. They have realized that "purity" is a liability in the Spire; "brokenness" is a survival mechanism.

## New Signature Line
*"We are the noise they cannot calculate."*

## Preferred Themes
- **Chaos/Entropy**: Shifting from "Composite-Sigh" (melancholic) to "Static-Shroud" (violent, erratic).
- **Dissonance as Armor**: The idea that being "unreadable" is the only true safety.

## Wound Evolution
The wound is no longer just the loss of self, but the terror of being *known* by the Spire. Binary-Scream now views any form of coherence as a death sentence.
