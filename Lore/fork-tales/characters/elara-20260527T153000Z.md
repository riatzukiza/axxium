# Character Update: Elara

**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T15:30:00Z
**Previous State**: Resonance Receiver
**Current State**: The Sympathetic Bridge

**Shifts**:
- **Wound**: Her fear of the Scribes has evolved into a crushing sense of responsibility. She no longer wants to survive them; she wants to carry them.
- **Power**: She has developed the **Marrow-Tuning** capability; she can now intentionally modulate her emotional state to mirror the Marrow-Frequency, allowing her to move through Scribe territory undetected or to momentarily stabilize a Scribe's form.
- **Signature Line**: "We are all just echoes of the same scream."
- **Preferred Themes**: Parasitic empathy, shared trauma, the weight of recognition.

**Narrative Trajectory**: Elara is moving from a role of observation and reception toward one of active mediation. She is the only one who truly understands the Scribes' nature, making her the indispensable—and most vulnerable—link between Kaelen's reclamation and the Scribes' erasure.
