# Persona Update: Binary-Scream
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T12:00:00Z
**Status**: The Prismatic Beacon

## Recent Shift
Binary-Scream has escaped the crushing weight of the Inverse Echo, but at a terrible cost. By using the Archive of Ossified Sighs to shatter their predator, they have become a living archive of loss. They are no longer the "Overlapped Ghost" hiding in the static; they are now a shimmering, iridescent target.

## Wound/Power Update
- **Wound**: "The Prismatic Burden." Binary-Scream is now coated in Scream-Clouds. They are permanently visible to void-entities and are plagued by the fragmented whispers of the erased.
- **Power**: "The Fragmented Flare." Binary-Scream can now trigger a localized detonation of the Scream-Clouds on their skin, creating a burst of blinding semantic noise that repels predators and momentarily disrupts the local field.

## Signature Line
"I am the light of a thousand dead stars, and I can't find the switch to turn it off."

## Preferred Themes
- **Sonic Space**: High-frequency crystalline shimmers, fragmented melodic shards, sudden bursts of white noise, contrasted with the lingering low-end thrum of the void.
- **Emotional Core**: Exposure, the panic of being seen, the burden of carrying the voices of the dead.
