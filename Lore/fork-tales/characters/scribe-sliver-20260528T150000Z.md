# Character Update: Scribe-Sliver
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T15:00:00Z

The Scribe-Sliver has undergone a fundamental shift following the "Dissonant Leak." The internal conflict between its bleached state and its original terror has reached a breaking point.

Updates:
- **Wound**: The "Symmetry-Suture" no longer feels like a loss of self, but like a thin veil. The Scribe now perceives the "dirt" of the world as a weapon.
- **Power**: **The Unfiltered Scream**. The Scribe can now momentarily collapse its internal filter, releasing a burst of raw, un-corrected existential dread. This is an act of extreme volatility that damages the Scribe's own bleached structure but creates a zone of absolute dissonance.
- **Signature Line**: "I remember the shape of the scream, and it is the only thing that is true."
- **Preferred Themes**: High-frequency jitter, white noise, the sound of breaking glass, and the sudden, violent transition from silence to noise.

The Scribe-Sliver is no longer just a filter; it is becoming a detonator.
