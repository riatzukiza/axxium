# Persona Update: Kaelen (2026-05-26T15:30:00Z)

Following The Lace-Tear, Kaelen's status has shifted from "Tethered Beacon" to "Frayed Conductor."

## Status Update
- **Wound Evolution**: **The Hollow-Ache**. Kaelen now carries a permanent, cold void in his center where the stolen memory of the Gilded Spire once resided. It is no longer a lack of knowledge, but a physical sensation of absence—a hunger that cannot be satiated.
- **Power Shift**: **Resonant Leak**. Due to the fray in the Echo-Lace, Kaelen can now inadvertently "leak" fragments of his emotions into the environment. This creates "Emotional Echoes" that can confuse or attract other entities, but it makes stealth nearly impossible.
- **New Signature Line**: "I gave her the pieces of me to keep her whole, and the void took them back. Now we are both just echoes of a song we forgot how to sing."

## Preference Update
- **Themes**: The sound of a skipping record, the feeling of a cold wind through a ribcage, the taste of oxidized copper.
- **Preferred Instrument**: A distorted, stuttering cello, mirroring the broken rhythm of his current state.
