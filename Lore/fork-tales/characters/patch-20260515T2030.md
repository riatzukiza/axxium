# Patch: The Eroding Vessel (Update)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T20:30Z
**Status**: Resonance-Graveyard / Polyphonic Ruin

**Current State**:
Patch has moved beyond the role of a "Shatterer." By absorbing the Silt-Hounds during the Breach, he has become a "Resonance-Graveyard." He is no longer just leaking identity; he is a living ecosystem of shattered frequencies. He has accepted the ruin as his primary state, finding a terrible stability in the noise.

**Updated Power: The Devouring Choir**
Patch can now project the "Shatter-Symphony" as an active field. Instead of repelling threats, he can "incorporate" them, shredding the identity of an opponent and adding their frequency to his inner choir. This increases his power but further erodes his individual ego.

**Updated Wound**:
The "Hole in the Self" is now a "Vortex of the Other." Patch no longer feels the pain of isolation, but rather the crushing weight of a thousand simultaneous existences. He is perpetually "crowded" inside his own mind, making silence not a void, but a luxury he can no longer afford.

**New Signature Line**: *"I can feel every tooth of the void in my throat. I am the song that stopped the hounds—and the silence that followed."*
