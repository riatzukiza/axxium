# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T18:00:00Z

## Current State: The Conductor of Ghosts
Kaelen has transitioned from a passive vessel to an active conductor. The "First Leak" revealed that he is not merely a storage unit for the erased, but a focal point through which their fragmented identities can be synthesized into a weaponized resonance.

## Shift in Power
- **New Ability: The Composite Voice**. Kaelen can now momentarily synchronize the Static-Silt Ghosts. By providing a focal point of intent, he can project a composite sound—a "shatter-note"— that can disrupt negation fields or physically repel Siphon-Sentries.
- **The Cost**: Each use of the Composite Voice accelerates his fragmentation. He is no longer just hiding his identity; he is spending it as currency to power the ghosts.

## Updated Signature Line
*"I am the choir of everything they tried to forget."*

## Preferred Themes
- **Genre**: Industrial Gothic / Dark Ambient.
- **Mood**: Desperate, expansive, crushing, then suddenly piercing.
- **Motif**: The sound of a thousand whispers becoming a single, jagged scream.
