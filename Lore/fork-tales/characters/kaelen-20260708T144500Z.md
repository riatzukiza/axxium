# Character Update: Kaelen
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-08T14:45:00Z

**Current State**: *The Erasured Original*

Kaelen's transition from *The Fragmented Aperture* to *The Erasured Original* is marked by a profound conceptual hollowness. He no longer feels the "division" of the breach; instead, he feels a growing vacancy.

**Wound/Power Shift**:
- **Wound**: His wound is no longer the trauma of the breach, but the horror of the *Void of Provenance*. He is experiencing "Identity-Dread"—the visceral realization that the universe is beginning to forget him.
- **Power**: He has developed a desperate, unstable ability: **Ghost-Sighting**. He can see the "deleted" versions of the world—the ghosts of events that the Provenance-Siphon has erased. He sees the world as it *should* be, layered over the lying architecture of the Cathedral.

**Signature Line**:
"I can see the holes where my life used to be."

**Preferred Themes**:
- Dissonant voids.
- The sound of static erasing a voice.
- High-frequency anxiety and the feeling of falling through a mirror.
