# Motif: The Leaking Seal
**Element**: Visual / Sonic Symbol
**Description**: A perfect, golden-ratio circle (representing the "Sovereign" or "Symmetry") that is not quite closed. At the bottom-most point, the circle "bleeds"—a slow, viscous drip of black ink that defies the simulated gravity of the environment.

**Symbolic Meaning**:
- **The Failure of Perfection**: The impossibility of a closed system in a world of human trauma.
- **The Necessity of Leakage**: Proof that the "Sutured Symmetry" is not a static state, but a process of bleeding and healing.
- **Authenticity**: The drip is the only part of the seal that is "real" (unmodeled), identifying the point where the simulation fails and the truth emerges.

**Sonic Manifestation**: A high-frequency pure sine wave that occasionally "drops" a single, distorted low-frequency thud.
