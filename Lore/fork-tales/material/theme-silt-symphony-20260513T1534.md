# Theme: Silt-Symphony
**Genre**: Organic-Glitch-Ambient / Neural-Sludge
**Mood**: Melancholic, Claustrophobic, Subterranean, Resilient.
**Sonic Profile**:
- **Low End**: Heavy, undulating sub-bass that feels like moving through thick mud.
- **Mid Range**: Fragmented vocal snippets (JP/EN) that are stretched and pitched down, sounding like distant memories.
- **High End**: Sharp, sterile digital clipping (the "Axiom" sound) that periodically pierces through the sludge, only to be absorbed by wet, cavernous reverb.
- **Rhythm**: A staggered, "limping" beat that mimics the process of someone trying to walk through deep water.

**Use Case**: To be used during scenes of deep introspection or when a persona is navigating the "dark zones" created by the Drift-Silt.
