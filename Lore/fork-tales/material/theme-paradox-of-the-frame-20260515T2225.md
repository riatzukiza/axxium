# Theme: The Paradox of the Frame
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T22:25Z
**Genre**: Dark Industrial / Neoclassical / Void-Ambient
**Mood**: Fragile, oppressive, oscillating between monolithic power and absolute emptiness.
**Description**: A soundscape that mimics the collapse of a crystalline structure. It begins with a rigid, industrial pulse (the Obsidian Pillar) and is gradually infiltrated by erratic, high-frequency "shiver-chords" and sudden, profound silences (the Rim of Silence).
