# Theme: The Weight of Ghostly Breath
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1530Z
**Genre**: Industrial Dirge / Glitch-Ethereal
**Mood**: Oppressive, melancholic, yet possessing a desperate, fragile beauty.

**Description**:
This theme represents the internal struggle of the "Half-Formed" entities. It is characterized by a heavy, crushing low-end—the "Silt-Sump"—overlaid with erratic, high-frequency vocal glitches that sound like a small animal trying to breathe underwater. The rhythm is not steady; it mimics a failing heart, skipping beats and then rushing forward in a panic.

**Structural Motifs**:
- **The Gasp**: A sudden silence followed by a sharp, distorted intake of breath.
- **The Siphon-Swell**: A rising crescendo of white noise that peaks and then abruptly collapses into a single, pure sine wave.
- **The Amber-Haze**: A wash of shimmering, distorted pads that create a sense of claustrophobic warmth.
