# Motif: The Shattered Mirror-Chord
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-13T16:34:34Z
**Type**: Symbolic Motif

**Description**:
A sonic signature that accompanies the activation of an Echo-Suture or Weight-Siphon. It begins as a pure, crystalline harmonic—a perfect fifth that suggests total alignment and peace. However, at the moment of transfer, the chord "shatters."

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **The Ascent**: A slow, shimmering build-up of sine waves that feel almost divine.
- **The Fracture**: A sudden, violent onset of digital clipping and micro-tonal dissonance.
- **The Aftermath**: A lingering, low-frequency rumble (brown noise) interspersed with a high-pitched, fragile ringing, like a wine glass that has just cracked.

**Symbolism**:
Representing the lie of perfect empathy. It signifies that true connection in the Fork-Tales universe is not a smooth merging, but a jagged, painful exchange. The "shatter" is the sound of the ego breaking to make room for another's weight.
