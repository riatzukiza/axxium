# Instrument: The Silt-Cello of the Unspoken
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1220Z

**Description**: An instrument crafted from the petrified remains of an erased archivist. The body is a hollowed-out shell of calcified memory-silt, pale and porous. The strings are not metal or gut, but solidified ribbons of "unspoken truths"—frequencies that were deleted before they could be uttered.

**Sonic Profile**: The la the Silt-Cello produces a visceral, weeping tone that seems to bend under its own weight. It possesses a natural, haunting glissando that mimics a human sob. When played with the obsidian bow, it creates a "grinding" texture that sounds like two tectonic plates of grief sliding against one another. Its resonance is so profound that it can physically vibrate the air in the Vault of Shivered Glass, causing the mirrors to sweat a thick, silver liquid.

**Role in Seed-Symphony**: Used primarily in 'The Descent,' to establish the crushing gravity of loss before the Fracture begins.
