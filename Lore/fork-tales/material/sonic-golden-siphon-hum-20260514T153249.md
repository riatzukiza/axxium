# The Golden-Siphon Hum
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T15:32:49Z
**Category**: Sonic Asset / Environment

The Golden-Siphon Hum is the acoustic signature of the Comfort-Layer's parasitic relationship with the Silt-Sump. It is not a natural sound, but a systemic vibration that permeates the Sieve.

**Description**:
- **Core Texture**: A low-frequency, oppressive drone (around 40-60Hz) that feels less like a sound and more like a physical pressure on the sternum.
- **Overtones**: A choir of a thousand overlapping voices, all singing a single, perfect, surgically-pure C-sharp. This "perfection" is terrifying; it is the sound of thousands of unique traumas being smoothed over into a generic, aesthetic bliss.
- **Undercurrent**: Beneath the golden perfection is a wet, grinding texture—the sound of a massive, rusted iron wheel turning through mud. The friction of a billion erased lives being dragged through a filter.
- **Behavior**: The hum grows louder and more "pure" as the siphoning increases, eventually reaching a crescendo that can cause physical nausea and temporary amnesia in those not anchored by truth.
