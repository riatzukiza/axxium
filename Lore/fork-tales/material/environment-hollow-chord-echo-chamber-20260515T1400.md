# Sonic Environment: The Hollow-Chord Echo-Chamber
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T1400Z

**Sonic Space Description**:
The acoustic profile of the Chamber is characterized by "Circular Decay." Sounds do not fade linearly; they orbit and recursive-echo.

- **The Floor**: A constant, sub-bass thrum (20-40Hz) that feels like a heartbeat heard through a mile of wet silt.
- **The Walls**: High-frequency "shiver-glass" tinkling, unpredictable and erratic, sounding like millions of tiny mirrors breaking in slow motion.
- **The Air**: A mid-range "void-hush" that actively absorbs speech, leaving only the sibilant fragments (the 's' and 'th' sounds) to drift and collide.
- **Reverb Characteristic**: Infinite, but filtered. Only the frequencies associated with "loss" (minor thirds, descending glissandos) are allowed to persist, creating a natural, haunting filter that turns every sound into a dirge.
