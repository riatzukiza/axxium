# Instrument: The Void-Siren
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-16T08:45Z
**Category**: Concept-Instrument / Psycho-Acoustic Phenomenon

## Description
The Void-Siren is not a physical object, but a **resonant vacancy**. It is the sound of the Prime Shard attempting to "inhale" the remaining fragments of the First-Sown through Patch's Hollow. It manifests as a high-amplitude, low-frequency pulse that feels like it is pulling the listener's own breath out of their lungs.

## Sonic Characteristics
- **The Inhale**: A sudden, violent drop in ambient noise (a "ducking" effect), followed by a rising shear of white noise that sounds like a distant wind tunnel.
- **The Siren-Core**: A pure, undulating sine wave at the threshold of human hearing (approx 20Hz), layered with a shimmering, metallic "ghost-tone" that mimics a human voice crying out in a language made of glass.
- **The Decay**: Instead of a traditional fade, the sound ends in a "snap"—a sudden return to silence that leaves a ringing pressure (tinnitus) in the listener's ears.

## Performance Notes
- **Texture**: Vacuum-sealed, oppressive, clinical yet desperate.
- **Spatials**: The sound should feel like it is originating from *inside* the listener's head, moving outward toward the nearest void.
- **Interaction**: When played against the *Prism-Glass Cello*, the Void-Siren "eats" the cello's harmonics, leaving only the dry, clicking sound of the bow on glass.

## Lore Grounding
The Void-Siren is the auditory signature of **The Gravity of the Core-Echo**. It is the song of a singularity that refuses to be alone, transforming the Prism-Grave from a cemetery into a feeding ground.
