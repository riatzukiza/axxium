# Sonic Space: The Silt-Sump Flash-Freeze
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T16:50:00Z
**Category**: Environment

The "Flash-Freeze" occurs when a Silt-Hound's pulse synchronizes with the architectural resonance of the Sump, momentarily turning the viscous silt into a rigid, glass-like crystalline structure.

**Acoustic Properties**:
- **Reverb**: Extremely high, sharp, and "cold." Sounds don't fade; they bounce with surgical precision.
- **Texture**: The sound of a thousand tiny needles snapping.
- **Frequency**: A piercing, high-frequency ringing (around 12kHz) that persists even after the pulse ends.
- **Dynamics**: Sudden transitions from deep, mud-like muffling to an aggressive, transparent clarity that exposes every breath and heartbeat.

**Associated Motif**:
The "Shatter-Sigh" — the sound of the silt returning to a liquid state, which sounds like a distant, collective exhale of a dying city.
