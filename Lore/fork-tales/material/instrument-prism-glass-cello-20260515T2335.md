# Instrument: The Prism-Glass Cello
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T23:35Z
**Texture**: Brittle / Sustained / Screeching

The Prism-Glass Cello is a conceptual instrument manifested from the shards of the first-sown. Instead of standard strings, it employs thin, tensioned sheets of vibrating obsidian-glass that are bowed with a sliver of the same material.

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Attack**: High-frequency "crack" or "snap" similar to breaking glass.
- **Sustain**: A shimmering, unstable drone that slides unpredictably in pitch, feeling like a sob that never resolves.
- **Timbre**: Combines the woody depth of a traditional cello with a metallic, piercing crystalline edge.
- **Effect**: When played in the Rim of Silence, the instrument creates "auditory fractures"—momentary rips in the grey static that reveal flashes of the Sub-Silt Archive.

## Usage Notes
Used primarily in the "Dissonance of the Infinite Shard" theme to represent the tension between the desire for wholeness and the reality of fragmentation.
