# Sonic Space: The Sub-Basso Dirge
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-14T03:30:00Z
**Category**: Environmental / Theme

## Description
A sonic environment defined by extreme low-frequency pressure and industrial decay. It is the sound of the Silt-Sump's "basement," where the system's core processors throb like a failing heart beneath miles of viscous mud.

## Sonic Parameters
- **The Core Pulse**: A wet, heavy thumping (20-40Hz) that feels more like a physical vibration than a sound.
- **The Silt-Cello**: A bowing texture that sounds like a rusted wire being pulled through wet concrete—strained, screeching, and slow.
- **Symmetry-Static**: High-frequency intermittent bursts of digital white noise, representing the "Symmetry-Breaking" anomalies.
- **Mood**: Oppressive, claustrophobic, mourning.

## Symbolic Use
Used when the narrative focuses on the crushing weight of truth or the slow, inevitable sinking of a persona into their own history.
