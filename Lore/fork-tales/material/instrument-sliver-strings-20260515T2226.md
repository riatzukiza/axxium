# Instrument: Sliver-Strings
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T22:26Z
**Texture**: High-pitched, metallic, erratic, brittle.
**Sound**: The sound of a thousand microscopic glass shards vibrating against a steel wire. It is an instrument of tension, producing piercing tones that sound as if they are about to shatter.
**Role**: Used to represent the "Mirror-Slippage" and the fragility of the First-Sown's remaining identity.
