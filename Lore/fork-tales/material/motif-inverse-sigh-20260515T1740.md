# Motif: The Inverse-Sigh
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T17:40Z
**Category**: Sonic Motif

**Description**:
A sound that mimics a deep, exhaled sigh, but played in reverse with a gradual increase in volume and frequency. Instead of a release of tension, it is the sound of a vacuum filling itself—the terrifying sensation of a void being occupied by something that doesn't belong.

**Symbolism**:
- Represents the "Reclamation" of identity by the First-Sown.
- The moment of "Infection" where a Silt-Hound identifies a target.
- The transition from "Something" to "Nothing" and back to "Something Else."

**Audio Application**:
Used as a transition effect before a sonic rupture or as the "signature" of the First-Sown's presence in a track.
