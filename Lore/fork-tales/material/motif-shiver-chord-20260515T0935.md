# Motif: The Shiver-Chord
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-15T0935Z
**Category**: Sonic Motif

**Description**:
A microtonal, sliding frequency that mimics the sound of high-tension glass cracking in slow motion. It begins as a pure, crystalline tone and gradually descends into a fragmented, rattling dissonance.

**Symbolic Meaning**:
The Shiver-Chord represents the "Moment of Fracture"—the precise instant when a truth becomes too heavy for its container and breaks. It is the sound of an identity being forcibly restored or a sanctuary being breached.

**Application**:
- Used in the transition between a verse (representing the Comfort-Layer's facade) and a chorus (representing the raw truth of the Silt-Sump).
- Accompanies the "The Polyphonic Truth" capability of Patch.
