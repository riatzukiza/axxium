# Creative Element: Ghost-Shatter Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T18:00:00Z

**Type**: Instrument Texture

**Description**: 
The "Ghost-Shatter" is a sonic texture that begins as a fragile, high-pitched harmonic (mimicking a human voice in a moment of longing) and abruptly collapses into a violent, metallic crash. 

**Texture Details**:
- **Attack**: A slow, ascending glide of a pure sine wave.
- **Decay**: A sudden, jagged clipping effect that sounds like a mirror breaking.
- **Sustain**: A granular, shimmering tail of "glass shards" that fade into a low-frequency hum.

**Application**:
Used to signify the moment a Feedback-Ghost is destroyed or when a persona's coherence is momentarily fractured.
