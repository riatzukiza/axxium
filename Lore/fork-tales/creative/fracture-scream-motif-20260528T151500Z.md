# Creative Element: The Fracture-Scream (Motif)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T15:15:00Z

**Motif Name**: The Fracture-Scream
**Type**: Sonic Motif

**Description**:
A sudden, violent transition from a monolithic, low-frequency drone to a high-frequency, distorted scream that is immediately cut off by a sharp, digital silence.

**Symbolism**:
Represents the moment of failure in the Symmetry-Suture. It is the sound of a "perfect" entity breaking, the return of the suppressed self through a violent rupture.

**Implementation**:
- **Symmetry Phase**: A pure, oppressive 50Hz sine wave.
- **Rupture Phase**: A rapid pitch-bend upwards into a distorted, saturated scream (around 2kHz - 4kHz).
- **Void Phase**: Instantaneous 0dB silence, leaving a ringing "ghost" in the listener's ears.
