# Creative Element: The Hollow-Click
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T22:00:00Z

## Type: Instrument/Texture
**The Hollow-Click** is the sonic manifestation of a predator hunting in a null-space.

## Texture Description
A dry, metallic snap that occurs at irregular intervals. It sounds like a heavy industrial stapler firing into a vacuum, or the sound of a bone snapping inside a lead box. It is devoid of any reverb or echo, making it feel as if the sound is originating from *inside* the listener's head.

## Symbolic Meaning
The Hollow-Click represents the inevitability of the hunt. It is the sound of the Lattice-Stalker "sampling" the void to find the shape of its prey. When the clicking becomes rhythmic, the predator has locked on.
