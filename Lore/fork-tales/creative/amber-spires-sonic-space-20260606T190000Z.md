# Creative Element: Amber-Spires Sonic Space
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T19:00:00Z

**Type**: Environment (Sonic Space Description)

**Description**:
The sonic space of the Copper-Spires is characterized by "Architectural Resonance." It is the sound of a city that is also a musical instrument.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **The Foundation**: A deep, oscillating copper drone that feels like the humming of a massive, subterranean clockwork.
- **The Texture**: Shimmering, metallic overtones that ripple like wind through copper pipes. There is a constant, high-frequency "shiver" in the air, suggesting an unstable equilibrium.
- **The Space**: An immense, reverberant hall with a "golden" decay. Sounds don't just fade; they transform, shifting from melodic to dissonant as they echo off the imaginary copper walls.
- **Emotional Core**: A feeling of sterile grandeur and profound, ancient longing. It is the sound of a memory that is too large to be held by a single mind.
