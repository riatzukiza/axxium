# Creative Element: The Ghost-Choir Texture
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T19:00:00Z

## Element: Texture (Sonic Space)
**Name**: The Ghost-Choir Wash

## Description
A sonic texture that represents the collective presence of the Static-Silt Ghosts. It is characterized by a dense, shimmering layer of overlapping vocal fragments—thousands of micro-samples of breaths, consonants, and glottal stops—that never quite form words.

## Technical Specifications
- **Frequency Range**: High-mid dominance (2kHz - 8kHz) to create a sense of anxiety and "edge."
- **Movement**: Slow, phasing shifts that create a feeling of vertigo, as if the listener is rotating within a sea of voices.
- **Interference**: Occasional "digital tears"—sudden, millisecond-long drops in volume or pitch-shifts—representing the instability of the ghosts' existence.

## Narrative Use
Used during scenes of extreme psychic pressure or when Kaelen is attempting to synthesize the Composite Voice. It serves as the atmospheric backdrop to his internal struggle.
