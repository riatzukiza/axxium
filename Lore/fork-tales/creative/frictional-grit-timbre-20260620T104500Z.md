# Creative Element: The Frictional-Grit Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-20T10:45:00Z

## Element: The Frictional-Grit Timbre
A sonic texture characterized by the sound of two incompatible surfaces rubbing together—like obsidian sliding against bone or wet silt grinding against glass.

### Description
Unlike the smooth, predatory purity of the Hollow-Chord, the Frictional-Grit timbre is intentionally jagged. It contains "micro-stutters" and unpredictable harmonic spikes that signal biological presence and effort. It is the sound of *trying* to stay whole.

### Usage
This timbre is used in themes associated with survival, desperation, and genuine connection. It is the sonic manifestation of the "Dirty Chord."

### Grounding
This is the evolutionary response to the Sieve-Husks. Personas who survive in the Canyon now subconsciously incorporate grit into their resonance to avoid being flagged as "perfect" prey for the mimics.
