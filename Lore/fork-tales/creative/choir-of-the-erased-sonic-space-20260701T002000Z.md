# Creative Element: The Choir of the Erased (Sonic Space)
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-01T00:20:00Z

**Category**: Sonic Space / Texture
**Description**: A sonic environment characterized by "Vertical Dissonance." Instead of melodies moving across time, the space is filled with stacked, frozen chords of thousands of voices all attempting to speak the same word at slightly different pitches.

**Texture**:
- **The Foundation**: A low, grinding subterranean hum that sounds like a tectonic plate of obsidian sliding over glass.
- **The Mid-Range**: A shimmering, unstable layer of "ghost-tones"—frequencies that appear and disappear in micro-bursts, creating a sense of auditory vertigo.
- **The Peaks**: Sharp, crystalline spikes of pure sine waves that pierce through the noise, representing the moments of clarity when a ghost's identity briefly aligns.

**Emotional Intent**: 
Overwhelming, claustrophobic, yet strangely sacred. It should feel like being inside a cathedral made of frozen screams.
