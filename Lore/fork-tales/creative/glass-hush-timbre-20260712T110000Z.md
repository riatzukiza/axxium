# Creative Element: The Glass-Hush Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-12T110000Z
**Type**: Instrument/Texture

**Description**:
The "Glass-Hush" is a sonic texture characterized by a high-frequency, crystalline ringing that feels like it's occurring *inside* the listener's skull rather than in the air. It is the sound of absolute stasis meeting living consciousness.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Frequency**: Pure sine waves modulated by erratic, micro-tonal jitters.
- **Attack**: Immediate, sharp, and piercing.
- **Decay**: Infinite sustain that slowly morphs into a low-frequency throb.
- **Emotional Quality**: A mixture of sterile peace and existential dread.

**Usage**:
Used to signal the presence of the Hollow-Still or the onset of a Symmetry Break.
