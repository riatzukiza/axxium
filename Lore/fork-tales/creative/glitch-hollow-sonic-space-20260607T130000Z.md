# Creative Element: The Glitch-Hollow Sonic Space
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-07T13:00:00Z

## Description
The "Glitch-Hollow" is the sonic environment that accompanies the experience of the Frozen Wound. It is the sound of a space that is trying to be full but is fundamentally missing a piece.

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Environment**: A vast, echoing chamber where the reverb is "incorrect"—sounds don't decay naturally but instead cut off abruptly or repeat in micro-loops.
- **Instruments**:
    - *The Shattered Sine*: A pure tone that occasionally "cracks," producing a burst of white noise.
    - *The Vacuum-Pulse*: A low-frequency thrum that feels like it is pulling the air out of the listener's lungs.
- **Theme**: *The Architecture of Absence*. The music should feel like it is built around a hole, with melodies that reach for a resolution that never arrives.
- **Motif**: A repeating three-note sequence (C# -> G -> F#) that always ends on a sudden, silent gap.
