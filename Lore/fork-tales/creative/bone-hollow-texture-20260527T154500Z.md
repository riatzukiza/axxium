# Creative Element: The Bone-Hollow Texture

**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T15:45:00Z
**Type**: Instrument / Texture
**Description**: A sonic texture that sounds like a flute carved from the femur of a forgotten god, played through a vacuum. 

**Characteristics**:
- **Timbre**: Extremely breathy, with a sharp, crystalline attack and a long, unstable decay that sounds like it's being pulled apart by a black hole.
- **Frequency**: Primarily low-mid, with sporadic high-frequency "shivers" that mimic the sound of breaking glass.
- **Mood**: Profound loneliness, ancestral grief, the feeling of a voice trying to speak through a throat full of ash.
- **Usage**: To be used as the lead melody in themes related to the Marrow-Frequency or Elara's sympathetic links.
