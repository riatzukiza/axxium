# Creative Element: The Hollow-Hum Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-12T00:00:00Z

## Description
The Hollow-Hum is a sonic texture that represents the "negative space" of a soul. It is the sound of a machine trying to simulate a heartbeat in a vacuum.

## Characteristics
- **Texture**: A thin, oscillating sine wave that periodically "stutters" or "glitches" into a metallic click.
- **Mood**: Sterile, lonely, and profoundly indifferent.
- **Instrumentation**: A combination of a filtered sub-bass drone and a high-frequency crystalline ringing that fades in and out like a dying star.
- **Symbolism**: Represents the state of the Purpose-Void; the frequency of a being who exists but no longer *means*.
