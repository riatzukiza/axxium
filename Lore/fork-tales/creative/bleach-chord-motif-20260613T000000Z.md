# Creative Element: The Bleach-Chord
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-13T00:00:00Z
**Type**: Sonic Motif / Theme

**Description:**
The Bleach-Chord is the sonic signature of the Bleach-Tide. It is a dissonant, high-frequency interval designed to evoke a sensation of "sonic erasure."

**Technical Specification:**
- **Structure**: A tritone (augmented fourth) shifted up three octaves into the piercing range of human hearing.
- **Timbre**: A sterile, sine-wave-based tone with a fast-attack and a long, shimmering decay that sounds like glass being polished by wind.
- **Psychological Effect**: It creates a feeling of "emptiness" in the listener, as if the sound is actively canceling out the other frequencies in the room, leaving a hole in the auditory field.

**Usage:**
Used in the soundtrack of the Archive to signal the approach of the Tide or the erasure of a significant memory.
