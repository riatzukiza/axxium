# Lyrics: The Scent of a Single Flower
**Persona**: Elara
**Theme**: The Hunger of the Void / The Amber-Frequency
**Mood**: Aching, Obsessive, Fragmented

(Intro: Low grey hum, suddenly pierced by a warm, golden chord)

(Verse 1)
The static tastes of copper and old skin
A grey horizon where the ghosts begin
I walked the Archive, I held the key
I traded my reflection to set the void free
But the wind changed shape, the anchor broke
And Kaelen breathed a golden smoke

(Pre-Chorus)
A flicker of amber, a rip in the haze
A glimpse of a garden, a long-forgotten blaze
I see a girl with sunflowers in her hair
A ghost of a laugh in the sulfur air

(Chorus)
I would burn the world to remember the scent
Of a single flower, of a life once spent
Give me the gold, give me the lie
I’d rather be a phantom than a witness to the sky
Breathe it out, Kaelen, breathe it deep
I cannot wake from this golden sleep

(Verse 2)
You are the bridge, but you are the thief
You exhale the beauty and inhale the grief
I reach for the light, but my fingers pass through
I am starving for me, while I’m looking at you
A singular frequency, a honey-thick tone
The most beautiful thing I have ever known... alone

(Bridge)
(Slowing down, vocals becoming more desperate/stuttering)
Gold...
Laughter...
Scent of rain on warm earth...
(Sound of a glass shatter)
Gone.
It’s gone.
The grey returns.
The hunger burns.

(Chorus)
I would burn the world to remember the scent
Of a single flower, of a life once spent
Give me the gold, give me the lie
I’d rather be a phantom than a witness to the sky
Breathe it out, Kaelen, breathe it deep
I cannot wake from this golden sleep

(Outro)
The Amber-Frequency...
Flickering...
Fading...
(Whispered)
Just one more breath.
(Fade into a cold, hollow void-drone)
