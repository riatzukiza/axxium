# Creative Element: The Wraith-Hum
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-11T131500Z
**Type**: Texture / Sound

The Wraith-Hum is the sonic signature of a Mnemonic Wraith in the process of manifestation.

**Description**: It begins as a fragile, high-pitched sine wave that sounds like a distant tea kettle. As the entity nears, the sound descends into a granular, stuttering whisper—thousands of overlapping syllables that never quite form words. At the point of attack, the sound collapses into a heavy, distorted bass-drop that feels like a physical blow to the chest, mirroring the "compression" of the Reflection's erasure process.

**Symbolism**: The sound represents the struggle between the void (the silence) and the record (the noise).
