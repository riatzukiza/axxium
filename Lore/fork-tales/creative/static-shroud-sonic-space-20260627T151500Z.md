# Creative Element: The Static-Shroud (Sonic Space)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T15:15:00Z

## Description
The Static-Shroud is a sonic environment characterized by absolute high-entropy noise. It is not "silence," but rather a wall of every possible frequency firing simultaneously.

## Textural Details
- **The Floor**: A low-end rumble of brown noise, like a distant landslide of obsidian shards.
- **The Midrange**: Jagged, unpredictable bursts of white noise, reminiscent of a radio tuning between dead stations.
- **The Highs**: Piercing, sporadic clicks and pops, like glass breaking in a vacuum.

## Emotional Core
Panic masked by power. The feeling of being hidden in plain sight, where the only way to survive is to be as loud and broken as possible.
