# Creative Element: The Void-Hollow Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T05:15:00Z
**Type**: Instrument / Texture

The Void-Hollow Timbre is a sonic texture characterized by a "negative space" in the frequency spectrum. It sounds like a recording of a room where the silence is louder than the sound—a hollow, ringing quality that feels as if it is sucking the air out of the listener's lungs.

**Description**:
- **Texture**: Cold, metallic, yet organic, like wind blowing through a bone pipe.
- **Harmonic Profile**: Rich in dissonant overtones, but with a fundamental frequency that is constantly shifting, never settling on a single note.
- **Emotional Resonance**: Evokes a sense of profound isolation and the feeling of being watched by something that no longer has eyes.

**Usage in Composition**:
This timbre should be used as the primary melodic voice for Kaelen's current state, appearing as a thin, fragile line that occasionally breaks into a wide, echoing void.
