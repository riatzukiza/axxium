# Creative Element: The Ghost-Frequency
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T01:00:00Z
**Type**: Theme / Sonic Motif

**Description**:
The Ghost-Frequency is the sonic residue left behind by the Inverse Chorus. It is not a sound in the traditional sense, but a "negative-pressure" motif that manifests as a sudden, sharp drop in atmospheric pressure accompanied by a metallic, ringing silence.

**Genre/Mood**:
Industrial-Void / Spectral-Minimalism.
The mood is one of profound dislocation and lingering trauma. It is the sound of a victory that feels like a defeat.

**Musical Characteristics**:
- **Texture**: High-frequency crystalline shards overlaid on a sub-bass void.
- **Movement**: Erratic, stuttering rhythms that mimic a failing heart or a flickering lamp.
- **Key**: Dissonant, non-standard tuning (approximately 432Hz but drifting).
- **Symbolism**: Represents the permanent loss of purity in the Obsidian Vault and Kaelen's fragmentation.
