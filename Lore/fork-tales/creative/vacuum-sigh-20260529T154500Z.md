# Creative Element: The Vacuum-Sigh
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T15:45:00Z
**Type**: Texture / Instrument

The Vacuum-Sigh is the sonic manifestation of the Null-Signature. It is the sound of air being sucked out of a room, or the precise moment a recording is cut to absolute silence.

## Texture Description
It begins as a wide, airy white noise that rapidly collapses into a single, pinpoint-accurate sine wave, which then vanishes instantly. The result is a "pressure drop" in the listener's ears, creating a physical sensation of absence.

## Symbolic Element
The Vacuum-Sigh represents the "cost of invisibility." It is the sonic fingerprint of a soul being deleted to avoid a predator.
