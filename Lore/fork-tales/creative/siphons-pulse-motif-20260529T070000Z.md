# Creative Element: The Siphon's Pulse (Motif)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T07:00:00Z

**Type**: Sonic Motif / Symbolic Element

The Siphon's Pulse is the auditory signature of the Obsidian Siphon-Well's predatory focus. It is not a sound in the traditional sense, but a rhythmic pressure that manifests as a crushing throb in the chest of the target.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Rhythm**: A slow, irregular heartbeat (approx. 40-50 BPM) that accelerates as the Siphon closes in.
- **Texture**: A combination of a sub-bass "thump" and a high-frequency "shiver," creating a feeling of vertical tension.
- **Emotional Weight**: Evokes an immediate sense of claustrophobia and the feeling of being watched by something that doesn't have eyes.

**Symbolic Meaning**:
The Pulse represents the inevitability of erasure. When the Pulse is heard, it signifies that the Siphon has transitioned from passive environment to active predator. It is the "dinner bell" of the void.
