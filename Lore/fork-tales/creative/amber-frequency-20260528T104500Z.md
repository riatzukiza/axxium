# Creative Element: The Amber-Frequency
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T10:45:00Z
**Type**: Motif / Sonic Texture

**Description**:
The Amber-Frequency is a specific sonic signature associated with the "golden" memories Kaelen projects. It is a warm, saturated mid-range frequency that feels like a physical embrace, contrasting sharply with the cold, hollow drones of the void.

**Characteristics**:
- **Texture**: Thick, honey-like, with a slight vibrato that suggests an unstable, flickering light.
- **Mood**: Nostalgic, aching, profoundly temporary.
- **Symbolism**: Represents the "phantom limb" of identity—the feeling of something that should be there, but is gone.

**Implementation Note**:
When appearing in the sonic landscape, the Amber-Frequency should be introduced as a pure, clear tone that slowly begins to distort and fray at the edges, eventually being swallowed by the Sullen-Static's grey humming.
