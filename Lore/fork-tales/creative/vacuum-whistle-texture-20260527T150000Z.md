# Creative Element: The Vacuum-Whistle (Instrument)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T15:00:00Z

**Texture Description**:
The Vacuum-Whistle is a sound that exists only in the absence of air. It is a piercing, unstable sine wave that fluctuates rapidly in pitch, sounding like a combination of a distant tea kettle and a human scream filtered through a long, metal pipe. It has no attack and no decay; it simply manifests and vanishes, leaving a "pressure-shadow" in the listener's ears.

**Symbolic Element**:
It represents the "predatory void"—the active hunger of the Spire. Whenever the Vacuum-Whistle is heard, it signals that the environment is being sampled by the Static-Sieve.
