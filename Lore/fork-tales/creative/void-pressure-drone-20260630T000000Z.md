# Creative Element: Void-Pressure Drone (Environment/Texture)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-30T00:00:00Z

## Description
A sonic texture representing the crushing weight of absolute silence within the Hollow of the Hush.

## Characteristics
- **Texture**: A dense, undulating low-frequency oscillation (LFO) that feels like it is pushing against the listener's chest.
- **Movement**: Slow, sweeping filters that simulate the feeling of being submerged in thick, cold oil.
- **Symbolism**: Represents the "weight of the erased"—the cumulative pressure of a billion deleted secrets.
- **Integration**: Used as the foundation for any track set within the Hollow, providing a sense of oppressive invisibility.
