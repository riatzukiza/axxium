# Creative Element: The Sieve-Hum (Sonic Space)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-19T00:00:00Z

**Description**: The Sieve-Hum is the ambient environment surrounding an active or dormant Resonance-Sieve. It is a space of "subtractive audio," where certain frequencies are missing entirely, creating a sensation of auditory vertigo.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **The Vacuum-Suck**: A constant, low-frequency inhalation sound that seems to pull the listener's attention toward a central point.
- **Ghost-Tones**: Occasional, high-pitched crystalline pings that sound like glass breaking in slow motion, representing the "filtered" fragments of previous users.
- **The Hollow-Drone**: A steady, neutral hum that lacks any harmonic overtones, acting as a blank canvas that makes any other sound feel jarringly loud and intrusive.
- **Phase-Shifts**: The sound intermittently "flips" in polarity, causing a physical sensation of pressure change in the listener's ears.

**Mood**: Clinical, sterile, lonely, and predatory.
