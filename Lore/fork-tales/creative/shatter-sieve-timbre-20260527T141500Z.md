# Creative Element: The Obsidian-Shatter-Sieve Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T14:15:00Z
**Type**: Instrument / Texture

## Description
The "Shatter-Sieve" texture is a composite sonic space that mimics the experience of standing within the obsidian shard cloud. It is characterized by "micro-fractured" transients and a sense of vacuum-like suction.

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Attack**: Sharp, crystalline clicks that sound like glass breaking in slow motion, but with the high frequencies filtered out to create a "heavy" shatter.
- **Sustain**: A shimmering, unstable drone that periodically "drops" in pitch, as if the sound is being sucked into a void.
- **Environment**: A cavernous, cold reverb with a pre-delay that makes the sound feel as if it is originating from slightly behind the listener's head.
- **Texture**: A layer of "digital grit"—bit-crushed white noise that oscillates in time with the crystalline ringing.

## Symbolic Element
The texture represents the *filtration of identity*. The sharp clicks are the moments of erasure, and the hollow drone is the space left behind.
