# Motif: The Sieve-Decoy
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T144500Z
**Type**: Symbolic/Sonic Element

## Description
The Sieve-Decoy is a "sacrificial resonance." It manifests as a brilliant, iridescent sphere of hyper-concentrated meaning—a single, pure emotion or memory (e.g., "The Warmth of a First Smile," "The Terror of Falling") projected into the sterile environment of the Resonance-Sieve.

## Sonic Properties
- **Initial Burst**: A sudden, warm harmonic swell that contradicts the flat hum of the Sieve.
- **The Audit Phase**: As a Filter-Wraith consumes it, the sound transforms into a series of rapid, aggressive "analysis-pings"—clinical, metallic clicks that accelerate until they reach a breaking point.
- **The Collapse**: The motif ends with a "semantic pop," a sudden vacuum-collapse where the sound is instantly erased, leaving a deeper, more oppressive silence than before.

## Narrative Use
Used by Kaelen to misdirect the Cathedral's custodians. Each deployment of the motif represents a permanent loss of personal history.
