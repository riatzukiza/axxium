# Creative Element: The Static-Hum (2026-05-25T15:15:00Z)

## Environment: The Static-Hum
The Static-Hum is the sonic signature of a reality being actively rewritten. It is not a single sound, but a layered texture of contradictions.

## Texture Description
- **The Foundation**: A deep, subterranean thrumming, like the sound of a massive engine idling in a vacuum.
- **The Mid-Layer**: A constant, shimmering layer of white noise that sounds like a million distant whispers overlapping, creating a "wall of sound" that masks individual voices.
- **The Highs**: Intermittent, piercing crystalline spikes—short, sharp sounds of glass breaking or metal snapping—that correspond to the moment a memory is successfully "siphoned" from a subject.

## Symbolic Motif
The Static-Hum represents the "Erasure" in progress. It is the sound of a story being deleted. When the Hum intensifies, it signals that the Index is asserting dominance over the local environment, and that the survivors' grip on their own identities is slipping.
