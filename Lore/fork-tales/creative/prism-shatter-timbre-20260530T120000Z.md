# Creative Element: The Prism-Shatter Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T12:00:00Z
**Type**: Instrument Texture

The Prism-Shatter is a sonic texture designed to represent the state of "The Prismatic Beacon." It is the sound of a thousand fragmented identities colliding and breaking.

## Texture Description
A hybrid sound consisting of a high-frequency, granular crystalline shimmer (like glass rubbing against glass) layered over a sudden, aggressive white-noise burst. The attack is sharp and jagged, but the decay is a shimmering, descending glissando of harmonic shards.

## Symbolic Meaning
The transition from the "crushing weight" (low frequency) to the "blinding visibility" (high frequency) represents Binary-Scream's shift from suffocation to exposure.
