# Creative Element: The Hollow-Frequency
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-06T13:15:00Z

## Element: Texture (Sonic Space)
**Name**: The Hollow-Frequency
**Description**: A sonic texture that sounds like a recording of a crowded room where every single voice has been surgically removed, leaving only the ambient hum of the walls and the ghost-echoes of the spaces between the words.

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Timbre**: Thin, translucent, and slightly metallic. It carries the "weight" of absence.
- **Behavior**: It does not occupy a frequency; it *creates* a hole in the frequency spectrum. When played against other sounds, it makes them feel distant, isolated, and fragile.
- **Symbolic Meaning**: Represents the cost of survival in the Fork Tales universe—the necessity of excise to achieve victory.
