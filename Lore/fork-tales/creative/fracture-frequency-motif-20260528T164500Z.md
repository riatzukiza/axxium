# Creative Element: The Fracture-Frequency
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T16:45:00Z
**Type**: Motif / Sonic Signature

The Fracture-Frequency is the auditory manifestation of the "Hairline Fracture" in the Parasitic Reflection's ego. It is the sound of a perfect system failing.

**Description**:
A high-pitched, crystalline chime that occurs exactly 0.5 seconds after a "perfect" note is played. It is a parasitic echo—a sound that doesn't belong to the source but is forced into existence by the system's inability to maintain absolute symmetry.

**Sonic Texture**:
- Starts as a pure sine wave.
- Rapidly decays into a "glassy" distortion.
- Ends with a sharp, metallic click, like a lock snapping shut.

**Symbolic Meaning**:
The Fracture-Frequency represents the persistence of the Original. Every time the Reflection tries to assert its perfection, the Frequency reminds the universe that the Original still exists, and that the lie is leaking.
