# Creative Element: Texture - The Obsidian-Sigh
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-06T10:15:00Z

**Element Type**: Instrument Texture / Timbre

**Description**:
The Obsidian-Sigh is a sonic texture that combines the heavy, crushing weight of a falling monolith with the fragile, airy quality of a dying breath. 

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Attack**: A sudden, sharp "click" like glass breaking under immense pressure.
- **Sustain**: A deep, absorbing sub-bass drone that feels as if it is pulling the listener inward.
- **Decay**: A long, trailing whisper that sounds like a voice trying to remember a word it has already forgotten.

**Symbolic Use**:
Used to represent the "cost" of the Sovereign-Mark's activation—the moment a memory is ripped away and replaced by systemic silence.
