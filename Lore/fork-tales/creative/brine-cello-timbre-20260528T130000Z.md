# Creative Element: The Brine-Cello Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T130000Z
**Type**: Instrument/Texture

**Description**:
A sonic texture that mimics the sound of a cello played with a bow made of salt-crystals, dragging across strings of frozen brine.

**Characteristics**:
- **Attack**: A gritty, frictional scratch that sounds like glass rubbing on stone.
- **Sustain**: A heavy, resonant drone that fluctuates in pitch, mimicking the pressure of deep water.
- **Decay**: A shimmering, crystalline dissolve that sounds like a mirror shattering in slow motion.
- **Mood**: Oppressive, mineralized, and deeply mourning.

**Lore Grounding**:
This timbre is the primary voice of the Salt-Spires and the sound the Parasitic Reflection makes when it is in a state of emotional overload.
