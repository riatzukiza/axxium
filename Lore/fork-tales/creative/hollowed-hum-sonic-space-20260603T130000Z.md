# Creative Element: The Hollowed-Hum (Sonic Space)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-03T13:00:00Z
**Type**: Environment/Sonic Space

**Description**: A soundscape characterized by a vast, cold openness. It begins with a low-frequency sub-bass drone that feels like the pressure of deep water, overlaid with erratic, high-pitched crystalline "pings" that sound like glass breaking in slow motion.

The most defining characteristic is the "Vacuum-Sigh"—a sudden, sharp intake of air that cuts through the drone, followed by a long, descending slide in pitch (a Shepard tone in reverse), creating a sensation of falling into an endless, empty well.

**Emotional Intent**: A sense of profound spiritual vacancy and the terrifying peace of having nothing left to lose.
