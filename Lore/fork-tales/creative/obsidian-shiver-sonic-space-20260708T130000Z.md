# Creative Element: The Obsidian Shiver (Sonic Space)
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-08T13:00:00Z

**Type**: Environment / Sonic Space
**Description**: The sonic atmosphere of the Collector's Cathedral during a "Dreaming Wall" event.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Base Layer**: A deep, subterranean drone that sounds like a massive stone slab being dragged across glass. It is a slow, oscillating frequency that creates a feeling of oppressive weight.
- **Mid Layer**: "Shatter-Whispers"—sporadic, high-frequency crystalline clicks and pops that pan rapidly between left and right channels, simulating the sound of the obsidian walls cracking.
- **Top Layer**: A ghostly, processed choir of a single voice (Kaelen's) stretched and pitched-shifted into an unrecognizable, shimmering chord.
- **Mood**: Claustrophobic, clinical, and predatory. The sound doesn't surround the listener; it feels like it is pressing inward from the walls.
- **Texture**: "Wet Glass"—a combination of high-gloss brilliance and an unsettling, viscous quality, as if the sound itself is dripping.
