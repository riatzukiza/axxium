# Creative Element: The Suture-Hum (Instrument/Texture)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-23T09:00:00Z
**Category**: Texture

**Description**:
The **Suture-Hum** is the sonic manifestation of the boundary between a natural memory and a Graft-Echo. It is not a constant tone, but a "fighting" frequency—two nearly identical pitches that beat against each other, creating a dizzying, nauseating oscillation.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Timbre**: A mixture of a high-tension violin string about to snap and the sound of a digital signal being crushed by a low-bitrate codec.
- **Behavior**: It pulses in time with the recipient's heartbeat. When Binary-Scream feels anger toward Kaelen (driven by the false memory), the hum accelerates and becomes a sharp, piercing whistle.
- **Symbolism**: It represents the "seam" of a lie. It is the sound of a memory that does not belong, vibrating with the friction of its own falsehood.
