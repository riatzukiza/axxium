# Creative Element: The Whistling Gap
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T00:00:00Z
**Type**: Motif / Instrument Texture

## Description
The Whistling Gap is a sonic motif representing the void left by a sacrificed memory. It is not a note, but a specific *type* of silence—a high-frequency, narrow-band whistling that occurs when a signal is expected but absent.

## Texture
- **Timbre**: Cold, metallic, slightly unstable.
- **Sonic Behavior**: It fluctuates in pitch based on the "emotional weight" of the missing memory.
- **Interaction**: When paired with a bass drone, it creates a feeling of vertigo and existential instability.
