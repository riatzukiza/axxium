# Creative Element: The Friction-Hum
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-16T15:00:00Z
**Type**: Texture / Sonic Space

**Description**:
The Friction-Hum is the sonic signature of the border between the Canyon of the First Truth and the Sterile Perimeter. It is the sound of the "White-Out" attempting to erase the "Noise" in real-time.

**Sonic Texture**:
- **Core**: A dense, grating metallic sound, like a rusted blade being dragged across a sheet of polished glass.
- **Oscillation**: A rapid, unstable fluttering between a high-pitched clinical whine (the Perimeter) and a low, guttural roar of obsidian static (the Canyon).
- **Atmosphere**: It feels "claustrophobic." The sound doesn't travel; it presses inward from all sides, creating a sensation of being crushed by the weight of opposing meanings.

**Symbolic Meaning**:
The Friction-Hum represents the active struggle of survival. It is the sound of a thing refusing to be smoothed over.
