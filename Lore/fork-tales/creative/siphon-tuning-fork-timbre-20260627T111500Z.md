# Creative Element: The Siphon-Sentry's Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-27T11:15:00Z

## Element: Instrument (Texture)
**Name**: The Siphon-Tuning-Fork

## Description
A sound characterized by an unnerving, surgical precision. It is a metallic, ringing tone that lacks any natural decay. Instead of fading, the note stays perfectly flat and piercing until it is abruptly cut off.

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Attack**: Instant, like a needle hitting glass.
- **Sustain**: Perfectly stable, with no vibrato or warmth.
- **Decay**: Non-existent; the sound is terminated by a hard-cut vacuum.
- **Harmonics**: Pure sine-like tones layered with a high-frequency "shimmer" that sounds like silver wire vibrating under extreme tension.

## Symbolic Meaning
The Siphon-Tuning-Fork represents the Spire's refusal to allow organic decay. Everything is a transaction; everything is a line on a ledger. The sound is the audit of a soul.
