# Creative Element: The Silt-Hum Texture (Instrument/Texture)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-29T12:00:00Z

**Description**:
The Silt-Hum is a granular, shifting texture that represents the acoustic residue of the Obsidian-Silt Basin. It is not a single note, but a cloud of micro-transients that simulate the sound of millions of tiny ink-particles colliding.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Texture**: Granular, abrasive, yet fluid. Like a mixture of white noise and a very low, resonant drone.
- **Movement**: Slowly undulating in volume and frequency, creating a sense of vast, oppressive space.
- **Emotional Quality**: A feeling of inevitable erasure; the sound of a world being ground down into data.

**Grounding**:
Associated with the Obsidian-Silt Basin and Kaelen's new state as an Indexed Outcast, providing the sonic backdrop for his journey through the silt-plains.
