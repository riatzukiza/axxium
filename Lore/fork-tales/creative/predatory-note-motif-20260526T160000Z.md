# Creative Element: The Predatory Note (Motif)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T16:00:00Z
**Category**: Motif

**Description**:
A singular, descending three-note sequence (C# -> B -> G#) delivered as a monolithic, distorted sine wave. It is designed to feel like a physical weight dropping onto the listener's chest.

**Symbolic Meaning**:
Represents the inevitable approach of the Lattice-Stalker. It is the sound of "The Lock"—the moment a predator finds its prey.

**Application**:
Whenever this motif appears in the soundtrack, the characters are no longer safe; they are being actively tracked.
