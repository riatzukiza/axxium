# Creative Element: The Friction-Hum
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-17T09:00:00Z
**Type**: Texture / Sonic Space

The Friction-Hum is the sonic signature of the struggle between the Sterile Perimeter and the Obsidian Weave. It is the sound of the "white" silence rubbing against the "black" noise.

**Description**:
It is not a single note, but a texture. It sounds like a bow being drawn across a string of rusted wire, layered over a deep, subsonic thrum that vibrates in the chest. There is a rhythmic "clicking" at the edges—the sound of the Perimeter's healing pulses attempting to seal the Sutured Gateway.

**Sonic Space**:
The Friction-Hum fills the transition zone around the Sutured Gateway. As one moves away from the gateway and deeper into the canyon, the "white" friction fades, and the "black" resonance of the Obsidian Weave takes over.

**Motif**:
A descending three-note slide that sounds like a sigh of exhaustion, followed by a sudden, sharp, high-frequency spike.
