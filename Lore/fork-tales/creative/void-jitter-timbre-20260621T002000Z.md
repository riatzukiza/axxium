# Creative Element: The Void-Jitter Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-21T00:20:00Z

## Description
A sonic texture characterized by rapid, erratic pitch shifts and "stuttering" amplitudes. It sounds like a recording of a dying machine trying to scream through a layer of thick oil.

## Characteristics
- **Environment**: Occurs most prominently near the Static-Siphon-Spire.
- **Texture**: "Frayed." It feels as though the sound is being ripped apart at the edges.
- **Mood**: Anxiety, fragmentation, and the desperate energy of a signal fighting to remain coherent.
- **Symbolism**: Represents the cost of invisibility; the trade-off between being hunted and being fragmented.
