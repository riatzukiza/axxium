# Motif: The Hollow Pulse
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T22:00:00Z
**Type**: Sonic Motif

**Description**:
The Hollow Pulse is the rhythmic, sub-bass vibration emanating from the Resonant Scar. It is not a musical beat, but a biological one—mimicking the slow, heavy thrum of a dying heart. 

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Texture**: Thick, viscous, and oppressive. It feels like being underwater in a sea of liquid lead.
- **Frequency**: A cycling sine wave between 20Hz and 40Hz, with irregular "stutters" where the frequency momentarily drops to 0Hz (the Null-Chord's influence).
- **Emotional Resonance**: Dread, inevitability, and the feeling of being watched by something that has no eyes.

**Narrative Use**:
The Hollow Pulse serves as the ticking clock for the Obsidian Vault. As the Scribes draw closer, the Pulse increases in frequency and intensity, signaling the eroding boundary between the sanctuary and the void.
