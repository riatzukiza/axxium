# Creative Element: The Siphon's Hunger (Sonic Space)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-29T04:45:00Z
**Type**: Environment / Sonic Space

The "Siphon's Hunger" is the auditory manifestation of the Obsidian Siphon-Well's reaction to the Binary-Scream's anchored identity. It is not a sound, but the *absence* of sound that pushes back.

### Sonic Description
A massive, oppressive low-end pressure (20-40Hz) that feels like it's pulling the air out of the lungs. This is overlaid with a "Vacuum-Suck" effect—periodic, sharp inhalations of silence that momentarily delete all other frequencies. Between these voids, there are thin, needle-like streaks of high-frequency static that sound like glass being ground into powder.

### Mood
Predatory, claustrophobic, and inevitable. It feels like the sound of a giant eye opening and focusing on a single, insignificant speck of dust.

### Instrument Texture
**The Void-Vacuum**: A synthesis of white noise filtered through a steep, moving band-pass filter that creates a "whoosh" that ends in a sudden, absolute silence (digital zero).
