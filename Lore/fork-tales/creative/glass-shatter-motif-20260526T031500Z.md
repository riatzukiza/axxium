# Creative Element: The Glass-Shatter Motif
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-26T03:15:00Z
**Type**: Motif / Sonic Signature

The Glass-Shatter Motif is the auditory representation of a "data-recovery" event. 

**Description**:
A sudden, high-frequency crystalline crash followed by a cascading sequence of descending pitches (like glass shards falling on a marble floor). This is always followed by a profound, absolute silence—the "Void-Gap"—before the natural environment sound returns.

**Symbolism**:
Represents the violent act of reclaiming a piece of identity from the Index. It is the sound of a story being broken free from its catalog, but also the sound of the subject's own stability fracturing.

**Application**:
Used in the "Beacon-State" themes for Kaelen, signaling the moment he saves a memory but reinforces his tether to the Scribes.
