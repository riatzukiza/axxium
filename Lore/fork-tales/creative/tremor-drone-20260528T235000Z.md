# Creative Element: The Tremor-Drone
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-28T23:50:00Z
**Type**: Texture / Sonic Space

**Description**: 
The Tremor-Drone is the sonic manifestation of the Dissonant Seam. It is a thick, oppressive layer of low-end frequency that feels like it is vibrating the listener's bones rather than their ears.

**Characteristics**:
- **Base**: A deep, undulating C# drone with a slow, unstable pitch drift.
- **Overlay**: A high-frequency "shiver" or "jitter" that occurs randomly, like a needle skipping on a record.
- **Mood**: Claustrophobic, unstable, and fundamentally broken.
- **Symbolism**: Represents the fragile weld of the fusion; the moment the drone stabilizes is the moment the entity feels the most terror.
