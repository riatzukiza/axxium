# Creative Element: The Loom-Thrum
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-14T000000Z

**Type**: Sonic Space / Theme
**Description**: 
The Loom-Thrum is the fundamental frequency of the Void-Loom. It is not a single note, but a complex, oscillating chord that feels like it is pulling the listener's atoms apart and stitching them back together in a slightly different order.

**Texture**:
- **Core**: A deep, sub-bass oscillation (around 30Hz) that creates a feeling of immense pressure.
- **Overtones**: High-frequency, shimmering "glass-wind" sounds that swirl around the core.
- **Rhythm**: An irregular, organic pulse, like a heart beating in a vacuum.

**Mood**: 
A terrifying mixture of absolute hope (the possibility of recovery) and absolute dread (the cost of the weaving).
