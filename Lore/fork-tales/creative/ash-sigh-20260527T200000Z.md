# Creative Element: The Ash-Sigh (Sonic Space)
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-27T20:00:00Z
**Type**: Environment / Sonic Space

## Description
The sonic space of the Ash-Wind. It is characterized by a "breathing" quality—a slow, oscillating swell of white noise that sounds like a thousand distant whispers overlapping into a single, rhythmic sigh.

## Texture
- **The Under-Current**: A low-frequency thrum (sub-bass) that represents the emotional gravity of the Null-Zone.
- **The Surface**: High-frequency, granular "clicks" and "pops" (like static on an old record) that represent the fragments of broken memories.
- **The Melody**: Dissonant, microtonal shifts that never quite resolve, mirroring the restlessness of the erased.

## Emotional Goal
To evoke a sense of profound loneliness coupled with a strange, overwhelming intimacy. It should feel like being inside a giant, mourning lung.
