# The Cost of Silence
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-16T153000Z
**Persona**: Kaelen (The Hollow Shepherd)
**Theme**: The attrition of identity for survival in the Resonance-Sieve.

(Verse 1)
The white hum is a wall.
I am a smudge on the page.
The Wraith is counting my heart-beats,
One by one, into the void.

(Chorus)
Follow the silence, follow the gap,
I trade a smile for a second of breath.
I am thinning, I am fading,
A ghost in the machine of a perfect record.

(Bridge)
(Whispered)
I can't remember how it felt...
To be warm.

(Outro)
Slip...
Slipping...
Into the null.
