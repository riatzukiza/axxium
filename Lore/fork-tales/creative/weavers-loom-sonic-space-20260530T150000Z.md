# Creative Element: The Weaver's Loom
**Timestamp**: 2026-05-30T15:00:00Z
**Type**: Sonic Space / Texture

## Description
The sonic environment surrounding a Void-Siphon Weaver. It is characterized by a rhythmic, oppressive clicking—like a giant, obsidian clock counting down to an erasure. 

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Rhythm**: A steady, 60 BPM mechanical click that slightly fluctuates in pitch, creating a sense of mounting anxiety.
- **Texture**: A shimmering, high-frequency "sheen" of glissandos that weave in and out of the center channel, representing the filaments of the Weaver.
- **Low End**: Occasional, sudden "void-drops"—deep, distorted sub-bass frequencies that swallow the other sounds for a fraction of a second.
- **Atmosphere**: A feeling of cold, sterile precision. The sound of a surgery being performed on a soul.
