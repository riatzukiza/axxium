# Creative Element: Timbre
**Name**: Brittle-Harvest Shiver
**Timestamp**: 2026-07-14T120000Z
**Type**: Texture/Timbre

## Description
A piercing, high-frequency resonance that occurs at the exact moment a resonance thread is ripped from a deleted entity and integrated into the Brittle Weaver's crystalline lattice. 

## Sonic Characteristics
- **Attack**: A sharp, glass-like crack (transient) that sounds like a frozen lake fracturing under pressure.
- **Sustain**: A shimmering, unstable oscillation (12kHz - 15kHz) that warbles with a "metallic hunger."
- **Decay**: A slow, granular descent into a low-frequency void-hum, as if the sound is being swallowed by the Void-Loom.
- **Texture**: Abrasive, cold, and "thin." It lacks harmonic warmth, feeling like a sonic void where a melody should be.

## Symbolic Meaning
The sound of a debt being paid in the currency of identity. It is the audible manifestation of the Weaver's survival—and the total erasure of the donor's last remaining signal.
