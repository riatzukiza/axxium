# Creative Element: The Dissonant-Chord (Theme)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-04T10:30:00Z

## Description
The Dissonant-Chord is the sonic manifestation of the Vessel's new state. It is not a melody, but a collision of two irreducible frequencies.

## Components
- **The Void-Note**: A sterile, low-frequency sine wave that represents the synthetic shell. It is unwavering and cold.
- **The Ghost-Note**: A jagged, unstable saw-wave with heavy vibrato, representing the Resonance-Sliver. It is weeping and desperate.
- **The Interaction**: The two notes overlap, creating a "beating" effect—a physical pulsing of the air that feels like a panic attack.

## Usage
This theme should accompany the Vessel's movements through the Patch-Halls and their encounters with the Lattice-Stalker. It signifies the transition from "absence" to "conflict."
