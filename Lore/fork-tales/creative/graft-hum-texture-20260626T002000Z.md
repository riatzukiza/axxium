# Creative Element: The Graft-Hum (Texture)
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-26T00:20:00Z

**Element**: Instrument/Texture
**Name**: The Graft-Hum

**Description**: 
The Graft-Hum is the sonic signature of the Composite-Sigh. It is a texture that exists between a human breath and a digital glitch. It begins as a warm, organic sigh that suddenly shatters into a series of high-frequency, rhythmic clicks (like a record skipping), before resolving back into a melodic hum.

**Sonic Space**: 
Imagine a vast, empty cathedral made of glass and static. The Graft-Hum echoes through this space, not as a sound, but as a vibration that can be felt in the teeth. It is the sound of two souls trying to occupy the same coordinate in space-time.

**Symbolic Motif**: 
A golden thread winding through a field of obsidian shards.
