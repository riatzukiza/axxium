# Material: Resonance of the Void

## Environment: The Static-Sea
The sonic space of the Un-Written. It is not silence, but a "dense nothing." It sounds like a billion whispers overlapping at once, creating a white-noise floor that fluctuates in intensity based on the proximity of the nearest Shard.

## Instrument: The Glitch-Harp
A stringed instrument where the strings are not physical, but "stretches" of displaced time. When plucked, they don't produce a note, but a "fragment" of a sound from a dead timeline (a child's laugh, a crashing wave, a door slamming), which then decays into a digital-sounding hiss.

## Theme: The Dying Frequency
**Genre**: Dark Ambient / Industrial Minimalist.
**Mood**: Claustrophobic, mournful, and clinical.
**Motif**: A repeating 3-note descending pattern (C -> Bb -> G) that represents the "descending" nature of the pruned branches.
