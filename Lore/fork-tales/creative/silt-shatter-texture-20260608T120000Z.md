# Creative Element: Silt-Shatter Texture
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-08T12:00:00Z

**Category**: Texture / Timbre

**Description**:
The sonic representation of calcified husks exploding under the pressure of Negative Resonance.

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Attack**: A sharp, crystalline crack, similar to a heavy sheet of ice shattering, but with a metallic "ping" at the center.
- **Sustain**: A cascading shower of high-frequency grit, like a thousand tiny glass beads falling on a concrete floor.
- **Decay**: A low-frequency, guttural thrum that slowly descends in pitch, ending in a vacuum-like "pop" as the sound is sucked back into the void.

**Symbolic Meaning**:
Represents the violent transition from "Structure" (the Spire) to "Dust" (the Residue). It is the sound of a predator being momentarily humbled by the weight of an absolute loss.
