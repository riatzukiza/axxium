# Creative Element: The Void-Pulse Timbre
**Timestamp**: 2026-06-18T00:20:00Z

**Type**: Instrument/Texture
**Description**: A sound that is defined by the absolute absence of frequency. It is a "negative sound" that feels like a sudden drop in air pressure, a sharp intake of breath that never exhales. 

**Sonic Profile**:
- **Attack**: Immediate, like a vacuum seal breaking.
- **Sustain**: A crushing, heavy silence that vibrates with an underlying, low-frequency thrum (around 20Hz).
- **Decay**: A slow, rushing return of ambient noise, like water filling a void.

**Symbolic Meaning**: Represents the moment of destructive interference—the instant where two opposing truths cancel each other out, leaving only the raw, terrifying gap of the unknown.
