# Creative Element: The Void-Pressure Drone (Environment/Sonic Space)

**Type**: Environment / Sonic Space
**Description**: The "Void-Pressure" is the acoustic experience of being near a Bleed-Event or an Archivist's aperture. 

**Sonic Characteristics**:
- **Foundation**: A massive, sub-bass drone (around 30-40Hz) that creates a physical sensation of pressure in the chest.
- **Texture**: Intermittent, rhythmic "static-pops" that sound like old vinyl records being scratched by a diamond needle.
- **High-End**: A piercing, oscillating sine wave that mimics the sound of a distant, malfunctioning emergency siren, but tuned to a non-human scale.
- **Emotional Impact**: A feeling of profound insignificance and inevitable erasure.
