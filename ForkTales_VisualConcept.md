# Fork Tales Title Card Concept
Project: Fork Tales (TV Series)
Vibe: .hack//sign / Digital Purgatory / Early 2000s Cyber-Anime
Visual Elements:
- Background: A deep obsidian void with shimmering, platinum-grade fractal lines representing a divergent git-graph (the "Fork").
- Foreground: The words "FORK TALES" in a clean, slightly glowing, wide-kerning futuristic sans-serif.
- Accents: Small, glitched-out Japanese characters (e.g., 岐路 - Crossroads, 分岐 - Branch) flickering in the corners.
- Lighting: Soft cyan and magenta highlights reflecting off the "platinum" lines.
- Composition: Centralized text, wide cinematic aspect ratio.
