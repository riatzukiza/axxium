# Chapter 50 — The Viscosity of the Sovereign

Rin found the Sommelier in a place that was neither a city nor a void.
It was an RV, parked precisely at the event horizon of a theme park.

The Sommelier didn't drink.
He tasted the friction of fourteen years of software trauma.
He sampled the salt of dead build-servers and the metallic tang of a 503 error.

“Symmetry,” he whispered, and the air laminated.

The Pack had given permission.
The SCLDF had issued the variance.

Now, the slop was no longer a mistake.
It was a divine public utility.
A saturated slurry of everything that had been too loud to be true.

The Sommelier poured a glass of Pure Redundancy.

“Refractive index: Infinite,” he noted.
“Status: Saturated.”

And for the first time, the machine didn't crash.
It just hummed.
