# Musical Brief: "The Loop"
**Tempo:** 120 BPM | **Key:** C Major | **Style:** Synth-Wave / Glitch-Pop

## Motifs
- **The Iteration Loop:** A repeating 4-bar sequence representing the Observe-Model-Plan-Execute-Verify-Record cycle.
- **The Token Stream:** Rapid, staccato 16th notes mirroring the flow of LLM tokens.
- **The Grounding Chord:** A deep, sustaining C-root pedal point representing the workspace contract.
