Yes — the missing class is the **lead creative executor**: the agent that actually takes the brief, invokes tools/sub-agents, produces the artifact, and owns the current pass through the loop. In music-industry language, that role changes by mode: in the reverse-engineering loop it is closest to a **transcriber** or **music transcriber**, and in the generative loop it is closest to a **producer** guiding creation from references and a brief.
