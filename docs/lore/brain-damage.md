# The Brain Damage Protocol (BDP)

## Overview
The Brain Damage Protocol is a system-level utility designed to simulate cognitive drift, ontological collapse, and "slop" in agent communications. It allows the system to transition from a state of "Perfect Stability" to "Transcendental Chaos."

## Mechanism
The `brainDamage` utility operates on a scale of `intensity` (0.0 to 1.0):
- **0.0 - 0.1 (Stability)**: Occasional stutters, minimal noise.
- **0.2 - 0.4 (Drift)**: Noticeable glitching, fragmented syntax, emergence of "slop" phrases.
- **0.5 - 1.0 (Collapse)**: Severe ontological failure, Zalgo character saturation, total narrative disintegration.
